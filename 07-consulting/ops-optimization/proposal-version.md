# Operations Optimization Proposal

## Prepared for: Jennifer Okonkwo, CEO, Meridian Logistics

## Executive Summary

Meridian Logistics has an operations problem—and it's costing you money. Your EBITDA margin has declined from 14% to 8% in three years. Your warehouse utilization is 58% (industry benchmark: 75-85%). Your order accuracy is 94.2% (industry benchmark: 99%+). You're losing customers to competitors with better technology. And your $8M in at-risk revenue is one bad quarter away from defecting.

We propose a 14-week operations optimization engagement that will improve efficiency, customer experience, and margins simultaneously. The result: EBITDA margin recovery from 8% to 15%+, warehouse utilization to 80%+, order accuracy to 99%+, and retention of $12M in at-risk revenue.

---

## Proposed Engagement

### Phase 1: Operations Assessment (Weeks 1-3) — $30,000
- Financial analysis, warehouse walk-throughs, process mapping, WMS assessment, space utilization analysis, labor productivity study, customer experience audit.

### Phase 2: Optimization Implementation (Weeks 4-11) — $110,000
- WMS implementation (Manhattan Associates), space optimization, labor restructuring, customer portal launch.

### Phase 3: Performance Stabilization (Weeks 12-14) — $40,000
- WMS hypercare, performance monitoring, continuous improvement program.
