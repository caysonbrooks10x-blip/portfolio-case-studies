# Operations Optimization: Deliverables Inventory

## Phase 1: Operational Diagnosis — Deliverables

### 1. Operational Assessment Report
**Description:** Comprehensive diagnostic of current-state operations.
**Pages:** 85 pages
**Contents:**
- Executive summary
- Methodology documentation
- Process maps (12 critical workflows)
- Observation notes
- Data analysis findings
- Employee interview synthesis (42 interviews)
- Supplier/customer interview findings
- Root cause analysis (5 whys per issue)
- Quick wins identification
**Owner:** Lead Consultant
**Tools:** Notion, Power BI
**Sign-off:** CEO and COO
