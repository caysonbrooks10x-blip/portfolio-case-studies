# Granitepeak Manufacturing: Operations Optimization

## The Situation

A 62-year-old precision machining company was facing margin compression, rising competition, and operational inefficiencies that had accumulated over decades. With 127 employees and $34M in revenue, they needed to modernize operations to survive and compete.

## The Approach

20-week operations optimization engagement:

1. **Operational Diagnosis** — Direct observation, process mapping, data analysis, employee interviews
2. **Operations Strategy** — 3 Pillars framework (Flow, Quality, Agility)
3. **Implementation** — Scheduling system, quality transformation, inventory optimization
4. **Sustainability** — Lean training, Kaizen events, succession planning

## The Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Production Cycle Time | 23 days | 17.7 days |
| On-Time Delivery | 78% | 96% |
| WIP Inventory | $4.2M | $2.8M |
| First-Pass Yield | 89% | 97% |
| Operating Margin | 11% | 17% |
| Annual Cost Savings | — | $2.8M |

Katana, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Power BI, Minitab, SAP Ariba, Asana
