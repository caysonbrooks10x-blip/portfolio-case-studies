# Elevate Digital: Offer Design + Monetization Strategy

## The Situation

A $4.2M marketing agency was stuck—busy but not profitable, custom projects consuming bandwidth, 31% win rate, and founder burning out at 55 hrs/week. The business model was broken: they were selling hours, not outcomes.

## The Approach

16-week offer architecture and monetization redesign:

1. **Offer Architecture Audit** — Financial analysis, value delivery assessment, competitive analysis
2. **Offer Redesign** — 4-tier outcome-based suite, value monetization model, package architecture
3. **Implementation** — New offers launched, value-based pricing, sales playbook
4. **Optimization** — Performance monitoring, pricing optimization, capacity planning

## The Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Annual Revenue | $4.2M | $7.8M |
| Average Project Size | $85K | $340K |
| Net Profit Margin | 18% | 34% |
| Proposal Win Rate | 31% | 58% |
| Monthly Recurring Revenue | $0 | $1.2M |
| Founder Hours/Week | 55 | 30 |

**Plus:** 35% of revenue now recurring retainers

## The Tools

HubSpot, Miro, Airtable, Notion, Tableau, QuickBooks, Zoom, Slack, Figma

## Investment

$85,000 fixed-fee | 16 weeks | 2-person team
