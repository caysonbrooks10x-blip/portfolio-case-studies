# Nexus Digital — Offer Design + Monetization Strategy

## Summary

Nexus Digital had product-market fit (users loved the product, NPS >50) but flawed unit economics (CAC $1,200 > LTV $800, 8% monthly churn). After a 10-week offer redesign and monetization strategy engagement, MRR grew from $540K to $1.8M (+233%), CAC dropped from $1,200 to $340 (-72%), and monthly churn fell from 8% to 2.5% (-69%).

**The Problem:** Misaligned pricing model (per-user vs. value delivery), no tiered offerings, no expansion revenue, and poor value communication.

**The Solution:** Value-based pricing redesign, 3-tier offering (Starter $99, Professional $349, Enterprise custom), expansion revenue engine, and self-service growth motion.

**Results (12 months):**
- MRR: $540K → $1.8M (+233%)
- CAC: $1,200 → $340 (-72%)
- Monthly Churn: 8% → 2.5% (-69%)
- Net Revenue Retention: 85% → 128%
- LTV:CAC Ratio: 0.67x → 12.4x

**Investment:** $75K fixed-fee | **Timeline:** 10 weeks
