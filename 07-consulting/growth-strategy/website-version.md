# Apex Manufacturing: Business Growth Strategy

## The Challenge

A third-generation family-owned precision machining company stuck flat at $12M for 4 years. Customer base aging (22-year average tenure), 60% revenue concentration in top 3 customers, no sales function, no digital presence. Owner 62 and thinking about succession—but business wasn't fundable or acquirable.

## The Approach

16-week growth strategy project:

**Phase 1: Strategic Assessment**
Financial analysis, customer analysis, competitive landscape, market segmentation, capacity analysis. Identified root causes: no go-to-market function, customer concentration risk, digital invisibility.

**Phase 2: Growth Strategy Development**
Market repositioning (aerospace/medical device), customer acquisition engine (sales + CRM + LinkedIn), capacity optimization, digital presence build-out, talent development.

**Phase 3: Implementation Support**
Sales hire and onboarding, HubSpot CRM implemented, new website launched, LinkedIn presence established, scheduling optimization, process documentation initiated.

## The Results

| Metric | Before | After (24 mo) |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Revenue | $12M | $18.5M |
| Customers | 28 | 51 |
| EBITDA Margin | 8% | 17% |
| Fortune 500 Customers | 0 | 4 |
| Acquisition Offer | — | $24M |
