# Growth Strategy Case Study: Velocite Systems

## The Challenge

A $18M ARR B2B SaaS company serving mid-market manufacturers was experiencing a 12% YoY decline in net new ARR—while their market was growing 8% annually. Their private equity owners knew something was fundamentally broken, but the symptoms pointed everywhere and the root causes were buried.

Sales was missing quota. Marketing was generating leads that never converted. Product was building features nobody used. And the executive team couldn't agree on where to focus.

## The Approach

Over 16 weeks, we conducted a comprehensive growth strategy engagement structured in four phases:

**Phase 1: Strategic Diagnosis**
We interviewed 34 stakeholders, analyzed 3 years of CRM data across 2,400+ opportunities, mapped 8 major competitors across 47 differentiating factors, and completed a full customer profitability analysis. What we found wasn't one big problem—it was five interconnected issues that were collectively draining growth.

**Phase 2: Strategy Development**
Using a 3 Horizons growth framework, we developed a three-year strategic roadmap anchored to four strategic pillars: reclaim "manufacturer-first" positioning, built-not-sold demand generation, revenue intelligence infrastructure, and customer success as a growth engine.

**Phase 3: Implementation Planning**
We converted strategy into a 90-day sprint roadmap with clear ownership, milestones, and success metrics. Quick wins were prioritized to build momentum while longer-term initiatives were properly resourced.

**Phase 4: Execution Support**
Rather than handing off documents and disappearing, we embedded with the team during initial execution—weekly strategic calls, bi-weekly tactical sessions, and on-demand advisory support via dedicated Slack channel.

## The Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Net New ARR YoY | -12% | +23% |
| Win Rate | 28% | 47% |
| Average Sales Cycle | 103 days | 71 days |
| Average Deal Size | $127K | $164K |
| Customer Churn | 14% | 9% |
| NPS | 23 | 58 |

**Revenue Impact:** +$2.1M annual revenue within 6 months of engagement completion.
