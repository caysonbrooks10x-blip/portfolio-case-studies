# Growth Strategy: Deliverables Inventory

## Phase 1: Strategic Diagnosis — Deliverables

### 1. Root Cause Analysis Report (47 pages)
**Description:** Comprehensive diagnostic document synthesizing all findings from stakeholder interviews, data analysis, and market research.

**Contents:**
- Executive summary of findings
- Methodology documentation
- Stakeholder interview synthesis (34 interviews)
- CRM data analysis findings (2,400+ opportunities, 3-year history)
- Competitive landscape analysis (8 competitors, 47 factors)
- Customer profitability analysis (187 customers)
- Product usage analytics findings
- Root cause identification and prioritization
- Strategic implications memo

**Owner:** Lead Strategy Consultant
**Tools:** Notion, Google Docs
**Sign-off:** Executive team alignment meeting

---

### 2. Competitive Positioning Blueprint
**Description:** Visual and narrative framework defining Velocite's differentiated position in the market.

**Contents:**
- Competitive landscape map (visual)
- Positioning quadrant analysis
- differentiation framework (8 key factors)
- Messaging hierarchy (primary, secondary, tertiary)
- Competitive response playbooks (top 3 competitors)
- Win/loss analysis summary

**Owner:** Lead Strategy Consultant
**Tools:** Miro, Notion
**Sign-off:** CMO and CRO alignment

---

### 3. Ideal Customer Profile Framework with Scoring Model
**Description:** Data-driven ICP definition with a scoring system for lead qualification and territory assignment.

**Contents:**
- ICP attributes definition (firmographic, technographic, behavioral)
- Scoring model methodology
- Scoring rubric (points per attribute)
- Validation analysis against historical data
- Territory assignment guidelines
- Application playbooks for Sales and Marketing

**Owner:** Sales and Marketing jointly
**Tools:** Airtable, HubSpot (scoring models), Excel
**Sign-off:** CRO approval

---

### 4. Revenue Leakage Assessment
**Description:** Analysis of pricing and discounting patterns driving margin erosion.

**Contents:**
- Discount trajectory analysis (18-month trend)
- Deal size distribution by discount level
- Approval exception analysis
- Margin impact modeling
- Pricing governance recommendations
- Discount authority matrix

**Owner:** Finance and Sales jointly
**Tools:** Salesforce (deal data), Excel financial modeling
**Sign-off:** CFO and CRO alignment

---

- Vision and mission alignment

- Strategic pillars (4 pillars with sub-initiatives)
- Resource allocation framework
- Investment requirements by phase
