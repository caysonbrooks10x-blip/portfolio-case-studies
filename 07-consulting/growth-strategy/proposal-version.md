# Velocite Systems: Growth Strategy Proposal

## Prepared for: Meridian Capital Partners / Velocite Systems Leadership

## Proposed Engagement

### Scope

We propose a 16-week growth strategy engagement designed to diagnose the root causes of Velocite's revenue decline and architect a sustainable growth engine. This is not a surface-level assessment or a set of generic best practices—it's a comprehensive, data-driven transformation of how Velocite goes to market.

### Phase 1: Strategic Diagnosis (Weeks 1-4) — $45,000

**Objective:** Establish the factual foundation for strategic decision-making.

**Activities:**
- Stakeholder interviews (target: 30+ across sales, marketing, product, CS, executive)
- CRM data analysis (3-year deal history, 2,400+ opportunities)
- Competitive intelligence audit (8 major competitors, 47 differentiating factors)
- Customer profitability analysis (all 187 current customers)
- Product usage analytics review (feature adoption, engagement patterns)
- Reference customer interviews (target: 12 customers)

**Deliverables:**
- Root Cause Analysis Report
- Competitive Positioning Blueprint
- Ideal Customer Profile Framework with scoring model
- Revenue Leakage Assessment

**Success Criteria:** Documented agreement on root causes; executive team alignment on strategic direction.

---

### Phase 2: Strategy Development (Weeks 5-8) — $55,000

**Objective:** Translate diagnostic insights into a coherent, actionable growth strategy.

**Activities:**
- Strategic options workshop (executive team)
- 3 Horizons growth model development
- Strategic pillar identification and prioritization
- Go-to-market approach redesign
- Pricing and packaging strategy
- Customer success and retention strategy

**Deliverables:**
- Growth Strategy Document (3-year view)
- Go-to-Market Playbook v2.0
- Pricing Strategy Framework
- Customer Success Operating Model
- Strategic initiative priorization matrix

**Success Criteria:** Board-level approval of strategic direction; committed initiative roadmap.

---

### Phase 3: Implementation Planning (Weeks 9-12) — $45,000

**Objective:** Convert strategy into executable plans with clear ownership.

**Activities:**
- Initiative decomposition workshops (functional teams)
- 90-day sprint roadmap development
- Resource and budget planning
- RACI matrix development
- Risk assessment and mitigation planning
- Quick wins identification

**Deliverables:**
- 90-Day Implementation Roadmap
- Functional execution plans (Sales, Marketing, Product, CS)
- RACI accountability framework
- Budget allocation model
- Risk mitigation playbook
- Quick wins execution checklist

**Success Criteria:** All functional leads have committed execution plans; resources allocated.

---

### Phase 4: Execution Support (Weeks 13-16) — $40,000

**Objective:** Ensure strategy translates into action, not just documents.

**Activities:**
- Weekly strategic steering committee (executive level)
- Bi-weekly tactical working sessions (functional leads)
- On-demand advisory support (Slack channel)
- Team training sessions (recorded)
- Issue resolution and course correction

**Deliverables:**
- Weekly status dashboards
- Decision frameworks for common scenarios
- Training materials and documentation
- Transition playbook for post-engagement continuity

**Success Criteria:** 100% of Phase 1 initiatives underway; internal ownership established.
