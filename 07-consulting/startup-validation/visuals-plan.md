## Brand Guidelines

### Color Palette
- Primary: #10b981 (emerald green - health, growth)
- Secondary: #6366f1 (indigo - tech, startup)
- Accent Orange: #f97316 (urgency, pivot)
- Alert Red: #ef4444 (before, problem)
- Background Light: #ecfdf5 (light green tint)
- Background Dark: #064e3b
- Text Primary: #064e3b
- Text Secondary: #64748b

### Typography
- Headlines: Inter Bold / Poppins Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Data/Numbers: JetBrains Mono

### Visual Style
- Health tech aesthetic
- Startup energy
- Data-forward storytelling
- Before/after pivot comparisons
- Platform and ecosystem imagery
