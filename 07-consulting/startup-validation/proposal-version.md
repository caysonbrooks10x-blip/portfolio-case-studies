# Startup Validation + Product Strategy Proposal

## Prepared for: Marcus Webb, CPO and Co-Founder, Vela Health

## Executive Summary

Vela Health has impressive technology—AI that predicts burnout with 87% accuracy—but no product-market fit. After 18 months and $1.4M spent, you have only 12 customers and $28K ARR. You're burning $80K/month with 4-5 months of runway left. Your product works; your market strategy doesn't.

We propose a 12-week startup validation and product strategy engagement that will help you identify the root causes of your traction problem, validate or invalidate your key assumptions, and develop a clear path to product-market fit. The result: a pivot that saves the company, achieves traction within 6 months, and positions you for a successful Series A.

## Proposed Engagement

### Our Philosophy: Startup Problems Are Usually Market Problems

Most founders think startup struggles mean product problems—more features, better tech, cleaner UI. But startup struggles almost always mean market problems: wrong target, wrong positioning, wrong pricing, wrong motion. The good news: market problems are solvable. Product problems often aren't.

For Vela specifically:
- Validate/invalidate target market assumptions
- Identify root causes of traction failure
- Develop pivot strategy (market, positioning, product, pricing)
- Execute initial pivot phase and build traction

**Objective:** Identify root causes of traction failure and validate/invalidate assumptions.

**Activities:**

- Competitive landscape analysis

- Go-to-market model evaluation

**Deliverables:**

**Success Criteria:** Clear understanding of traction failure root causes

---

### Phase 2: Product Strategy Pivot (Weeks 5-8) — $40,000

**Workstream 1: Market Pivot**
- Shift from direct-to-employer to platform/embedded model
- Target: HR tech platforms, benefits platforms, employee experience platforms
- Positioning: "Burnout risk intelligence" for platform partners
- Motion: API-first, embeddable, white-label capable

**Workstream 2: Product Repositioning**
- Reposition from "burnout prediction" to "workforce intelligence"
- Lead with productivity and retention metrics
- Create embeddable dashboards and widgets
- Build API for platform integration

**Workstream 3: Pricing Redesign**
- Drop per-employee pricing to $0.30-0.50/employee/month
- Add platform licensing model ($50K-200K/year)
- Offer freemium tier for developer access

**Workstream 4: GTM Motion Redesign**
- Developer relations program
- Platform partnership program
- Integration marketplace presence
- Technical content and documentation

**Workstream 5: Traction Strategy**
- Target: 5-8 HR tech platforms for initial partnerships
- Develop integration connectors
- Build developer sandbox and documentation

**Deliverables:**
- Pivot Strategy Document
- Product Repositioning Plan
- Pricing Strategy Redesign
- Go-to-Market Playbook
- Platform Partnership Framework

**Success Criteria:** Clear pivot strategy ready for execution
