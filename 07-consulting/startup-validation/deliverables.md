# Startup Validation: Deliverables Inventory

## Phase 1: Customer Discovery — Deliverables

### 1. Customer Segmentation Analysis
**Description:** Comprehensive analysis of market segments with size-of-opportunity estimates.

**Contents:**
- Market sizing by segment (TAM, SAM, SOM)
- Segment definition (firmographic, behavioral, attitudinal)
- Segment attractiveness scoring (size, growth, competition, accessibility)
- Segment prioritization recommendation
- Size-of-opportunity estimates with methodology
- Key findings from customer interviews (synthesized)

**Owner:** CEO, Product, Sales
**Tools:** Notion, Airtable, Miro
**Sign-off:** Founding team alignment

---

### 2. Jobs-to-Be-Done Framework
**Description:** Customer needs analysis using JTBD methodology.

**Contents:**
- Jobs-to-Be-Done statement by segment
- Functional jobs (what customers are trying to accomplish)
- Emotional jobs (how customers want to feel)
- Social jobs (how customers want to be perceived)
- Pain points by job (frequency, intensity, magnitude)
- Existing solutions and their shortcomings
- Opportunity areas for NovaTech

**Owner:** Product, Marketing
**Tools:** Notion, Dovetail, Miro
**Sign-off:** CPO approval

---

### 3. Competitive Landscape Deep-Dive
**Description:** Systematic competitive analysis of 8 key competitors.

**Contents:**
- Competitor profiles (product, pricing, positioning, distribution)
- Feature comparison matrix (47 features across 8 competitors)
- Positioning map (price vs. complexity; target segment focus)
- Win/loss analysis by competitor
- Competitive differentiation opportunities
- Threat assessment (new entrants, substitute solutions)
- Strategic implications

**Owner:** CEO, Product
**Tools:** Notion, Airtable, Miro
**Sign-off:** CEO approval

---

### 4. Product-Market Fit Assessment
**Description:** Scorecard evaluating current product-market fit state.

**Contents:**
- Product-market fit score (0-100) with methodology
- Segment-by-segment fit assessment
- Feature adoption analysis (what's used, what's not)
- Retention cohort analysis
- NPS breakdown by segment
- Willingness-to-recommend verbatim analysis
- Gap analysis (what's missing, what's excessive)
- Strategic recommendations

**Owner:** Product, CEO
**Tools:** Mixpanel, Notion, Airtable
**Sign-off:** Full founding team

---

### 5. Hypothesis Validation Report
**Description:** Evidence-based assessment of core startup hypotheses.

**Contents:**
- Problem hypothesis validation (evidence for/against)
- Solution hypothesis validation
- ICP hypothesis validation
- Pricing hypothesis validation
- Channel hypothesis validation
- Confidence levels for each hypothesis
- Key learnings and implications
- Recommended pivot decisions

**Owner:** Full founding team
**Tools:** Notion, Dovetail
**Sign-off:** CEO decision memo

---

## Phase 2: Strategic Positioning — Deliverables

**Contents:**

- Value proposition framework

- Position sustainability analysis
- Competitive positioning strategy
- Messaging hierarchy (primary, secondary, tertiary)

**Owner:** CEO, Marketing
**Tools:** Notion, Miro
**Sign-off:** Full founding team alignment

---

### 7. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) with Scoring Model
**Description:** Detailed ICP definition with qualification scoring.

**Contents:**
- ICP firmographic criteria (company size, industry, geography)
- ICP technographic criteria (tech stack, digital maturity)
- ICP behavioral criteria (buying signals, engagement patterns)
- ICP psychographic criteria (attitudes, values)
- Scoring rubric (points per attribute)
- Must-have vs. nice-to-have criteria
- Anti-customer profile (who NOT to target)
- Application guide (outbound targeting, inbound qualification)

**Owner:** Sales, Marketing
**Tools:** Airtable, HubSpot
**Sign-off:** CEO and Sales lead

## Phase 3: Product Strategy — Deliverables

### 11. Product Strategy Document (18-Month View)
**Description:** Long-term product roadmap aligned with market needs.

**Contents:**
- Product vision (18-month view)
- Strategic bets (3-5 major initiatives)
- Feature strategy (core, differentiate, table stakes)
- Platform/ex integration strategy
- UX/UI principles
- Technical architecture considerations
- Roadmap by quarter (Q1-Q6)
- Success metrics by initiative
- Resource requirements

**Owner:** CPO, CEO
**Tools:** Notion, Productboard
**Sign-off:** Full founding team + board (if applicable)

---

### 12. Prioritized Feature Roadmap
**Description:** Quarter-by-quarter feature prioritization.

**Contents:**
- Feature inventory (all proposed features)
- RICE/JTBD scoring for each feature
- Prioritization decision framework
- Q1 recommended features (immediate)
- Q2-Q3 features (near-term)
- Q4-Q6 features (medium-term)
- Backlog categorization (must-have, should-have, could-have)
- Dependencies and prerequisites
- Technical effort estimates

**Owner:** Product, Engineering
**Tools:** Productboard, Notion
**Sign-off:** CPO + CTO

---

### 13. MVP Specification for Positioning Pivot
**Description:** Definition of minimum viable product for new positioning.

**Contents:**
- MVP scope definition (features, boundaries)
- User stories for MVP features
- Acceptance criteria for each feature
- Out-of-scope list (explicit)
- Success metrics for MVP
- Timeline and milestones
- Rollout plan (phased vs. big bang)
- Validation plan (how we'll measure success)

**Owner:** Product, Engineering
**Tools:** Notion, Productboard
**Sign-off:** CPO

---

### 14. User Research Summary
**Description:** Synthesized findings from all user research.

**Contents:**
- Research methodology documentation
- Participant descriptions (anonymized)
- Key findings by research type
- Usability testing findings
- Interview transcript synthesis
- Verbatim quotes (organized by theme)
- Behavioral patterns identified
- Recommendations for product team

**Owner:** Product
**Tools:** Dovetail, Notion
**Sign-off:** CPO

---

### 15. Technical Debt Assessment
**Description:** Evaluation of technical debt implications for roadmap.

**Contents:**
- Technical debt inventory (major items)
- Impact assessment (on velocity, reliability, scalability)
- Prioritization recommendations
- Remediation roadmap
- Trade-off analysis (fix vs. work-around)
- Resource implications
- Tool/architecture recommendations

**Owner:** CTO, Engineering
**Tools:** Notion, GitHub (code analysis)
**Sign-off:** CTO
