# Service Business Scaling: Elevate Consulting Group

## The Challenge

A boutique HR consulting firm with $8.2M revenue was trapped in the founder ceiling—the CEO was involved in 85% of all engagements, couldn't hire consultants fast enough, had no marketing or lead generation (100% referrals), and had no methodology, training program, or scalable business model. They were profitable but not scalable.

## The Approach

12-week service business scaling framework project:

**Phase 1: Business Model Assessment**
Financial analysis, founder dependency analysis, service offering analysis. We identified the founder-dependent business model as the core constraint.

**Phase 2: Scaling Framework Development**
Five pillars: methodology & intellectual property, service design & packaging, demand generation engine, talent development system, and operations infrastructure.

**Phase 3 & 4: Roadmap & Launch**
12-month implementation roadmap, HubSpot CRM, first strategic hires, content marketing launched, execution governance established.

## The Results

| Metric | Before | After (18 mo) |
|--------|--------|---------------|
| Revenue | $8.2M | $14.8M |
| Founder Billable Time | 85% | 30% |
| Consultants | 18 | 42 |
| Inbound Leads | 0 | 85/mo |
| EBITDA Margin | 14.6% | 23% |
