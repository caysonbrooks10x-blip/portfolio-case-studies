# Service Business Scaling Framework Case Study: Pinnacle Advisory Group

## The Challenge

A wealth management firm with $2.1B AUM and $18M revenue had hit a ceiling. Advisors at full capacity, revenue plateaued for 3 years, and 60% of the firm's revenue depended on one person—the founder. The business was successful, but unscalable.

## The Approach

Over 24 weeks, we designed and implemented a comprehensive scaling framework:

**Phase 1: Business Model Diagnosis**
Financial analysis, client segmentation, advisor productivity assessment, technology audit. We identified the core problem: the business model was constrained by advisor time. Every dollar of revenue required an advisor's hours.

**Phase 2: Scaling Framework Design**
Four interconnected workstreams: tiered service model (Premier, Elite, Signature), operating system (workflow management and automation), knowledge management (proprietary methodology and client portal), and growth engine (referral program and thought leadership).

**Phase 3 & 4: Implementation & Optimization**
New service tiers launched, Orbit workflow system deployed, Pinnacle Method training program implemented, client referral program launched, advisor coaching completed.

## The Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Annual Revenue | $18M | $27M |
| Revenue per Advisor | $1.0M | $1.35M |
| Client Referral Rate | 12% | 34% |
| Client Retention | 91% | 97% |
| Advisor Turnover | 11% | 3% |
| Founder Concentration | 60% | 31% |

**Plus:** 3 new service lines generating $3.68M in Year 1
