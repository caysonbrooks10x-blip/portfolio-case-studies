# Cost Reduction + Process Redesign Proposal

## Prepared for: Thomas Brennan, CEO, Pinnacle Manufacturing

## Executive Summary

Pinnacle Manufacturing has margin compression—but it's not inevitable. Your EBITDA margin has declined from 16% to 11% over two years. You have $8M+ in waste that can be eliminated without sacrificing quality or customer service. And you can build a continuous improvement culture that sustains savings post-engagement.

We propose a 12-week cost reduction and process redesign engagement that will identify and implement $8M+ in annualized savings, improve EBITDA margin from 11% to 16%+, and build a sustainable continuous improvement culture. The result: substantial cost reduction AND a capability that keeps giving.

---

## Proposed Engagement

### Phase 1: Cost Assessment (Weeks 1-3) — $30,000
- Financial analysis, material cost analysis, labor productivity study, overhead analysis, quality cost analysis, supplier benchmarking.

### Phase 2: Implementation (Weeks 4-10) — $85,000
- Material cost reduction (supplier consolidation, volume purchasing, scrap reduction), labor productivity (lean, cellular, cross-training), overhead reduction, quality improvement, continuous improvement culture.

### Phase 3: Sustainment (Weeks 11-12) — $30,000
- Cost tracking, CI program handover, training, dashboards.
