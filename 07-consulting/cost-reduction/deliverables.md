# Cost Reduction + Process Redesign: Deliverables

## Meridian Healthcare Engagement

## Phase 1: Cost Analysis & Opportunity Identification

### 1.1 Comprehensive Cost Analysis Report
**Description:** Complete analysis of cost structure across all 8 hospitals and 24 clinics
**Components:**
- Cost structure by category (labor, supply, overhead, facilities)
- Cost structure by hospital and service line
- Cost trends over 5 years
- Benchmark comparison vs. health system industry
- Cost reduction opportunity identification
- Risk assessment for each opportunity
- Prioritization framework

**Format:** 65-page report + executive summary
**Owner:** Cayson Brooks
**Reviewer:** Patricia Hernandez, CFO

---

### 1.2 Supply Chain Opportunity Assessment
**Description:** Detailed analysis of supply chain costs and optimization opportunities
**Components:**
- Vendor analysis (47 vendors, 12 pharmaceutical distributors)
- Contract analysis (pricing, terms, compliance)
- Utilization analysis (actual vs. contracted volumes)
- Price variance analysis (23 different price points)
- GPO contract optimization opportunities
- Inventory optimization opportunities
- Just-in-time delivery feasibility

**Format:** 35-page assessment + Excel workbook (vendors, contracts, savings)
**Owner:** Supply Chain Expert
**Reviewer:** Michael Torres, VP Supply Chain

---

### 1.3 Administrative Overhead Analysis
**Description:** Analysis of administrative functions and optimization opportunities
**Components:**
- FTE analysis by function, by hospital (1,840 FTEs)
- Process mapping (HR, payroll, IT, finance, procurement)
- Technology assessment (12 HR systems, 8 payroll systems)
- Benchmark comparison (admin FTEs per bed vs. industry)
- Shared services feasibility analysis
- Automation opportunity identification
- Cost reduction opportunity quantification

**Format:** 40-page assessment + Excel workbook (FTEs, processes, savings)
**Owner:** Cayson Brooks
**Reviewer:** Patricia Hernandez, James Wilson, VP HR

---

### 1.4 Clinical Workflow Optimization Study
**Description:** Analysis of clinical workflows and administrative burden
**Components:**
- Time-motion studies (nursing, physicians, allied health)
- Documentation burden analysis
- Supply management burden analysis
- EHR utilization analysis (Epic workflows)
- Burnout assessment (Maslach Burnout Inventory)
- Turnover cost analysis
- Clinical productivity opportunity identification

**Format:** 45-page assessment + video documentation
**Owner:** Healthcare IT Consultant, Process Improvement Specialist
**Reviewer:** Dr. Robert Kim, Sandra Powell

---

### 1.5 Opportunity Prioritization Matrix
**Description:** Ranked list of opportunities with impact and risk analysis
**Components:**
- Opportunity listing (50+ opportunities identified)
- Impact scoring (financial, operational, strategic)
- Risk scoring (clinical quality, staff morale, implementation)
- ROI calculation for each opportunity
- Implementation sequencing recommendation
- Quick wins vs. strategic investments analysis
- Board presentation preparation

**Format:** Prioritized matrix + implementation roadmap + board deck
**Owner:** Cayson Brooks
**Reviewer:** Patricia Hernandez, Steering Committee
