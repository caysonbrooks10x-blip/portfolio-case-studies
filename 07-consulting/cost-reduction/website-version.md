# Cost Reduction Case Study: Meridian Healthcare Systems

## The Challenge

A 14-clinic healthcare network was operating at 6% operating margin—half the industry benchmark. With $78M in revenue but a $4.2M operating loss, the PE-owned organization needed rapid transformation to support their growth strategy.

The symptoms were everywhere: $2.1M in annual claims rework, 23% patient no-show rate, wildly inconsistent staffing across locations, and an EHR system used at only 40% capacity despite millions in investment.

## The Approach

Over 16 weeks, we conducted a comprehensive cost reduction and process redesign engagement:

**Phase 1: Diagnostic Assessment**
Site visits to all 14 clinics, financial analysis, revenue cycle audit, EHR utilization assessment, staffing model analysis. We identified four major cost drivers: revenue cycle dysfunction ($2.9M), staffing inefficiency ($1.8M), no-shows ($1.4M), and EHR underutilization ($800K).

**Phase 2: Process Redesign**
Four parallel workstreams: revenue cycle transformation (brought RCM in-house, new Waystar platform), staffing optimization (evidence-based model, floating pools), no-show reduction (multi-channel reminders, wait-list management), and EHR optimization (enabled underutilized features).

**Phase 3 & 4: Implementation & Sustainability**
New RCM platform go-live, staffing model rollout across 14 locations, patient reminder system implementation, KPI monitoring dashboards, and knowledge transfer.

## The Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Operating Margin | 6% | 18% |
| Annual Cost Savings | — | $4.7M |
| Claims Denial Rate | 18% | 7% |
| Patient Wait Times | 45 min | 18 min |
| No-Show Rate | 23% | 9% |
| Patient Satisfaction | 3.2/5 | 4.6/5 |
| Staff Utilization | 62% | 81% |

**Financial Impact: $4.7M annual savings + $8.8M revenue improvement**
