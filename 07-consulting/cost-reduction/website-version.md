# Cost Reduction + Process Redesign: Meridian Healthcare

## The Challenge

An $1.8B regional healthcare system with 8 hospitals and 12,000 employees faced operating margin decline—from 3.2% to 0.8% over three years. Cash reserves were depleting. The board mandated $40M in cost reductions. Previous cost cutting attempts had failed, compromising nurse morale and increasing turnover.

## The Approach

20-week cost reduction and process redesign project:

**Phase 1: Cost Analysis**
Financial analysis, supply chain assessment, administrative overhead analysis, clinical workflow analysis. We identified $47M in opportunities across supply chain, administrative, and clinical productivity.

**Phase 2: Process Redesign**
Four workstreams: supply chain standardization, administrative shared services, clinical workflow optimization, and technology implementation. We redesigned systems before implementing technology.

**Phase 3 & 4: Implementation & Optimization**
New vendor contracts, Workday HRIS, ADP payroll, ScanVan inventory, BCMA deployment, RPA implementation, and continuous improvement systems.

## The Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Operating Margin | 0.8% | 4.1% |
| Annual Cost Savings | — | $47M |
| Supply Chain Costs | $520M | $406M |
| Administrative Overhead | $180M | $124M |
| Nurse Turnover Rate | 24% | 14% |
| Patient Satisfaction | 68th pct | 76th pct |

## Tools & Methods

This engagement leveraged:
- Workday, ADP Workforce Now, UiPath RPA (HR, payroll, automation)
- Epic EHR optimization, BCMA, ScanVan (clinical technology)
- Power BI, Tableau, Oracle Financials (analytics and financials)
- ServiceNow, Microsoft 365 (IT and collaboration)
