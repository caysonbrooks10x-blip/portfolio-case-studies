## 01 — AI & Machine Learning

### Proposal Snippet: AI Client Intake Automation

When Meridian Legal Solutions came to us, they had a problem common to high-value service firms: their best people — the partners and senior associates — were spending 6+ hours per week answering the same qualification questions that a well-trained assistant could handle. Their response time averaged 4.2 hours, which meant 47% of after-hours leads were cold by the time anyone followed up. In legal services, where client relationships start at first contact, this was silently killing deals they never knew they lost.

We built a multi-channel AI intake system — deployed across their website, WhatsApp, and SMS — trained on 200+ documents of firm-specific knowledge. The bot qualifies prospects using a 7-factor matrix, routes urgent matters to on-call attorneys, and books consultations directly into Calendly. Critically, it integrated with their Clio CRM so no lead ever fell through the cracks. The result: response time dropped from 4.2 hours to 47 seconds. Qualified leads increased 340%. Cost per lead fell from $47 to under $9. The firm attributed $3.8M in annual revenue to the new intake system — and the project paid back in 4.1 months.

Our approach to AI automation isn't about replacing your team. It's about ensuring your team spends their expertise where it actually matters. We'll build a system that handles the repeatable, the routine, and the time-sensitive — so your people can focus on the complex, the relationship-driven, and the high-value work that justifies your billing rate.

## 02 — Cybersecurity

### Proposal Snippet: Enterprise Security Audit

NovaPay is a Series B fintech processing $40M in monthly transactions. When they came to us, they'd had a near-miss security incident — the kind that makes investors nervous and enterprise prospects disappear. Their Series C was 90 days away. Enterprise deals were being blocked by security questionnaires they couldn't answer. And they'd never had a comprehensive third-party assessment.

Over six weeks, our four-person security engineering team conducted a hybrid assessment: automated scanning with Burp Suite and OWASP ZAP, manual API penetration testing, full source code review, and architecture threat modeling. We found 47 vulnerabilities — including three critical severity issues that would have allowed account takeover and unauthorized transaction execution. The critical findings alone were worth the engagement: authentication bypass via parameter tampering, JWT algorithm confusion, and insecure direct object reference in their transaction API.

Sixty days later, after following our prioritized remediation roadmap, NovaPay had zero critical or high-severity vulnerabilities. They closed their Series C for $18M. They unlocked a $2.4M enterprise ARR deal that had been blocked by the security questionnaire. And they'd established a security champion program to maintain that posture as they continue scaling. The $78K investment generated over $20M in measurable outcome. That's not a security audit — it's a business unlock.

## 05 — Copywriting

### Proposal Snippet: Landing Page Rewrite

Meridian Consulting was a well-established executive coaching firm with a credible reputation and a website that was quietly costing them hundreds of thousands of dollars per year. Their conversion rate was 1.8%. Their cost per lead was $340. They were generating volume, but the leads that came through weren't qualified for a $15,000 engagement and the sales cycle stretched to 90+ days. The website was describing what the firm did. It needed to describe what clients actually got.

We restructured the landing page using a pain-first AIDA framework — the same framework used in direct response advertising, adapted for high-ticket B2B. The hero shifted from firm credentials to prospect pain points. Generic testimonials ("Great coaching!") were replaced with quantified, outcome-specific results ("Revenue up 40% in 8 months"). The "Discovery Call" was reframed as a "Strategy Alignment Session." Mobile-first CTA placement put the conversion mechanism within thumb reach. A new trust architecture made the premium price feel not just reasonable but necessary.

Result: conversion rate improved from 1.8% to 5.2% — a 189% increase. Cost per lead dropped from $340 to $118. Within 90 days of launch, the page had generated $340K in new pipeline from inbound requests. The investment was $18,500. The return was measurable, immediate, and ongoing.

## 06 — UI/UX Design

### Proposal Snippet: SaaS Dashboard Redesign

Prism Analytics had a powerful analytics platform. Their customers couldn't use it. Task completion rates sat at 34%. Their NPS had dropped from 42 to 28 in two years. Enterprise renewal rates were falling — procurement teams were seeing the demo and passing. Support tickets were climbing as users called in to accomplish tasks the product should have guided them through. The problem wasn't the analytics engine — it was the experience layer built on top of it.

We conducted a full UX overhaul: 140 screens across three fidelity levels, a 47-component design system in Figma, an interactive prototype for user testing, and a Storybook component library for the engineering handoff. Outcomes at launch: task completion jumped from 34% to 78%. Time-to-first-insight dropped from 47 minutes to 8 minutes. NPS improved from 28 to 61. Enterprise renewal rates recovered from 71% to 94%. Support tickets fell 80%. The product that was losing enterprise deals started winning them. The redesign didn't just fix the UX — it changed what was possible for the business.

The CEO who was drowning became the CEO who was scaling.
