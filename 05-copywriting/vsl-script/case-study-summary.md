# Case Study Summary: VSL Script
## Velocity Coaching — 4.1x ROAS on $50K Ad Spend

---

**Project:** Video sales letter script for $12,500 high-ticket coaching program  
**Problem:** Webinar bottleneck, limited scale, 8% attendee-to-buyer conversion  
**Solution:** Direct-to-video funnel with problem-agitation-solution narrative  
**Result:** 11.2% landing page CVR, 4.1x ROAS, $38 cost per application

---

**What Changed:**
- VSL funnel replaced webinar requirement (scale without live events)
- Problem-agitation-solution structure with social proof stacking
- Objection handling built into script (not saved for sales call)
- Authentic scarcity (15-person cohort, real waitlist)

**Investment:** $15,000 + $3K/month × 2 months | **Timeline:** 4 weeks  
**Revenue Impact:** $155K net profit from $50K ad spend

**Tools:** Google Docs, Zoom, Camtasia, Kajabi, Loom
