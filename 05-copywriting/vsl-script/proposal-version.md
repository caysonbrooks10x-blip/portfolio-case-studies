## Problem Statement

Your webinar funnel is converting at 8% (attendee to buyer). That's solid — but the webinar model has a ceiling.

You need live attendance to run it. That limits scale, creates scheduling friction, and requires you to be "on" every time. More importantly, cold traffic has to raise their hand and commit to a 60-minute call before they've learned anything. That's a high-friction entry point.

A VSL funnel solves this. Cold traffic → landing page → pre-recorded video → application. The video does the heavy lifting: pre-qualifies, pre-sells, handles objections. Your sales team (or you) closes the ones who apply.

But here's the thing: VSLs live or die on the script. The video production can be perfect, but if the copy doesn't convert, nothing else matters.

That's what I'm going to write.

---

## Proposed Solution

A complete VSL script designed to convert at 10%+ of landing page visitors into applications.

### Structure

1. **Hook** — Acknowledge the skepticism immediately. Don't pretend the audience trusts you.
2. **Problem** — Describe the actual constraint in their language, not coach-speak.
3. **Agitation** — Why the obvious solutions fail. Make them feel understood.
4. **Proof** — Jason's story + client transformations. Specific, not vague.
5. **Offer** — What Velocity actually is. Clear, specific, value-stacked.
6. **Objection Handling** — Address the main reasons people don't apply.
7. **Urgency** — Real scarcity. Cohort-based, limited spots, no manufactured pressure.
8. **CTA** — Clear next step. Low-friction application process.

### Format

- Full script with on-camera directions
- Client testimonial segments (with guidance on what to film)
- Notes on pacing, tone, and emphasis
- Alternate openings and closings for testing
