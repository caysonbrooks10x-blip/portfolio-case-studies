# Case Study Summary: Product Page Conversion Copy
## Lumina Skincare — 340% Add-to-Cart Increase

---

**Project:** E-commerce product page copy for premium skincare brand  
**Problem:** 1.8% CVR, ingredient-focused copy, no urgency  
**Solution:** Outcome-first positioning, benefit storytelling, real scarcity  
**Result:** 5.4% CVR (+200%), 28.1% add-to-cart (+243%), $1.6M+ annual revenue impact

---

**What Changed:**
- Descriptive titles → Evocative outcome headlines
- Ingredient lists → Benefit storytelling
- Generic testimonials → Specific, outcome-focused reviews
- No urgency → Real batch-production scarcity

**Investment:** $16,000 | **Timeline:** 5 weeks  
**Revenue Impact:** $135K/month additional, $1.6M+ annualized

**Tools:** Google Analytics, Hotjar, Shopify, Gorgias, Google Docs, Notion
