## 3. Testimonial Sourcing Guide
**File:** `lumina-testimonial-guide.pdf`

Questions to ask customers for better reviews:
- Outcome questions ("What were you hoping to change?")
- Timing questions ("When did you first notice a difference?")
- Specificity prompts ("Give me a number if you can")
- Comparison questions ("How was this different from other products you've tried?")

Template outreach email for review collection.

Implementation notes for Shopify apps.
