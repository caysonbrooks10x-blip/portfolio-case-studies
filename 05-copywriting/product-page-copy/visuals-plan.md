# Visuals Plan: Product Page Conversion Copy
## Lumina Skincare — Photography & UX Recommendations

---

## Visual Strategy Overview

The new copy tells a story of transformation and clinical efficacy. The visuals must support that story without competing with it.

**Core principles:**
- Product is the hero — not buried in lifestyle
- Skin context (before/after, texture) where appropriate
- Clean, minimal backgrounds that let the product shine
- Consistent lighting across all hero shots (warm, not clinical)

---

## Hero Image Guidelines

### Primary Hero Shot

**Composition:**
- Product centered, slightly off-center (rule of thirds)
- Simple background (marble, concrete, or clean white)
- Morning/lnatural light from 45-degree angle
- Subtle shadow for depth
- Room for text overlay on collection pages

**Technical specs:**
- Resolution: 3000×3000px minimum
- Format: PNG with transparency (for variant use)
- Color space: sRGB
- White background version: #FFFFFF

**Examples:**
- [Product on white marble with lemon/citrus slices]
- [Product on concrete with botanicals]
- [Product held in hand (lifestyle)]

### Lifestyle Context Shots

**Purpose:** Show the product in use — morning routine, bathroom shelf, travel

**Composition:**
- Natural settings (bathroom counter, bedroom, travel bag)
- Warm, natural lighting
- Other Lumina products visible (builds routine context)
- Human elements (hands applying product, open cabinet)

---

## Before/After Photography

### Guidelines

**Authenticity:** Real results, unedited or minimally edited (FTC compliance)

**Consistency:** Same lighting, same angle, same camera for all before/afters

**Standardization:**
- Neutral expression
- No makeup in before/after
- Same lighting setup
- Same camera distance

**Disclosure:** "Individual results may vary" or "Results from clinical study, n=X"

---

## Texture/Detail Shots

### Purpose
Show product texture, absorption, and skin interaction

**Shots to capture:**
- Serum texture (on glass or skin)
- Cream texture (swatch on skin)
- Absorption sequence (0 seconds, 30 seconds, 60 seconds)
- Packaging details (pump, bottle, cap)

---

## Packaging Photography

### Clean Packaging Shots
- All angles (front, back, 45-degree)
- Ingredient list clearly visible
- Volume/weight clearly visible
- Batch number visible (for authenticity)

### Usage Context
- Open bottle/pump in use
- Dropper (for serums)
- Pump action (for moisturizers)

---

## Typography for Graphics

### Font Recommendations
- **Headlines:** Inter or Helvetica Neue (clean, modern)
- **Body:** Same family, lighter weight
- **Accent text:** Small caps for "Dermatologist formulated" type claims

### Size Guidelines
- **On-product text:** Minimum 12px (readable at thumbnail size)
- **Feature callouts:** 14-16px
- **Price:** 24px+

---

## Mobile Considerations

### Image Optimization
- Hero images must load fast (WebP, <200KB)
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 for grid views
- Thumbnails: 500×500px minimum

### Layout
- Hero image first (above fold)
- Thumbnails below or as carousel
- Avoid horizontal scrolling for images

---

## User-Generated Content (UGC)

### Strategy

### Review Imagery

- Screenshots of reviews with their photo (with permission)

- **Shopify** — Image optimization and upload

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Over-edited before/afters** — FTC compliance and authenticity
2. **Inconsistent lighting** — Products must look the same in all shots
3. **Busy backgrounds** — Compete with product for attention
4. **Tiny text in images** — Mobile viewers can't read small details
5. **Missing alt text** — Accessibility and SEO impact
