## Conversion Funnel Analysis

| Stage | Before | After | Improvement |
|-------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Visitor → View Product | 100% | 100% | — |
| View → Add to Cart | 8.2% | 28.1% | +243% |
| Add to Cart → Checkout | 22% | 19% | -14% |
| Checkout → Purchase | 68% | 71% | +4% |
| **Overall CVR** | **1.8%** | **5.4%** | **+200%** |

**Key insight:** The improvement was almost entirely in the Add-to-Cart stage. The new copy influenced the initial decision, not the checkout process.

## A/B Test Results (Headlines)

**The Radiance Protocol:**

| Headline Variant | Traffic Split | CVR | Winner? |
|-----------------|---------------|-----|---------|
| "The Radiance Protocol — Wake Up Your Skin" | 33% | 6.1% | — |
| "Visible Brightening in 14 Days" | 34% | 6.4% | ✓ (control) |
| "The Vitamin C Serum Your Sensitive Skin Has Been Waiting For" | 33% | 5.8% | — |

**Finding:** Outcome-specific headlines ("Visible Brightening in 14 Days") outperformed brand-forward headlines.

## Customer Feedback (Post-Implementation)

**Review Quality Improvement:**
- Before: Average review length: 12 words, generic praise
- After: Average review length: 47 words, specific outcomes mentioned

**Return Rate Decrease:**
- Before: 8.4% return rate
- After: 5.1% return rate
- **Hypothesis:** Better copy set more accurate expectations, reducing mismatch returns

**Repeat Purchase Rate:**
- Before: 23% (90-day)
- After: 31% (90-day, tracked)
- **Hypothesis:** Customers knew what they were buying, so they bought with confidence
