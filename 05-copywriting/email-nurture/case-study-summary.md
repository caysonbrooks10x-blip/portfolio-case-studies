# Case Study Summary: Email Nurture + Launch Sequence
## ArcLight Courses — $180K in 30 Days

---

**Project:** Email nurture system + product launch sequence  
**Problem:** $31K revenue from 47K list (0.07% CVR), no automation, no segmentation  
**Solution:** Strategic launch arc, behavioral triggers, welcome sequence, segmentation  
**Result:** $180K in 30 days (+481%), 0.38% CVR, 89 unsubscribes (-74%)

---

**What Changed:**
- 30-day launch sequence with problem-first, trust-building arc
- Welcome sequence for new subscribers (Day 1-30)
- Behavioral triggers: browse/cart abandonment, re-engagement, post-purchase
- List segmentation by engagement and purchase history
- Scarcity and urgency that was real (cohort-based)

**Investment:** $22,000 | **Timeline:** 10 weeks  
**Revenue Impact:** $180K in 30 days from cold list

**Tools:** Klaviyo, Google Sheets, Notion, Zoom, Typeform
