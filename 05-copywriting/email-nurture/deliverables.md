## Phase 1: Discovery

### 1. List Analysis Report
**File:** `archlight-list-analysis.xlsx`

- Segment breakdown (engagement, purchase history, course interest)
- Engagement metrics by segment
- Revenue attribution by segment
- Opportunity identification
- Cold list health assessment

### 2. ESP Audit
**File:** `archlight-esp-audit.docx`

- Current automation inventory
- Gaps in trigger coverage
- Configuration issues (deliverability, list hygiene)
- Recommended ESP settings changes

### 3. Competitive Review
**File:** `archlight-competitor-email-analysis.pdf`

- 6 competitor email programs analyzed
- Subject line patterns
- Send frequency and timing
- Sequence structures
- Differentiation opportunities

## Phase 2: Launch Sequence

### 4. 30-Day Launch Sequence
**File:** `archlight-launch-sequence.klaviyo` (import ready) + PDF backup

12 emails for the Cloud Architecture Professional launch:

| Email | Day | Purpose | Subject Line |
|-------|-----|---------|--------------|
| 1 | 1 | Problem seeding | "The cloud job market just shifted" |
| 2 | 3 | Problem deepening | "What cloud architects actually do" |
| 3 | 5 | Trust/social proof | "The certification that changed my career" |
| 4 | 8 | Credibility | "Why most cloud courses teach you to pass, not do" |
| 5 | 10 | Credibility | "Meet your instructor" |
| 6 | 12 | Curriculum | "The curriculum, deconstructed" |
| 7 | 15 | Offer presentation | "What the program includes" |
| 8 | 18 | Price anchoring | "The price" |
| 9 | 20 | Scarcity | "The enrollment window" |
| 10 | 24 | Last proof | "One more reason this works" |
| 11 | 27 | Urgency | "Enrollment closes tonight" |
| 12 | 29 | Soft re-engagement | "A question for you" |

Each email includes: Subject line, preview text, body copy, CTA, timing notes.

### 5. Segment-Specific Variants
**File:** `archlight-segment-variants.docx`

- Warm list (engaged, buyers): Full sequence
- Warm list (engaged, non-buyers): Full sequence + extra proof
- Cold list (no engagement 60+ days): Re-engagement sequence first
- Recent purchasers (30 days): Offer removed, referral emphasis

## Phase 4: Behavioral Triggers

### 8. Browse Abandonment Trigger
**File:** `archlight-browse-abandonment.klaviyo`

- Trigger: 3+ course page views, no purchase in 7 days
- 3-email sequence
- Personalized product recommendations
- Social proof for specific courses

### 9. Cart Abandonment Trigger
**File:** `archlight-cart-abandonment.klaviyo`

- Trigger: Add to cart, no purchase in 48 hours
- 2-email sequence
- Cart contents referenced
- Small incentive (free add-on) on second email

### 10. Re-Engagement Trigger
**File:** `archlight-reengagement.klaviyo`

- Trigger: No open in 60 days
- 4-email sequence
- "We miss you" → "Here's what's new" → "Last chance" → "Goodbye"
- Suppression at final email
