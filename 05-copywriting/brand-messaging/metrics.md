# Metrics: Brand Messaging + Offer Positioning

|--------|-------|-------------------|

## Post-Implementation Results (6 Months)

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Average Deal Size | $185,000 | $267,000 | +44% |
| Win Rate | 34% | 52% | +53% |
| Average Sales Cycle | 87 days | 61 days | -30% |
| Pipeline Value | $2.1M | $3.4M | +62% |
| Inbound Inquiry Rate | 12% | 31% | +158% |
| Revenue (Annualized) | $4.2M | $7.0M | +67% |
| Client Retention Rate | 73% | 78% | +7% |

---

## Revenue Impact

**Annual revenue increase:** $4.2M → $7.0M = **$2.8M incremental**

**Pipeline improvement:** $2.1M → $3.4M = **$1.3M additional pipeline**

**Win rate improvement:** 34% → 52% = **53% more deals closed**

---

## Sales Funnel Analysis

| Stage | Before | After | Change |
|-------|--------|-------|--------|
| Inbound Inquiry → Meeting | 58% | 72% | +24% |
| Meeting → Proposal | 44% | 61% | +39% |
| Proposal → Close | 34% | 52% | +53% |
| **Overall Close Rate** | **34%** | **52%** | **+53%** |

**Key insight:** The biggest improvement was in the Inquiry → Meeting stage (+24%), indicating that the new positioning was resonating immediately with prospects.

---

## Deal Size Distribution

| Deal Size Range | Before | After |
|-----------------|--------|-------|
| <$100K | 42% | 18% |
| $100K-$200K | 31% | 29% |
| $200K-$400K | 19% | 38% |
| >$400K | 8% | 15% |

**Key insight:** The shift in deal distribution (+19% to $200K-$400K tier) shows the positioning attracted larger, more strategic deals.

---

## Lead Quality Analysis

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Average Lead Score | 42 | 68 |
| "Strategic fit" mentions in discovery calls | 12% | 41% |
| Price objections in first call | 67% | 31% |
| Competitive mentions in first call | 89% | 47% |

**Key insight:** Leads arrived with higher intent and fewer price objections — indicating positioning attracted the right prospects.

---

## Inbound vs. Outbound Performance

| Channel | Win Rate Before | Win Rate After | Change |
|---------|-----------------|----------------|--------|
| Inbound | 41% | 64% | +56% |
| Outbound | 28% | 38% | +36% |
| Referral | 58% | 71% | +22% |

**Key insight:** The positioning improvement had the biggest impact on inbound leads (+56%), confirming that the new messaging resonated with prospects finding Northridge organically.

---

## Client Retention (Post-Implementation)

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| 12-month retention | 73% | 78% |
| Referral rate | 23% | 34% |
| Net Promoter Score | 47 | 62 |

**Key insight:** Not only did win rates improve, but existing clients became better advocates.

## Competitive Win/Loss Analysis
