# Case Study Summary: Brand Messaging + Offer Positioning
## Northridge Partners — 67% Revenue Increase

---

**Project:** Brand messaging and offer positioning for B2B consulting firm  
**Problem:** Generic consulting positioning, price competition, 34% win rate  
**Solution:** Problem-first positioning, relationship over process, mid-market differentiation  
**Result:** 52% win rate (+53%), $267K avg deal (+44%), $7M revenue (+67%)

---

**What Changed:**
- "Operational transformation" → "The continuity you need when everything is changing"
- Process language → Relationship language (presence, accountability, continuity)
- Methodology focus → Moment focus (when pressure is on)
- Generic positioning → Mid-market differentiation (serious capability without enterprise overhead)

**Investment:** $28,000 | **Timeline:** 8 weeks  
**Revenue Impact:** $2.8M annualized increase (67% growth)

**Tools:** Google Docs, Notion, Zoom, Miro, Google Slides
