## Problem Statement

NovaTech is spending $180,000/month on Google Ads and LinkedIn. At 1.2x ROAS, you're essentially burning VC money to acquire customers who pay for themselves over 18+ months. That's not a growth strategy — that's a subsidy.

The root cause isn't budget or product. It's ad copy that positions NovaTech as a generic category player instead of a specific solution for a specific problem.

"Project management for engineering teams" is what every competitor says. When everyone says the same thing, the only differentiators are price and brand — and you're not winning either.

---

## Proposed Solution

Build a complete ad copy system: not just better ads, but a repeatable framework for generating high-converting ad variations that can scale profitably.

### Core Components

1. **Strategic Positioning** — Shift from category advertising to problem advertising. "Stop missing sprint deadlines" vs. "project management software."

2. **Search Intent Matching** — Every ad group gets a dedicated landing page. Someone searching "Jira alternative" sees comparison copy. Someone searching "sprint planning tool" sees problem-solution copy. Relevance = Quality Score = lower CPL.

3. **Modular Copy Templates** — 40+ proven templates organized by audience segment and funnel stage. Your team can generate new ad variations in 10 minutes without sacrificing conversion quality.

4. **Negative Keyword Architecture** — Systematic elimination of wasted spend on queries that will never convert.

5. **Testing Protocol** — A structured testing framework so we can identify winners quickly and scale what works.
