## A/B Test Results

### Headline Test (Category 1: Sprint Planning)

| Headline | CTR | CVR | Winner? |
|----------|-----|-----|---------|
| "Stop Missing Sprint Deadlines" | 4.2% | 22.1% | ✓ Control |
| "Sprint Planning Is a Mess" | 3.8% | 20.4% | — |
| "Your Team Has Skills. The Process Is Broken." | 4.1% | 21.8% | — |
| "87% Sprint Accuracy in 60 Days" | 5.1% | 23.9% | ✓ New winner |

**Result:** "87% Sprint Accuracy in 60 Days" (specificity + outcome + timeframe) outperformed emotional framing.

### CTA Test

| CTA | CTR | CVR | Winner? |
|-----|-----|-----|---------|
| "Start Free Trial" | 3.2% | 19.4% | Control |
| "See It in Action" | 2.9% | 17.8% | — |
| "14-Day Free Trial, No CC" | 4.1% | 22.6% | ✓ Winner |

**Result:** Low-commitment CTAs with specificity ("14-day," "no CC") outperformed action CTAs.

## What the Data Taught Us

1. **Specificity beats emotion in B2B SaaS** — "87% Sprint Accuracy" outperformed "Sprint Planning Is a Mess" despite being less emotionally evocative.

2. **Low-commitment CTAs win for trials** — "14-Day Free Trial, No CC" outperformed "Start Free Trial" by 28%.

3. **Intent matching matters more than copy quality** — The dedicated landing pages (4x CVR improvement) contributed as much as the ad copy (2x CTR improvement).

4. **Negative keywords are invisible but massive** — Cutting 73% of wasted spend sounds painful but was the single biggest lever for ROAS improvement.
