- **Avoid:** Stock photos of people at computers, handshake images, lightbulbs

### Video Concepts (15-30 sec)
- **Concept 1:** Screen recording of NovaTech sprint board with "before/after" velocity stats
- **Concept 2:** "Interview" format with engineering leader discussing sprint problems, then solution
- **Concept 3:** Simple animation showing blocker cascade vs. NovaTech surfacing blockers early
