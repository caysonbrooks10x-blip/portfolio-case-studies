# Talking Points: Ad Copy System
## NovaTech Case Study

---

## For Sales Calls & Pitches

### The Core Problem We Solved

"They were spending $180K/month on ads and getting 1.2x ROAS — essentially break-even. The issue wasn't product quality (their NPS was 62) or market fit (retention was 89%). It was that their ads were advertising a category, not a solution. 'Project management for engineering teams' is exactly what every competitor says. You can't win a bidding war when everyone sounds the same."

### What Made the Difference

1. **Problem-first positioning** — "We shifted from 'project management software' to 'stop missing sprint deadlines.' That specific pain resonates 3x harder than generic category claims."

2. **Intent matching** — "Every ad group directed to a dedicated landing page. If you searched 'Jira alternative,' you saw comparison copy. If you searched 'sprint planning tool,' you saw problem-solution copy. Relevance drives Quality Score, which drives down CPL."

3. **Negative keyword architecture** — "We cut 73% of spend on irrelevant queries. The remaining 27% was highly qualified traffic. That's how you go from 1.2x to 4.1x ROAS — not by spending more, by wasting less."

4. **Modular copy system** — "I didn't just write ads. I built a system with 40+ templates that their team can use to generate new variations in 10 minutes. The system compounds over time."

### The Results Story

"In 90 days:
- ROAS went from 1.2x to 4.1x (+242%)
- At $180K/month spend, that's $522K more in MRR every month
- Annualized: $6.2M+ in incremental ARR

Elena (the founder) told me: 'We went from burning VC money to funding our own growth.'"

## Key Objection Responses

### "Can't we just hire an in-house person?"

"Possible, but the learning curve is 6-12 months before they're effective. By then, you've spent $100K+ on salary and made $200K+ in ad mistakes. My system approach gets you to 4x ROAS in 90 days with a team that can maintain it afterward. The templates mean your team doesn't need to be expert copywriters — they just need to know your product and your customers."

### "Our product is more expensive than some competitors."

"Price is only a differentiator if you're competing on price. We're not — we're competing on specificity. 'Stop missing sprint deadlines' positions you as the solution to a problem people feel daily. That specificity justifies premium pricing because it maps to value, not features."

### "Why didn't our previous agency work?"

"Two likely causes: First, they were writing ads, not building systems. Ads decay over time as audiences fatigue. If they weren't generating new variations, performance dropped. Second, they probably didn't match intent — generic ads to generic landing pages. That's a Quality Score killer."

### "We don't have bandwidth to manage this."

"That's what the retainer covers. I run the optimization weekly, identify test opportunities, and manage the testing process. Your team just approves winners. The system is designed to minimize your time investment while maximizing results."
