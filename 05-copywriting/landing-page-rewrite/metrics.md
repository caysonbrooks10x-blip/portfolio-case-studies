## Revenue Impact Calculation

**At $3,200 average program price:**  
Incremental monthly revenue: $800,000  
**90-day pipeline impact:** ~$2.4M

## Traffic Quality (Post-Rewrite)

No significant change in traffic quality indicators:
- Bounce rate remained stable (38-41%)
- Traffic source distribution unchanged
- Device breakdown consistent

**Conclusion:** CVR improvement was copy-driven, not traffic-driven.
