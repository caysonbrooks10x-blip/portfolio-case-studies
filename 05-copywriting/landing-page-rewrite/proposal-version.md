## Problem Statement

Meridian's current landing page is converting at 1.8% — roughly half the industry benchmark for B2B coaching programs at this price point ($12K/year). At a $340 cost-per-lead, the math barely works. You're paying for attention but not closing the gap between interest and action.

The root issue isn't traffic quality. It's page copy that reads like a brochure rather than a conversion tool.

---

## Proposed Solution

A full rewrite of the landing page using evidence-based conversion principles, focusing on:

1. **Pain-first architecture** — Restructure every section to lead with the prospect's pressure, not Meridian's credentials
2. **Quantified social proof** — Replace vague testimonials with outcome-specific results from real clients
3. **Mobile-first conversion flow** — Optimize CTA placement and hierarchy for mobile scroll behavior
4. **Outcome framing** — Shift from "what the program includes" to "what changes after the program"
5. **Risk reversal language** — Add positioning that reduces perceived risk of a $12K commitment
