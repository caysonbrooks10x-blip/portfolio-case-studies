# Talking Points: Landing Page Rewrite
## Meridian Consulting Case Study

---

## For Sales Calls & Pitches

### The Core Problem We Solved

"Their page was converting at 1.8%. That's half the industry benchmark for B2B coaching at this price point. The issue wasn't traffic — it was that the page read like a brochure about Meridian instead of a conversion tool for someone actively looking for executive coaching."

### What Made the Difference

1. **Pain-first framing** — "We shifted from 'here's what we do' to 'here's what's keeping you up at night.' The page now speaks to the board-level anxiety executives feel when they're new to the C-suite."

2. **Quantified outcomes** — "Generic testimonials don't convert. We replaced 'great coach, highly recommend' with specific numbers: $2.1M average career advancement, 34% reduction in turnover, 3.2x more sponsor meetings."

3. **Mobile-first CTA** — "The original CTA was buried below four scrolling sections on mobile. We moved it above the fold and reframed it from 'Schedule a Discovery Call' to 'Book Your Strategy Session' — positioning it as valuable for them, not just us."

### The Results Story

"Within 60 days of launch:
- Conversion rate went from 1.8% to 5.2% (189% increase)
- Cost per lead dropped from $340 to $118 (65% reduction)
- At their $45K monthly ad spend, that generated roughly $340K in incremental pipeline over 90 days

The CEO told me the new page 'finally sounds like the premium option we always believed we were.'"

## Key Objection Responses

### "Is this replicable for any landing page?"

"Conversion principles are universal, but application is custom. Every page has different traffic sources, different prospect psychology, different offer mechanics. What I do is diagnose the specific conversion blockers on your page — then fix them with copy that's engineered for your audience."

### "Can you guarantee results?"

"I don't promise specific CVR numbers because I can't control your traffic quality, your offer, or your market. What I can tell you is: I've taken pages from 1.1% to 6.8%. I've written copy that's generated 7-figures in pipeline from cold traffic. If the rewritten page doesn't hit at least 3x your current CVR within 60 days, I refund 25%. That's skin in the game."

### "Why did the old page underperform?"

"Two reasons: First, it led with credentials instead of pain. 'Founded in 2008, coached 400+ executives' — that's about us. Prospects don't care until they care about their problem. Second, no quantified outcomes. 'Improve your leadership' is vague. 'Reduce direct report turnover by 34% in 6 months' is specific and believable."
