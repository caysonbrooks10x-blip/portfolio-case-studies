# Vantage Analytics — Website Version

## How Vantage Analytics Captured 6 Years of Proprietary Knowledge

**Industry:** Supply Chain Consulting  
**Timeline:** 14 weeks  
**Stack:** Electron, React, Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL  

---

### The Problem

Vantage Analytics had built a $45M consulting business on proprietary methodologies. The problem: everything lived in Excel templates and senior analysts' heads. Susan from Dallas — 6 years of pattern recognition — was 3 months from retirement.

Proposals took 3 weeks. Quality varied by team. Analysts spent 60% of their time wrangling data instead of analyzing.

---

### Our Approach

**Week 1-3: Knowledge Archaeology**

We didn't ask analysts to document their processes. We shadowed them, then asked "why?" The answers revealed 6 years of tacit knowledge no one had ever written down.

- 12 senior analyst interviews
- 47 Excel templates reverse-engineered
- 200+ proprietary calculations documented
- 20+ decision trees formalized

**The Build:**

- On-premise Electron app (enterprise security)
- Custom SAP/Oracle/Salesforce connectors
- Methodology Engine with automated quality gates
- Smart Proposal Generator (3-day turnaround)
- Real-time collaboration for analyst teams
