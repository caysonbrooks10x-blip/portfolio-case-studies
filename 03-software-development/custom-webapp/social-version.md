# Vantage Analytics — Social Version

---

**Built an enterprise web app that captures 6 years of proprietary knowledge.**

Vantage Analytics had a $45M problem. Their supply chain methodology — built over 6 years — lived in 47 Excel files and senior analysts' heads. Susan from Dallas was retiring in 3 months.

**What we built:**
→ Electron desktop app (on-premise, enterprise security)
→ Custom SAP/Oracle/Salesforce connectors
→ Methodology Engine (200+ proprietary calculations)
→ Smart Proposal Generator (3-day turnaround)
→ Real-time collaboration for analyst teams

**Results:**
• Proposal time: 21 days → 2.8 days (87% faster)
• Analyst utilization: 40% → 75%
• Year 1 ROI: 655%
• $2.1M additional revenue
• 85% of institutional knowledge systematized

The insight that mattered: analysts couldn't tell you their methodology. We had to watch them work, then ask "why?" The answers were priceless.
