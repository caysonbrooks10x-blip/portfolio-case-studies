# Vantage Analytics — Case Study Summary

## The Brief
Fortune 500 supply chain consultancy needed to systematize 6 years of proprietary knowledge trapped in 47 Excel templates and senior analysts' heads.

## Approach
- 3-week embedded knowledge capture with 12 senior analysts
- On-premise Electron + React application
- Custom SAP/Oracle/Salesforce connectors
- Methodology engine capturing proprietary IP
- Phased rollout to 50-person analyst team

## Results
- **Proposal time:** 21 days → 2.8 days (87% faster)
- **Analyst utilization:** 40% → 75%
- **Proposals/month:** 12 → 38 (3.2x)
- **Year 1 ROI:** 655%
- **Knowledge captured:** 85% of institutional expertise
- **Additional revenue:** $2.1M in Year 1

## Tech Stack
Electron, React 18, TypeScript, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Python (Flask), D3.js, Redis, Material UI

## Key Differentiator
Tacit knowledge extraction protocol surfaced 6 years of undocumented pattern recognition that became the foundation for the Methodology Engine — their proprietary IP.
