# FieldSync SaaS MVP — Case Study Summary

## The Brief
FieldSync needed a field service management MVP for HVAC/plumbing contractors — fast, affordable, and actually usable by field techs on iPads.

## The Approach
- 3-day embedded discovery with 4 contractor design partners
- 9-week fixed-scope development sprint
- Next.js PWA for iPad compatibility (avoided App Store delays)
- Real-time dispatch board with Supabase WebSockets
- Full Stripe Connect + QuickBooks integration

## Results
- **Launched:** On time, $178K (under $180K budget)
- **Users:** 0 → 500 in 14 weeks
- **MRR at Month 3:** $34,500
- **Churn:** 4.2% vs 22% industry average
- **Performance:** 94 Lighthouse, 99.94% uptime
- **Series A:** Closed $8M, 4 months post-launch

## Technical Stack
Next.js 14, Node.js, PostgreSQL (Railway), Redis, Supabase Realtime, Stripe Connect, QuickBooks API, Twilio, Vercel

## Key Differentiator
Built alongside field techs — rode along on service calls to understand real pain points. Voice-to-job-notes became a flagship feature no competitor offered.
