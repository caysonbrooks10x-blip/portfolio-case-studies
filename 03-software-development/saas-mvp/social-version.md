# FieldSync — Social Version

---

**Built a field service SaaS MVP in 9 weeks.**

FieldSync came to us with a vision: challenge ServiceTitan with a mobile-first platform contractors would actually use.

Their seed funding was running out. They needed to launch before they ran out of money.

**What we built:**
→ Next.js PWA (iPad-optimized, offline-capable)
→ Real-time dispatch board (Supabase WebSockets)
→ Full Stripe Connect + QuickBooks integration
→ Voice-to-job-notes (flagged feature after shadowing field techs)

**Results:**
• $178K development cost (agencies quoted $350K+)
• 9-week timeline
• 0→500 users in 14 weeks
• $34.5K MRR at Month 3
• 4.2% churn vs 22% industry average
• Closed $8M Series A 4 months later

The insight that mattered most: we rode along with a field tech in a 100°F attic. Watched him struggle to type on an iPad.

Voice notes became a flagship feature.

Sometimes the best product decisions come from leaving the conference room.
