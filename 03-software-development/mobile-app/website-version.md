# MealMoxie — Website Version

## How MealMoxie Built a Mobile App MVP in 12 Weeks

**Industry:** Food Tech / Corporate Benefits  
**Timeline:** 12 weeks  
**Stack:** React Native, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Stripe Connect  

---

### The Problem

Corporate meal benefits are broken. Catering orders need 48-hour notice. Stipends get spent randomly. Employees don't know their balances. HR has no visibility.

MealMoxie had a thesis: real-time meal ordering with daily credits and corporate controls. They had $2.1M in seed funding and 12 weeks to prove it.

---

### Our Approach

**Week 1-2: Validation Sprint**

We didn't start coding. We talked to 24 corporate employees and 8 HR directors. We analyzed competitors. We identified 3 differentiators: real-time ordering, universal restaurant support, and offline reliability.

**The Build:**

- React Native (Expo) — 70% code sharing iOS + Android
- Offline-first architecture — orders queue when connectivity drops
- Stripe Connect for corporate billing
- Google Places for restaurant discovery
- Algolia for search
- Corporate dashboard with budget analytics
