# MealMoxie — Social Version

---

**Built a mobile app MVP for corporate meal benefits — 12 weeks, iOS + Android.**

MealMoxie had $2.1M seed funding and a thesis: corporate meal benefits should work like Venmo, not like expense reports.

They had 12 weeks to prove it.

**What we built:**
→ React Native (70% code sharing iOS + Android)
→ Offline-first architecture (orders queue when WiFi drops)
→ Real-time restaurant discovery (Google Places + Algolia)
→ Stripe Connect corporate billing
→ Corporate dashboard with budget analytics
→ 3 corporate beta partners (150 users) before public launch

**Results:**
• $193K development cost
• 12-week timeline (on budget)
• 4.7 App Store rating
• 0 → 8,900 users in 6 months
• $890K GMV at Month 6
• 34 corporate partners at Month 6
• 97% corporate retention

The insight that mattered: corporate WiFi is terrible. Offline-first ordering was the differentiator DoorDash for Business couldn't match.
