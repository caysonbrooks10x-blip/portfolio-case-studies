# Nordic Table — Proposal Version

## Executive Summary

Nordic Table sells Scandinavian furniture with a strong brand and growing revenue ($28M ARR, 42% YoY). But customers couldn't find what they wanted. The website was a product grid — competitors offered room-by-room browsing, visual search, and AR visualization.

The gap: three custom features that would transform product discovery and conversion.

We propose a 10-week, fixed-price engagement to build:
1. **Room-by-Room Galleries** — Curated room collections with "Shop This Room"
2. **Visual Search** — Upload any image, find similar products
3. **WebAR Product Viewer** — See furniture in your space (no app needed)

---

## Scope of Work

### Phase 1: Discovery & Strategy (Weeks 1-2)

**User Research:**
- 2 years of Google Analytics analysis
- 16 customer interviews
- 340 abandoned cart survey responses
- Competitor feature review

**Deliverable:** Product Discovery Enhancement Strategy

### Phase 2: Design (Weeks 2-4)

**UX Design:**
- 60+ Figma frames
- 3 rounds of prototype testing
- Motion design for AR interactions
- Design system extension

**Key Decisions:**
- Room galleries → product grid (not replacement)
- Visual search as complement (not replacement)
- WebAR over native (no app download)

### Phase 3: Development (Weeks 4-9)

**Room-by-Room Galleries:**
- 24 curated room collections
- "Shop This Room" aggregation
- "Complete the Look" suggestions
- Lazy-loaded images with blur placeholders

**Visual Search:**
- TensorFlow.js product embedding
- Image upload/camera capture
- Style/color/shape matching
- Top 12 similar products display

**WebAR Viewer:**
- USDZ/GLTF model generation
- @google/model-viewer integration
- "Place in Room" one-tap activation
- AR screenshot sharing

**Backend:**
- Shopify Storefront API integration
- Image processing pipeline
- A/B testing framework
- Analytics event tracking

### Phase 4: QA & Launch (Weeks 9-10)

- Performance testing (3G throttling)
- 32-device testing matrix
- A/B test setup (50/50)
- Analytics validation
- Shopify theme integration

## Why This Works

**Performance-First:** We obsess over load times. Room galleries load in 1.8s on 3G. Competitor AR takes 8+ seconds.

**Complementary:** New features don't replace the product grid — they augment it. Customers who use visual search still browse normally.

**WebAR:** No app download required. 89% of users who see AR actually use it — because it works instantly.
