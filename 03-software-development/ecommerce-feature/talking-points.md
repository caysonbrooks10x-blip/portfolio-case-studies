# Nordic Table — Talking Points

## For Sales Conversations

### Opening Hook
"Nordic Table had a $28M business with a product discovery problem. Their website was a grid — competitors had room galleries, visual search, and AR. We built all three in 10 weeks. Result: 39% conversion increase, $1.8M in attributed revenue over 3 months."

### Differentiator #1: Performance-First
"Most AR e-commerce takes 8+ seconds to load. Ours loads in 1.8s on 3G. The difference: we obsessed over every millisecond. Lazy loading, image optimization, critical CSS inlining. 89% of users who saw the AR button actually used it — because it worked instantly."

### Differentiator #2: Complementary Features
"Visual search and AR didn't replace the product grid — they supplemented it. Customers who used visual search still browsed normally. Room gallery visitors still used search. The features worked together, not against each other."

### Differentiator #3: Shopify Native
"We built on Shopify's Storefront API, not around it. The checkout still worked perfectly. The inventory still synced. The theme still functioned. Custom features, zero checkout disruption."

### ROI Story
"They paid $143.5K. Revenue attributed to new features in 3 months: $1.8M. That's a 12x return in a single quarter. And the features keep generating revenue."

## For Portfolio Presentations

### The Problem Slide
"$28M ARR, 42% YoY growth, beautiful products. But a product grid where competitors had room galleries, visual search, and AR. Customers couldn't find what they wanted. Conversion suffered."

### The Insight Slide
"67% of customers shop room-by-room mentally. 43% of cart abandonment was 'couldn't find what I wanted.' 58% requested visual search. The data was clear — the website didn't match how customers thought about furniture."

### The Build Slide
"10 weeks, 3 features. Room galleries (24 curated collections). Visual search (TensorFlow.js similarity matching). WebAR (model-viewer, 1.8s load). All integrated with Shopify Storefront API."

### The Results Slide
"78% longer sessions. 44% higher add-to-cart. 39% conversion increase. $1.8M revenue in 3 months. 89% of AR viewers actually used it."

## Objection Handlers

### "Shopify already has apps for this. Why custom build?"
"Shopify apps for these features exist — and they add 4-8 seconds of load time, don't match your brand, and can't be customized. We built exactly what Nordic Table needed, integrated natively, with performance that apps can't match."

### "Our customers aren't technical. They won't use AR."
"89% of customers who saw the AR button used it. On iOS Safari and Android Chrome — no app download, no account. It just works. Your customers are more ready for AR than you think."

### "Can this scale to our full catalog?"
"Yes. We built the pipeline to handle 400+ SKUs. Adding new products takes 5 minutes (upload photos, the system generates the AR model and embeddings automatically)."
