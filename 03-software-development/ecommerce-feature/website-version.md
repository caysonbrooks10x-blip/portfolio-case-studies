# Nordic Table — Website Version

## How Nordic Table Increased Conversion by 39% with Custom E-commerce Features

**Industry:** Direct-to-Consumer Furniture  
**Timeline:** 10 weeks  
**Stack:** React, Shopify Storefront API, TensorFlow.js, WebAR  

---

### The Problem

Nordic Table sold beautiful Scandinavian furniture. Strong brand, growing revenue ($28M ARR). But their website was a product grid — customers couldn't visualize rooms, find similar styles, or see products in their space.

Competitors had room-by-room browsing, visual search, and AR. Nordic Table didn't.

---

### Our Approach

**Week 1-2: Discovery Sprint**

We analyzed 2 years of Google Analytics, interviewed 16 customers, and surveyed 340 abandoned cart shoppers. Key insight: 67% of customers shopped room-by-room mentally.

**The Build:**

- **Room-by-Room Galleries:** 24 curated collections with "Shop This Room"
- **Visual Search:** Upload any image, find similar products (TensorFlow.js)
- **WebAR Viewer:** See furniture in your space, no app download
- **Performance-first:** 1.8s load on 3G (vs 8+ seconds for competitors)

---

### Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Session duration | 2.3 min | 4.1 min |
| Pages per session | 3.2 | 6.8 |
| Add-to-cart rate | 8.4% | 12.1% |
| Checkout conversion | 61% | 67% |
| Overall conversion | 2.8% | 3.9% |

**Revenue (3 months):** $1.8M attributed to new features  
**AOV increase:** $42
