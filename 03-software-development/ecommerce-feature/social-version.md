# Nordic Table — Social Version

---

**Built 3 e-commerce features. Increased conversion by 39%. $1.8M revenue in 3 months.**

Nordic Table sold Scandinavian furniture. Strong brand. $28M ARR. But their website was a product grid — no room browsing, no visual search, no AR.

Customers couldn't find what they wanted. They left.

**What we built:**
→ Room-by-room galleries (24 curated collections)
→ Visual search (upload image, find similar)
→ WebAR product viewer (see furniture in your space)
→ All integrated with Shopify in 10 weeks

**Results:**
• Session duration: +78%
• Pages per session: +113%
• Add-to-cart rate: +44%
• Overall conversion: +39%
• Revenue attributed: $1.8M (3 months)
• AOV increase: $42
• AR usage rate: 89% (of those who saw it)

The insight that mattered: 67% of customers shop room-by-room mentally. The website was a grid. The brand was a room. We built the bridge.
