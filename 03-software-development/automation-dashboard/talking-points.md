# Prism Health — Talking Points

## For Sales Conversations

### Opening Hook
"Prism Health had 2.3 million patients and 340 care coordinators drowning in manual work. Fourteen EHR systems, 4+ hours of daily data entry, 23% of care plans incomplete. After 16 weeks, they have 81% less manual work, 98% care plan completeness, and $11.3M in Year 1 value created."

### Differentiator #1: Clinical Validation First
"We shadowed care coordinators for 3 weeks before writing code. We documented 47 workflow steps. We identified 12 automation opportunities. Every feature was validated by the people who'd use it. This isn't software built in a conference room — it's a tool built by people who understand clinical reality."

### Differentiator #2: Automation That Augments
"We didn't try to replace clinical judgment. We automated the pattern-matching work — identifying patients who need outreach, generating care plan drafts, sending appointment reminders. The clinical decisions stayed with the nurses. This was the key to adoption: 94% of care coordinators actively using the platform within 30 days."

### Differentiator #3: HIPAA-First Architecture
"Security wasn't a checkbox — it was the foundation. End-to-end encryption, comprehensive audit logging, role-based access, MFA. We passed HIPAA audit on the first submission. That's rare."

### ROI Story
"They paid $380K. Year 1 value: $11.3M. That's a 29.7x return. The breakdown: $4.2M in productivity gains, $3.8M in quality bonuses earned, $890K in penalties avoided, $2.4M in avoided headcount. The platform paid for itself in 12 days."

## For Portfolio Presentations

### The Problem Slide
"14 EHR systems. 4+ hours of daily manual work per care coordinator. 23% incomplete care plans. 12 CMS audit findings. Prism was managing 2.3M patients with healthcare software from the 1990s."

### The Insight Slide
"Three weeks of shadowing revealed: 68% of coordinator time spent gathering data, not caring for patients. Most 'clinical decisions' were pattern matching — diabetes + hypertension + no recent A1C = outreach needed. That's automation opportunity, not clinical complexity."

### The Build Slide
"Patient 360 dashboard unifying 14 EHRs. Care plan generator with evidence-based guidelines. Workflow automation engine with visual builder. Multi-channel outreach via Twilio and SendGrid. Real-time quality dashboards. All HIPAA-compliant, SOC 2 Type II certified."

### The Results Slide
"81% reduction in manual work. 82% faster care plans. 98% completeness (was 77%). 7x more outreach. 52% reduction in no-shows. 83% fewer compliance findings. $11.3M Year 1 value. 29.7x ROI."

## Objection Handlers

### "Our EHR vendors won't share data."
"We've integrated with Epic, Cerner, Allscripts, athenahealth, eClinicalWorks, and 9 others. We use FHIR R4 where available, HL7 for legacy systems, and direct database connections (read-only) where APIs don't exist. If your EHR has data, we can access it."

### "Clinicians won't adopt another tool."
"We heard this concern. Here's why adoption was 94% within 30 days: we automated work, not decisions. Nurses still make clinical judgments. We just eliminated the data gathering that consumed 68% of their time. The tool makes them more effective, not obsolete."

### "HIPAA compliance is a nightmare."
"We build HIPAA-first, not HIPAA-at-the-end. Every layer has security: encryption at rest and in transit, comprehensive audit logging, role-based access, MFA. We passed HIPAA audit on first submission. We provide the BAA. You focus on care, we focus on compliance."
