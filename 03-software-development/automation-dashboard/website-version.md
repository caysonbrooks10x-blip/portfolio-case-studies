# Prism Health — Website Version

## How Prism Health Automated 3+ Hours of Daily Manual Work Per Care Coordinator

**Industry:** Healthcare Technology  
**Timeline:** 16 weeks  
**Stack:** React 18, Node.js, FHIR R4, PostgreSQL  

---

### The Problem

Prism Health managed 2.3 million patients across 8 states. Their care coordinators spent 4+ hours daily on manual tasks: copying data between 14 EHR systems, building care plans in Word, calling patients for appointments.

23% of care plans were missing required elements. CMS compliance auditors found 12 issues in 2 years.

The care coordinators became data entry clerks, not nurses.

---

### Our Approach

**Week 1-3: Clinical Discovery**

We shadowed 12 care coordinators. We documented 47 workflow steps. We identified 12 automation opportunities.

The insight: coordinators spent 68% of time gathering data, not caring for patients. Most "clinical decisions" were pattern matching — diabetes + hypertension + no recent A1C = outreach needed.

**The Build:**

- Patient 360 dashboard (unified view across 14 EHRs)
- Automated care plan generator (evidence-based guidelines)
- Visual workflow automation engine
- Multi-channel outreach (SMS, email, voice)
- Real-time quality dashboards
- HIPAA-compliant architecture

---

### Results

| Metric | Before | After |

| Manual work/day | 4+ hours | 45 min |
