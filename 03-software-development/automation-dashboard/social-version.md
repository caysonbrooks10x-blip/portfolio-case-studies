# Prism Health — Social Version

---

**Built a healthcare workflow automation dashboard. Saved care coordinators 3+ hours daily.**

Prism Health had a care coordination crisis. 14 EHR systems. 4+ hours of daily manual work per coordinator. 23% of care plans missing elements. 12 CMS audit findings in 2 years.

Nurses weren't nursing. They were data entry clerks.

**What we built:**
→ Patient 360 dashboard (unified view across 14 EHRs)
→ Automated care plan generator (evidence-based guidelines)
→ Visual workflow automation engine
→ Multi-channel outreach (SMS, email, voice)
→ Real-time quality dashboards
→ HIPAA-compliant architecture

**Results:**
• Manual work: 4+ hours → 45 min/day (81% reduction)
• Care plan time: 45 min → 8 min (82% faster)
• Care plan completeness: 77% → 98%
• Outreach volume: 7x increase
• No-show rate: 23% → 11% (52% reduction)
• Compliance findings: 83% reduction
• Year 1 ROI: 29.7x
• Year 1 Value: $11.3M

The insight that mattered: automate pattern-matching (who needs outreach), not clinical judgment. Clinicians adopted it because it made them more effective, not obsolete.
