# Case Study Summary: Content Operations Pipeline

**Client:** Sarah Chen, Founder & CEO of Maven Digital (performance marketing agency, e-commerce focus)

**Problem:** Content production ceiling at 4 pieces/month (Sarah's capacity), 60% of Sarah's time on content oversight, 5-7 day approval delays, strategic misalignment, agency capped by founder hours, 6-month waitlist.

**Solution:** Complete content operations pipeline — Notion hub, content brief templates, client playbooks, 12-type content framework, Asana workflow, tiered approval system, analytics integration.

**Results:**
- Content output: 4 → 28 pieces/month (700% increase)
- Production lead time: 18 days → 5 days (72% faster)
- Sarah's oversight: 60% → 15% of content
- Quality consistency: 2.1/10 → 8.4/10
- Client retention: 72% → 94%
- Agency revenue: $1.2M → $2.8M (133% increase)
- Clients served: 8 → 24 (no waitlist)
- Content-attributed revenue: $280K → $1.4M (400% increase)
- Sarah's billable strategic work: 20% → 65%

**Duration:** 8 weeks | **Investment:** $20,000
