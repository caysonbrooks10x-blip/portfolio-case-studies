# Case Study: Maven Digital — Content Operations Pipeline

## The Challenge

Maven Digital had a reputation for excellent content and a production problem. Sarah Chen was bottlenecking every piece through her personal approval — 60% of her time on content oversight. Output was capped at 4 pieces a month. The agency had a 6-month waitlist for new business. Demand wasn't the constraint. Production capacity was.

## The Solution

We built Maven a complete content operations pipeline. Notion content calendar hub with all clients in one view, strategic content brief templates, client content playbooks, 12-type content framework covering full-funnel needs, Asana workflow management, tiered approval system, and analytics connecting content to revenue.

## Results

- **Content output: 4 → 28 pieces/month** (700% increase)
- **Production lead time: 18 days → 5 days** (72% faster)
- **Sarah's oversight: 60% → 15%** of content
- **Quality consistency: 2.1/10 → 8.4/10**
- **Client retention: 72% → 94%**
- **Agency revenue: $1.2M → $2.8M** (133% increase)
- **Clients served: 8 → 24** (waitlist eliminated)
- **Content-attributed revenue: $280K → $1.4M** (400% increase)
- **Sarah's billable strategic work: 20% → 65%**

## Tools

Notion, Asana, Google Workspace, Loom, Zapier, Semrush, Dash Hudson, HubSpot

## Duration

8 weeks | $20,000 investment
