# Proposal: Content Operations Pipeline for Maven Digital

## Submitted to: Sarah Chen, Founder & CEO

---

## Executive Summary

You're running an agency with a content production problem disguised as a staffing problem. The constraint isn't how many writers you can hire — it's that every piece of content goes through you. Your ceiling is 4 pieces a month because you're bottlenecking the approval process. Meanwhile, you have a 6-month waitlist and can't serve more clients.

We will build you a content operations pipeline — strategy, production, workflow, and analytics — that lets your team produce quality content without requiring your personal oversight on every piece. The goal isn't more content. It's scalable content quality.

---

## Scope of Work

### Phase 1: Content Audit & Strategy (2 weeks)
- 6-month content output analysis across all clients
- Quality, consistency, and business alignment audit
- Client content journey and conversion path mapping
- High-ROI content type identification
- Content pillar definition per client

### Phase 2: Content Infrastructure (2 weeks)
- Notion content calendar hub (all clients unified view)
- Content brief templates (strategic framework, not just prompts)
- Client content playbooks (voice, brand, audience)
- Asset library structure and organization
- Tiered approval workflow design

### Phase 3: Production Pipeline (2 weeks)
- Structured content creation process
- Writer onboarding system (Maven methodology)
- 12 content type templates
- Asana content project tracking setup
- Quality assurance checklists

### Phase 4: Analytics & Optimization (2 weeks)
- Content performance to business metrics connection
- Monthly reporting dashboards
- Content ROI framework implementation
- Optimization protocols
- Full operations playbook documentation
