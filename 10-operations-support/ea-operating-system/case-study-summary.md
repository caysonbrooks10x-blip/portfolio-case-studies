# Case Study Summary: Executive Assistant Operating System

**Client:** Marcus Chen, CEO of Velozity (B2B SaaS, $2M→$10M ARR)

**Problem:** CEO drowning in operational chaos — 200+ emails/day, 45 meetings/week, zero visibility, 3 failed VAs in 18 months.

**Solution:** Built complete EA Operating System with Notion hub, ClickUp task management, Zapier automation, and refined delegation protocols.

**Results:**
- **14.5 hours/week CEO time recovered**
- Email processing: 45 min → 12 min daily
- Meetings reduced 60% (45 → 18/week)
- Process documentation: 12% → 89%
- EA retention problem solved (14+ month retention)
- Series A completed 4 months ahead of schedule

**Duration:** 12 weeks | **Investment:** $18,500
