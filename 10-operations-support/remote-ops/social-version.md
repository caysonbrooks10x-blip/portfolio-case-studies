# Remote Operations Management — Case Study

🌍 23 people. 6 countries. 18 tools. Zero integration.

Stellarwave Marketing was scaling fast — and falling apart operationally. Project hand-offs failing 34% of the time. Decisions taking 3-5 days. Burn rate 40% over budget. Just signed their biggest client ($2.4M) and about to implode.

After 10 weeks:
✅ Tools: 18 → 4 (78% fewer, 56% cheaper)
✅ Project handoff success: 66% → 96%
✅ Decision time: 3.5 days → 4 hours
✅ Meetings: 22 → 8/week (64% cut)
✅ Burn rate: 40% over → 3% over
✅ Gross margin: 34% → 52%
✅ $2.4M client retained and expanded

"We almost lost our biggest client. Cayson rebuilt our entire operating model."

Distributed teams don't have to mean distributed chaos. The right infrastructure makes all the difference.
