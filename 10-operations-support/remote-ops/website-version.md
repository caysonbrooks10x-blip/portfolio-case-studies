# Case Study: Stellarwave Marketing — Remote Operations Management

## The Challenge

Stellarwave Marketing had exploded from 4 people to 23 across 6 countries in 18 months. The growth was exciting. The operations were a disaster. 18 different tools with zero integration, project hand-offs failing a third of the time, decisions taking 3-5 days due to timezone gaps, and a burn rate 40% above plan. They'd just signed a $2.4M enterprise contract — their biggest client — and everyone knew the operations needed to step up.

## The Solution

We didn't add more tools. We rebuilt the entire operating model. Asana as project hub (replacing 5 tools), Slack restructured for async-first communication, Notion knowledge base with 247 pages, Loom-based async protocols, and real-time utilization tracking. 18 tools became 4 integrated systems.

## Results

- **Tools: 18 → 4** (78% reduction)
- **Tool spend: $4,200 → $1,840/month** (56% reduction)
- **Project handoff success: 66% → 96%**
- **Decision cycle: 3.5 days → 4 hours**
- **Meetings: 22 → 8/week** (64% reduction)
- **Burn rate variance: 40% over → 3% over**
- **New hire ramp: 6 weeks → 2 weeks**
- **Gross margin: 34% → 52%** (18pp improvement)
- **$2.4M enterprise client retained and expanded**

## Tools

Asana, Slack, Notion, Loom, Zapier, Grain, Clockwise, Google Workspace

## Duration

10 weeks | $32,000 investment
