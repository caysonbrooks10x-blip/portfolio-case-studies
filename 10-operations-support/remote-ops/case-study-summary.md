# Case Study Summary: Remote Operations Management Setup

**Client:** Jordan Willis, COO of Stellarwave Marketing (23 people, 6 countries)

**Problem:** Explosive growth (4→23 people, 6 countries) created operational collapse — 18 tools with no integration, 34% project hand-off failures, 3.5-day decision cycles, 40% burn rate over budget, about to handle $2.4M enterprise contract.

**Solution:** Complete remote ops infrastructure rebuild — Asana hub (replacing 5 tools), Slack restructuring, Notion knowledge base (247 pages), async video protocols, utilization tracking, timezone-aware workflows.

**Results:**
- Tools: 18 → 4 (78% reduction)
- Tool spend: $4,200 → $1,840/month (56% reduction)
- Project handoff success: 66% → 96%
- Decision cycle: 3.5 days → 4 hours
- Meetings: 22 → 8/week (64% reduction)
- Burn rate variance: 40% over → 3% over
- New hire ramp: 6 weeks → 2 weeks
- Gross margin: 34% → 52%
- Revenue per employee: $124K → $187K

**Duration:** 10 weeks | **Investment:** $32,000
