# Case Study: Cadence Home Development — Internal SOP + Documentation System

## The Challenge

Cadence Home Development had built a 14-year reputation on tribal knowledge — processes living in people's heads, not systems. 12% documentation coverage. 60% of senior staff with no documented backups. New hires taking 14 weeks to become productive. Everything escalating to the CEO. Zero compliance documentation. And in 90 days, PE investors would ask about operational maturity. There was nothing to show.

## The Solution

We built Cadence a complete internal documentation system from the ground up. Notion workspace with 8 department hubs, 87 critical SOPs documented, 12 video walkthroughs for complex processes, 45 role onboarding guides, compliance package for audit readiness, and a documentation governance system that ensures it stays current.

## Results

- **Documentation coverage: 12% → 94%**
- **87 critical processes documented**
- **New hire ramp: 14 weeks → 5 weeks** (64% faster)
- **Decision escalations: 47 → 8/week** (83% reduction)
- **Process consistency: 34% → 91%**
- **Cross-training coverage: 18% → 87%**
- **$12M PE deal closed** (operational readiness was key factor)
- **Estimated $340K/year** in reduced errors and rework

## Tools

Notion, Loom, Google Workspace, Zapier, ClickUp, Guru

## Duration

12 weeks | $28,000 investment
