# Case Study Summary: Internal SOP + Documentation System

**Client:** Rebecca Thornton, President of Cadence Home Development (45 employees, $85M projects)

**Problem:** Company built on tribal knowledge — 12% documentation coverage, 60% of senior staff with no backups, 14-week new hire ramp, everything escalating to CEO, zero compliance documentation, PE investors requiring operational maturity proof.

**Solution:** Complete documentation system build — Notion workspace (8 department hubs), 87 SOPs documented, 12 video walkthroughs, 45 role onboarding guides, compliance package, decision trees, documentation governance.

**Results:**
- Documentation coverage: 12% → 94%
- SOPs documented: 89 critical processes
- New hire ramp: 14 weeks → 5 weeks (64% faster)
- Decision escalations: 47 → 8/week (83% reduction)
- Process consistency: 34% → 91%
- Cross-training coverage: 18% → 87%
- PE deal closed: $12M (operational readiness was key)
- Estimated savings: $340K/year in reduced rework/errors

**Duration:** 12 weeks | **Investment:** $28,000
