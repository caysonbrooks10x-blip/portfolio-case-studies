## Executive Summary

Your company's knowledge walks out the door every time someone takes vacation, gets sick, or leaves. You've built something remarkable on tribal knowledge — and now you're preparing for a growth phase that requires operational maturity you don't currently have. Your PE conversation is in 90 days. They will ask about your documentation. Right now, you'd have nothing to show.

We will build you a complete internal documentation system — Notion-based, company-wide, built for actual adoption — that documents 87+ critical processes, trains your team on using it, and positions you as operationally mature for investors and growth.

## Scope of Work

### Phase 1: Documentation Audit (2 weeks)
- All 45 employee interviews to map undocumented knowledge
- 30+ workflow observations across 8 departments
- 89 distinct undocumented processes identified
- Risk/frequency/complexity prioritization
- Documentation standards and templates development

### Phase 2: Framework Build (2 weeks)
- Notion workspace architecture (8 department hubs)
- SOP template library (15 standardized templates)
- Process naming conventions and taxonomy
- Documentation governance rules
- Review and update cadence schedule

### Phase 3: Documentation Sprint (6 weeks)
- 87 critical SOPs documented
- 12 video walkthroughs (Loom) for complex processes
- 45 role onboarding guides (one per employee)
- Decision trees for common escalations
- Compliance documentation package
- Project management process library

### Phase 4: Implementation & Adoption (2 weeks)
- Team training on documentation system
- Department documentation champions appointed
- Weekly documentation review ritual established
- Performance review integration
- Annual documentation audit calendar
