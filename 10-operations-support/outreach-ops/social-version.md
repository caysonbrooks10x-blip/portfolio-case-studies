# Lead Generation + Outreach Operations — Case Study

📧 40 cold emails a week. 0.8% reply rate. 4 meetings a year from outbound.

Derek Okafor was spending 70% of his BD time on outreach that wasn't working. His consulting firm was entirely dependent on referrals — feast or famine, no predictability.

After 8 weeks:
✅ Outbound volume: 40 → 400/week (10x)
✅ Reply rate: 0.8% → 12.4% (1,450% improvement)
✅ Meetings: 4/year → 31/quarter
✅ Pipeline: $50K → $480K/quarter
✅ Revenue from outbound: $50K → $910K/year
✅ CAC: $4,200 → $890 (79% drop)

"We went from feast or famine to having actual predictability. The $16K investment has returned over $900K in new client revenue."

The fix: Complete outreach rebuild — proper data, 12-touch multi-channel sequences, automated follow-up, persona-specific messaging.
