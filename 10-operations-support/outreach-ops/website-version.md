# Case Study: Summit Business Advisors — Lead Generation + Outreach Operations

## The Challenge

Summit Business Advisors had a dream client profile and nothing to show for it. Derek's team was sending 40 cold emails a week with generic templates, getting 0.8% reply rates, and producing just 4 qualified meetings per year from outbound. His 70% of business development time was going to outreach that wasn't working. The business was entirely dependent on referrals — feast or famine.

## The Solution

We built Derek a complete lead generation machine. Apollo.io for prospecting data, a 12-touch multi-channel sequence (email, LinkedIn, phone), HubSpot tracking, Lemlist automation, and persona-specific messaging that actually resonated. 40 emails became 400. Generic spray-and-pray became targeted, research-driven outreach.

## Results

- **Outreach volume: 40 → 400/week** (10x)
- **Reply rate: 0.8% → 12.4%** (1,450% improvement)
- **Meetings from outbound: 4/year → 31/quarter**
- **Pipeline from outbound: $50K → $480K/quarter**
- **Outbound revenue: $50K → $910K/year**
- **Client acquisition cost: $4,200 → $890** (79% reduction)
- **BD time freed from low-leverage activities: 70% → 25%**

## Tools

Apollo.io, HubSpot, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Lemlist, Zapier, Google Workspace

## Duration

8 weeks | $16,000 investment
