# Case Study: Lead Generation + Outreach Operations

## Building a Scalable Lead Machine for Summit Business Advisors

---

### The Client

**Derek Okafor**, Managing Partner at Summit Business Advisors — a B2B consulting firm specializing in operational transformation for mid-market manufacturing companies. Derek had a dream client profile — companies with $20M-$200M revenue, 100-500 employees, concrete operational pain points — but his outreach was producing nothing but silence.

After 18 months of trying everything in-house (cold emails, LinkedIn, referrals, conferences), his team had generated exactly 4 qualified meetings from outbound efforts. His close rate on those was 50%, which was good — but with a $25K average retainer, even 2 clients would change his business. The problem was volume. Derek was spending 70% of his business development time on outreach that wasn't working.

His business was relying entirely on referrals, which meant feast or famine — great quarters followed by quiet ones. He needed predictable, scalable pipeline generation.

### The Challenge

The symptoms were clear but the causes weren't obvious:

- **Outreach volume too low**: 40 cold emails/week maximum (all manual)
- **Research quality poor**: Generic templates sent to generic contacts
- **Response rates near zero**: Under 1% reply rate on cold outreach
- **No follow-up discipline**: 80% of prospects received only 1 touch
- **Pipeline invisible**: No systematic tracking of where prospects were
- **BD time wasted**: Derek spending 70% of BD time on low-leverage activities
- **Inconsistent messaging**: Every email sounded like a different company

The real issue: Derek's team was doing outreach the way everyone does it wrong — spray and pray with no system, no data, no follow-up.

### The Approach

We didn't just improve the outreach. We built a complete lead generation machine with proper infrastructure, data, and execution.

**Phase 1: Lead Generation Audit (Weeks 1-2)**
- Analyzed all past outreach (18 months, 2,400+ touches)
- Mapped ideal client profile with 47 firmographic indicators
- Identified decision-maker personas (titles, seniority, concerns)
- Audited competitive positioning and messaging
- Reviewed tech stack and identified gaps

**Phase 2: Data Infrastructure (Weeks 3-4)**
- Implemented Apollo.io for targeted prospecting data
- Built custom ICP filters matching Summit's ideal client
- Created decision-maker persona database (287 verified contacts)
- Set up LinkedIn Sales Navigator for social outreach
- Configured outreach tracking in HubSpot

**Phase 3: Outreach System Build (Weeks 5-6)**
- Built 12-touch outbound sequence (email, LinkedIn, phone)
- Created persona-specific email templates (5 variants)
- Implemented multi-channel warm-up strategy
- Built follow-up automation (6-step nurture for non-responders)
- Created LinkedIn connection sequence with engagement tactics
- Set up reply detection and auto-pause rules

**Phase 4: Execution & Optimization (Weeks 7-8)**
- Ran first campaign cohort (480 targets)
- A/B tested subject lines and messaging
- Analyzed response patterns and refined approach
- Built weekly reporting dashboard
- Documented full playbook for in-house handoff

### The Results

**Outreach Performance:**
- Weekly outreach volume: **40 → 400** (10x increase)
- Research quality: **Generic** → **Personalized (per prospect)**
- Reply rate: **0.8%** → **12.4%** (1,450% improvement)
- Meeting conversion: **4/year** → **31/quarter** (675% increase)
- Pipeline generated: **$50K/quarter** → **$480K/quarter** (860% increase)

**Time & Efficiency:**
- BD time on outreach: **70%** → **25%** (freed for actual sales)
- Time per prospect research: **18 min** → **3 min** (automated)
- Follow-up completion: **20%** → **97%** (automated sequences)
- Outbound revenue attribution: **8%** → **64%**

**Revenue Impact:**
- New clients from outbound: **2/year** → **14/year**
- Revenue from outbound: **$50K/year** → **$910K/year** (1,720% increase)
- Average client acquisition cost: **$4,200** → **$890** (79% reduction)
- Pipeline coverage ratio: **0.3x** → **2.8x** quarterly quota

### Tools Implemented

- **Apollo.io**: Prospecting data, contact verification, email finder
- **HubSpot**: CRM, pipeline tracking, sequence management
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator**: Social outreach, connection management
- **Lemlist**: Email sequencing, A/B testing, deliverability monitoring
- **Zapier**: Automation between tools
- **Google Workspace**: Email, calendar, drive

### Key Sequences Built

1. **12-Touch Outbound Sequence**: 6 emails, 4 LinkedIn touches, 2 phone attempts over 45 days
2. **C-Suite Persona Sequence**: Personalized around operational transformation pain points
3. **Mid-Level Manager Sequence**: Focus on tactical value and quick wins
4. **Re-engagement Campaign**: 90-day dormant leads back into nurture
5. **Referral Accelerator**: Post-meeting touch sequence to unlock referrals
6. **Event Follow-Up**: Conference/webinar attendees into pipeline within 48 hours
