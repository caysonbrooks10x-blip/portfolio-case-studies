## Objection: "We tried cold email before and it didn't work"

**Response:** "Did you try it with proper data, personalized research, multi-channel sequences, AND automated follow-up? Most 'tried' cold email means sent a few hundred generic templates, maybe followed up once, and quit when nobody replied. Derek sent 18 months of exactly that. We built him a machine that touches each prospect 12 times across 3 channels with personalized content. 'Didn't work' usually means 'didn't do it right.'"

## Objection: "Our buyers are too sophisticated for cold outreach"

**Response:** "Exactly right — and that's precisely why generic templates bounce off them. Our approach is research-intensive: every email references something specific about the prospect's company, role, or current challenges. We're not selling; we're starting conversations with people who've already identified a problem we solve. Sophisticated buyers respond to sophistication, not spray-and-pray."
