# Case Study Summary: Lead Generation + Outreach Operations

**Client:** Derek Okafor, Managing Partner at Summit Business Advisors (B2B consulting, $25K avg retainer)

**Problem:** Outbound producing nothing — 40 emails/week, 0.8% reply rate, 4 meetings/year from outreach, 70% of BD time wasted, entirely dependent on referrals.

**Solution:** Complete outreach rebuild — Apollo.io data infrastructure, 12-touch multi-channel sequence (email, LinkedIn, phone), HubSpot tracking, Lemlist automation, persona-specific messaging, full playbook for in-house handoff.

**Results:**
- Outreach volume: 40 → 400/week (10x)
- Reply rate: 0.8% → 12.4% (1,450% improvement)
- Meetings from outbound: 4/year → 31/quarter
- Pipeline from outbound: $50K → $480K/quarter
- Outbound revenue: $50K → $910K/year
- Client acquisition cost: $4,200 → $890 (79% reduction)
- BD time freed: 70% → 25% on low-leverage activities

**Duration:** 8 weeks | **Investment:** $16,000
