# Case Study: Northvista Realty — CRM Cleanup + Automation

## The Challenge

Northvista Realty had a HubSpot problem disguised as a marketing problem. $40K/year in lead generation spending was flowing into a CRM that nobody trusted — 73% of contacts were dead data, agent follow-up was mythical, and the pipeline reports showed deals that had been dead for 6 months. The broker wanted answers. Priya needed a solution.

## The Solution

We didn't "clean" the CRM. We rebuilt it. 8,400 contacts remediated in 2 weeks, 14 automated sequences implemented, lead scoring model redesigned from scratch, and accountability systems that made ignoring hot leads impossible.

## Results

- **8,400 contacts cleaned** in 2 weeks (12,400 total, 3,200 permanently removed)
- **Lead response time: 6.3 hours → 8 minutes** (96% improvement)
- **Agent CRM adoption: 40% → 94%**
- **Lead conversion rate: 2.1% → 8.7%** (314% improvement)
- **Marketing ROI: $0.31 → $1.87 per dollar** (503% improvement)
- **Revenue attributed to CRM: $2.1M → $6.8M** (224% increase)

## Tools

HubSpot, Zapier, Airtable, Calendly, Yesware, Google Workspace
