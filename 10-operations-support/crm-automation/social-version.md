# CRM Cleanup + Automation — Case Study

💀 A $40K/year marketing budget going into a digital graveyard.

Northvista Realty had 12,400 contacts in HubSpot. 73% were dead data. Agents had given up. Response times averaged 6.3 hours. Nobody was winning.

After 8 weeks:
✅ 8,400 contacts cleaned in 2 weeks
✅ Lead response: 6.3 hrs → 8 min
✅ Agent adoption: 40% → 94%
✅ Lead conversion: 2.1% → 8.7% (314% improvement)
✅ Marketing ROI: $0.31 → $1.87/dollar (503% improvement)
✅ Revenue from CRM: $2.1M → $6.8M

"We went from $2.1M to $6.8M in closed business from marketing leads. That $24K investment returned over $3.6M in new revenue."

The fix: Complete CRM rebuild — data remediation, 14 automated sequences, new scoring model, accountability systems.
