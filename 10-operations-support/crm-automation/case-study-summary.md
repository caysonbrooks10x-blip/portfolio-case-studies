# Case Study Summary: CRM Cleanup + Automation

**Client:** Priya Sharma, Director of Operations at Northvista Realty (42 agents, $180M volume)

**Problem:** HubSpot CRM was a data graveyard — 8,400 contacts, 73% unusable, zero follow-up discipline, $40K marketing budget leaking into dead leads.

**Solution:** Complete CRM rebuild — data remediation (8,400 contacts cleaned), 14 automated sequences, new lead scoring, agent training, accountability systems.

**Results:**
- **8,400 contacts cleaned in 2 weeks**
- Lead response time: 6.3 hrs → 8 min (96% improvement)
- CRM adoption: 40% → 94%
- Lead conversion: 2.1% → 8.7% (314% improvement)
- Marketing ROI: $0.31 → $1.87 per dollar (503% improvement)
- Revenue from CRM: $2.1M → $6.8M (224% increase)

**Duration:** 8 weeks | **Investment:** $24,000
