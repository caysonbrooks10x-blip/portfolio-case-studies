# Case Study: Ad Creative Production System for Velocity Commerce

## Client Overview

**Client:** Velocity Commerce — a direct-to-consumer supplement brand launching a new pre-workout product line. They had a proven product and $2M in annual revenue from their existing supplement line, but zero video ad experience. Their Facebook and Instagram ad creative was limited to static images and carousel ads, which their media buyer reported were experiencing "ad fatigue" with rising CPMs and declining ROAS.

**Challenge:** Velocity needed to create a scalable video ad creative system that could produce 20-30 video ad variants per month for testing, reduce their creative production cost from $2,400 per video (agency rate) to under $300 per video, and achieve a target ROAS of 3.5x or higher. Their team had no in-house video production capability and a 6-week product launch deadline.

**Objective:** Build an ad creative production system that could generate continuous video ad variations at scale, optimized for Facebook and Instagram's video-first algorithm, while maintaining creative quality that converts viewers into buyers.

## The Problem with Their Previous Approach

Before our engagement, Velocity was relying on:

- **Static images:** Converting at 1.2% CTR, driving CPMs up as Meta's algorithm deprioritized low-engagement formats
- **Agency-produced video:** $2,400 per 30-second video, too expensive for the 20+ variants needed for proper testing
- **Limited creative testing:** Running 3-4 ad variations due to production costs, leading to rapid audience exhaustion
- **No UGC integration:** Missing the authentic creator content that outperforms polished brand content in feed environments
- **Repetitive visuals:** Same product shots, same testimonials, same format every time

## Our Approach: Scalable Creative Production System

### Phase 1: Creative Infrastructure (Weeks 1-2)

We established a production system designed for volume without sacrificing quality:

**Production Setup (Under $2,000):**
- iPhone 14 Pro with gimbal ( Zhiyun Smooth 5)
- Godox LED panel lights (2x)
- Rode Wireless GO II lavalier system
- Green screen kit for versatility
- Basic backdrop options (white, black, lifestyle)

**Software Stack:**
- CapCut Pro (primary editing)
- Premiere Pro (advanced color and effects)
- Canva (quick thumbnail and static ad creation)
- Descript (transcription and caption generation)
- Canva Pro (static ad templates)

**Creative Framework: The 6-Second Hook System**
We developed a library of proven hook templates:
1. **The Product Reveal:** Close-up dramatic product shot
2. **The Transformation:** Before/after quick cut
3. **The Stat Hook:** Bold number overlay
4. **The Problem Agitation:** Pain point demonstration
5. **The Social Proof:** Testimonial quick-cut
6. **The Benefit Promise:** Outcome-focused statement

### Phase 2: Template Production System (Weeks 3-4)

We created a templated production workflow that allowed Velocity's team to produce ads rapidly:

**Template Library Development:**
- **Hook templates (6):** Proven 6-second openers
- **Body templates (8):** Different product demonstration styles
- **CTA templates (4):** Call-to-action variations
- **Format variations:** 1:1 feed, 9:16 Stories/Reels, 4:5 in-feed

**Batch Production Protocol:**
- Weekly "creative sprint" sessions: 4 hours, 8-12 ad variants produced
- Product showcase rotation: 4 different setups, unlimited combinations
- UGC-style filming guide: How to film authentic creator content
- Testimonial filming kit: DIY guide sent to customers

**The "Creative Assembly Line" System:**
1. Film product showcase segments (2 hours)
2. Film testimonial segments (1 hour)
3. Film lifestyle/context segments (1 hour)
4. Edit into 8-12 variations in post (CapCut, 4 hours)

### Phase 3: Testing & Optimization (Weeks 5-8)

We implemented a data-driven creative iteration system:

**A/B Testing Framework:**
- Test hook type vs. hook type (same body)
- Test body style vs. body style (same hook)
- Test CTA vs. CTA (same creative)
- Test format vs. format (same message)

**Performance Tracking Dashboard:**
- CTR by hook type
- Watch time by duration
- Conversion rate by CTA
- Cost per result by creative element

**Creative Refresh Cadence:**
- Swapped creative every 2-3 weeks (before fatigue)
- Refreshed hooks on high performers monthly
- Killed low performers within 7 days

## Key Wins

**Rapid Creative Iteration:** By week 4, Velocity was testing 8 new ad variants per week — vs. their previous 3-4 per month. This volume allowed the algorithm to find winning creatives faster and reduced CPA by 47%.

**UGC Integration:** We trained Velocity's team to collect and produce customer testimonial content. One "real customer" testimonial ad achieved 4.8x ROAS — 62% better than their best brand-produced creative.

**Format Optimization:** 4:5 in-feed videos outperformed 1:1 by 34% in CTR. Stories/Reels format drove 28% lower CPM but higher volume. We built a format allocation strategy around these findings.

**The "Evergreen" System:** By creating "story arcs" across ad sequences (problem → product → proof → offer), Velocity reduced creative fatigue from 2 weeks to 6 weeks, extending the life of high-performing ad concepts.

## Lessons Learned

1. **Hook is 80% of the ad.** The first 3 seconds determine whether someone watches or scrolls. We tested 6 hook types and found 3 that consistently outperformed — focusing production on these drove CTR from 1.2% to 2.8%.

2. **Volume beats perfection.** At $285/video, you can test 8 variants vs. 1. The algorithm finds winners faster with more shots on goal.

3. **Authentic beats polished.** UGC-style testimonial ads outperformed studio-produced content by 62% in conversion rate. The "real person" aesthetic triggers trust signals that polished production can't replicate.

4. **Format determines distribution.** 4:5 in-feed outperformed 1:1 for direct response. Different formats suit different objectives — we built a format matrix for Velocity.

5. **Refresh, don't abandon.** High-performing ads can be refreshed with new hooks rather than killed. We extended creative lifespans by 3x with strategic refreshing.
