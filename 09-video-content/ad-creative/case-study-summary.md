# Ad Creative Production System — Case Study Summary

## Velocity Commerce | Video Ad Creative at Scale

**Industry:** Direct-to-Consumer Supplements
**Timeline:** 8 weeks
**Services:** Ad creative production system, template library, UGC integration, testing framework

---

## The Challenge
Velocity Commerce had a proven product but was paying $2,400 per video ad to an agency. With only 3-4 ad variants per month, they were experiencing rapid creative fatigue, declining CTR (1.2%), and ROAS of just 1.8x. They needed a system for producing video ads at scale — without a massive production budget.

## What We Built
A scalable video ad creative production system featuring:
- Complete production infrastructure (under $2,000)
- 6 hook templates + 8 body templates + 4 CTA templates
- "Creative Assembly Line" batch production workflow
- UGC testimonial collection and production system
- A/B testing framework for creative elements
- Format optimization matrix (1:1, 4:5, 9:16)
- Weekly creative sprint protocol (8-12 variants per session)

## Results at 8 Weeks
- **Video Ad Variants/Month:** 3-4 → 24-32 (+700%)
- **Cost Per Video:** $2,400 → $285 (-88%)
- **Average CTR:** 1.2% → 2.8% (+133%)
- **Video Completion Rate:** 28% → 67% (+139%)
- **ROAS:** 1.8x → 4.2x (+133%)
- **CPM:** $42 → $28 (-33%)
- **New Customers:** 180 → 640/month (+256%)

## Tools
CapCut Pro, Premiere Pro, Descript, Canva Pro, Meta Ads Manager, Zhiyun Gimbal, Godox LED Panels, Rode Wireless GO

## Outcome
Client operates creative sprints independently, producing 24-32 ad variants monthly at $285/video. System expanded to TikTok advertising. ROAS sustained at 4.2x through ongoing creative testing.
