# Visuals Plan: Ad Creative Production System

## Velocity Commerce | Video Ad Visual Strategy

---

## Visual Identity System

### Brand Elements for Ads
- **Lower Thirds:** Brand name, product name, key benefit
- **CTA Overlays:** Branded button graphics
- **Color Grading:** Consistent look across all variants
- **Logo Placement:** Subtle watermark, bottom-right

### Color Palette for Ad Creative
- **Primary:** Electric blue (#0066FF)
- **Secondary:** Clean white (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent:** Vibrant orange (#FF6B00)
- **Background:** Black (#000000) or white for contrast
- **Text:** White on dark, black on light

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Montserrat Black, 48pt+
- **Body/Stats:** Montserrat Bold, 32pt
- **CTAs:** Montserrat ExtraBold, 36pt+
- **Lower Thirds:** Montserrat Medium, 24pt

---

## Video Ad Format Specifications

### Format 1: 1:1 Square (Feed)
**Use:** Facebook/Instagram feed, generic testing
**Specs:**
- Resolution: 1080×1080
- Duration: 15-30 seconds
- Safe zone: Center 80% of frame
- File size: < 15MB

### Format 2: 4:5 Portrait (In-Feed)
**Use:** Primary format, highest CTR
**Specs:**
- Resolution: 1080×1350
- Duration: 15-30 seconds
- Safe zone: Center 85% of frame
- File size: < 20MB

### Format 3: 9:16 Stories/Reels
**Use:** Stories, Reels placement, volume
**Specs:**
- Resolution: 1080×1920
- Duration: 10-15 seconds (Stories), 15-30 seconds (Reels)
- Safe zone: Center 70% of frame (edges cut on some devices)
- File size: < 15MB

## Hook Visual Templates

### Hook 1: Product Reveal (0:00-0:06)
**Visual:** Close-up product shot, dramatic reveal
**Style:**
- Black background or clean white
- Product enters frame with motion
- Bold product name text overlay
- Duration: 3 seconds

**Variations:**
- Slow motion product pour/unwrap
- Hand-to-product transition
- Product against lifestyle background

### Hook 2: Transformation (0:00-0:06)
**Visual:** Before/after quick cut
**Style:**
- Split screen OR quick sequential cuts
- Bold text: "BEFORE → AFTER"
- Subtle transition effect
- Duration: 3 seconds

**Variations:**
- Static transformation (supplement packaging)
- Person-focused transformation (energy levels)
- Results-focused transformation (progress photos)

### Hook 3: Stat Hook (0:00-0:06)
**Visual:** Bold number/text on screen
**Style:**
- Large number (72pt+): "84%" or "3x"
- Supporting text below: "of athletes prefer..."
- Subtle animation (pop-in, scale)
- Duration: 3 seconds

**Variations:**
- Single stat
- Comparison stat
- Countdown stat

### Hook 4: Problem Agitation (0:00-0:06)
**Visual:** Pain point demonstration
**Style:**
- Subject demonstrates problem (frustrated expression)
- Text overlay: Problem statement
- Music/ sound: Tension-building
- Duration: 3 seconds

### Hook 5: Social Proof (0:00-0:06)
**Visual:** Customer testimonial quick-cut
**Style:**
- Real person on camera (UGC style)
- Quote overlay or voiceover
- Star rating or result text
- Duration: 3 seconds

### Hook 6: Benefit Promise (0:00-0:06)
**Visual:** Outcome-focused statement
**Style:**
- Subject in aspirational moment
- Bold benefit statement overlay
- Confident expression/pose
- Duration: 3 seconds

## Text Overlay Guidelines

### Headline Text
- **Font:** Montserrat Black
- **Size:** 48-72pt (readable on mobile)
- **Color:** White with black outline OR brand colors
- **Position:** Upper 40% of frame
- **Animation:** Pop-in, 0.3s duration

### Benefit Callouts
- **Font:** Montserrat Bold
- **Size:** 32-40pt
- **Color:** White or brand accent
- **Position:** Center or lower-third
- **Animation:** Fade-in, 0.2s duration

### Stat Overlays
- **Font:** Montserrat ExtraBold
- **Size:** 72-96pt for main number
- **Color:** Brand accent or white
- **Position:** Center frame
- **Animation:** Scale pop, 0.2s duration

### Legal/Disclaimer Text
- **Font:** Montserrat Regular
- **Size:** 14-18pt
- **Color:** White with 60% opacity
- **Position:** Bottom of frame
- **Duration:** Visible for 3+ seconds

## Lighting & Color Grading

### Product Shots
- **Key Light:** 45° angle, soft box
- **Fill Light:** Opposite side, lower intensity
- **Background Light:** Rim light for separation
- **Color Grade:** Slight warm, high contrast, crushed blacks

### Lifestyle/Talking Head
- **Three-Point Setup:** Key, fill, hair/rim
- **Skin Tone Priority:** Warm, natural (not orange)
- **Brand Consistency:** Same LUT applied to all variants

### Green Screen (if used)
- **Background:** Pure green (#00FF00)
- **Lighting:** Even coverage, no hot spots
- **Key:** Clean separation, no spill

## UGC Filming Guide

### Subject Instructions
- Natural daylight near window
- Phone at eye level or slightly above
- Conversational tone (like talking to a friend)
- 30-60 second takes
- Multiple takes encouraged

### Content Requests
- Unboxing/opening product
- First time trying product
- Personal result story
- "What I love about..." format
- Before/after (if applicable)

### Quality Standards
- Horizontal phone holding (for 1:1/4:5)
- Landscape mode for best quality
- Good lighting (natural or ring light)
- Clean audio (minimal background noise)
- Stabilized (tripod or propped)

---

## Editing Pacing Standards

### Cut Frequency by Section
- **Hook (0:00-0:06):** Fast cuts, 2-3 seconds each
- **Body (0:06-0:20):** Medium pace, 3-4 seconds each
- **CTA (0:20-0:30):** Slower, emphasis cuts

### Transition Style
- **Hard cuts:** Between sections
- **Dissolve:** Between testimonial segments
- **Match cuts:** For product reveals
- **No:** Overly complex transitions

### Music/Sound
- **Trending sounds:** Monitor TikTok Creative Center
- **Library music:** Upbeat, high-energy (YouTube Audio Library)
- **Levels:** Music at -18dB to -15dB (under voice)
- **No:** Copyrighted music without license

## Production Checklist

### Pre-Shoot
- [ ] Equipment charged and tested
- [ ] Product samples prepared
- [ ] Lighting setup complete
- [ ] Background styled
- [ ] Shot list reviewed

### During Shoot
- [ ] Multiple takes per segment (3+)
- [ ] Audio levels checked between takes
- [ ] Lighting consistency maintained
- [ ] All B-roll captured per shot list
- [ ] UGC content collected if available

### Post-Production
- [ ] Footage organized by batch
- [ ] Best takes selected
- [ ] Hook assembled first
- [ ] Body variations created
- [ ] CTAs added to all variants
- [ ] Text overlays applied
- [ ] Color grade consistent
- [ ] Export at correct format/resolution
