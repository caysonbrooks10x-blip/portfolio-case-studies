## Post 4: The 80/20 Framework
Most founders post 80% about themselves:
"We achieved X" "I'm proud to announce Y" "Buy our services"

Stop.
The content framework that works:
→ 80% value: insights, frameworks, lessons, opinions
→ 20% business: indirect, never "buy this"

You're not selling. You're educating. Trust builds.

---

## Post 5: The Production Secret
Don't create content daily.
Batch produce.
4 hours per week produces 15-18 pieces:
→ 3 long-form posts
→ 5 short-form videos
→ 7 quote graphics
→ 3 commentary posts

One content day per week. The rest of the week: schedule and engage.

Build carousel frameworks. Not posts — resources people bookmark.

## Post 7: The Content Bank
Always have 2-3 weeks of content in reserve.
Why?
→ Business travel happens
→ Busy client weeks happen
→ Consistency matters more than virality

When you have a content bank, you never miss a posting day. And consistency beats virality every time.

---

## Post 8: The Video Advantage
Video on LinkedIn converts followers to connections at 3x the rate of text.
Why?
→ People see your face, hear your voice
→ Trust builds faster
→ You become "real" to your audience

You don't need to be a "natural." You need to share expertise conversationally. That's it.
