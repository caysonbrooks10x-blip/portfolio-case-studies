# Founder Personal Brand Content System — Case Study Summary

## Apex Operations | Thought Leadership System

**Industry:** Operations Consulting / B2B
**Timeline:** 6 months
**Services:** Personal brand strategy, content production system, LinkedIn growth, thought leadership positioning

---

## The Challenge
Daniel Reyes had 15 years of operational expertise but only 340 LinkedIn followers. He wasn't posting consistently, had no content system, and believed LinkedIn "didn't work for consulting." Meanwhile, competitors with less expertise were building audiences of 50,000+ and generating inbound leads.

## What We Built
A complete personal brand content system featuring:
- Strategic positioning and content pillars (5 pillars)
- 80/20 value framework (80% value, 20% business)
- Content production assembly system (15 pieces/week from 4-hour session)
- Content bank strategy (2-3 weeks in reserve)
- Distribution and engagement system
- "Creator mindset" coaching
- Newsletter integration (Substack)
- Book launch content strategy

## Results at 6 Months
- **LinkedIn Followers:** 340 → 31,400 (+9,135%)
- **Connection Requests Accepted:** 2-3 → 180/month (+6,000%)
- **Inbound Consulting Leads:** 0 → 22/month
- **Speaking Inquiries:** 0 → 3/month
- **Podcast Requests:** 0 → 4/month
- **Newsletter Subscribers:** 0 → 8,400
- **Book Pre-Orders:** 0 → 4,200
- **Content Output:** 2-3 → 15-18 pieces/week
- **Time Investment:** 8-10 hrs → 6-7 hrs/week (-30%)

## Tools
LinkedIn, Canva, CapCut, Riverside, Notion, Buffer, Substack, Loom

## Outcome
Inbound leads now exceed referral pipeline. Daniel turned away more clients than he took. Speaking, podcast, and media opportunities now inbound. System running autonomously with 3-week content bank.
