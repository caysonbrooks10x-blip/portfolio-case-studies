## Content Quality QA

### Carousel Posts
- [ ] Title slide: Clear, attention-grabbing
- [ ] Content slides: One point per slide
- [ ] Text: Readable (48pt+), minimal
- [ ] Visual: Consistent branding, professional
- [ ] CTA: Present on final slide
- [ ] Hashtags: Relevant, varied
- [ ] Value-first: No direct selling

### Short-Form Video
- [ ] Hook: Attention-grabbing first 5 seconds
- [ ] Lighting: Professional, well-lit
- [ ] Audio: Clear, no background noise
- [ ] Framing: Eye level or slightly above
- [ ] Background: Clean, branded
- [ ] Captions: Accurate, synced
- [ ] Text overlays: Brief, supportive
- [ ] CTA: Present (follow or question)
- [ ] Duration: 60-90 seconds optimal

### Quote Graphics
- [ ] Quote: Accurate, insightful
- [ ] Attribution: Correct name and title
- [ ] Design: Professional, on-brand
- [ ] Readability: Text readable at mobile size
- [ ] File quality: High resolution (1080×1080)

### Long-Form Posts
- [ ] Hook: Strong first 2 lines (shown before "see more")
- [ ] Structure: Paragraphs with line breaks
- [ ] Formatting: Easy to scan
- [ ] Value: Genuinely helpful or insightful
- [ ] Length: 800-1,500 words
- [ ] Hashtags: Relevant, 3-5 at end
- [ ] No: Direct selling or pitches

### Commentary Posts
- [ ] Timely: References current news or trends
- [ ] Opinion: Clear, defensible take
- [ ] Value: Adds insight beyond restating
- [ ] Engagement: Ends with question
- [ ] Tone: Conversational, authentic

## Post-Project Lessons

1. **Content pillars needed adjustment.** Founder Lessons pillar underperformed initially. Shifted focus to Operational Frameworks (core expertise) at week 6.

2. **Video confidence grew over time.** Daniel's first videos were stiff. By video #10, his natural conversational style emerged. Built confidence framework for new creators.

3. **Content bank was critical.** By week 4, busy client period depleted content bank. Built mandatory 3-week reserve into system.

4. **Engagement lagged posting initially.** Posting cadence established before engagement system. Made engagement a daily habit alongside posting.

5. **Long-form posts hardest for client.** Daniel was most comfortable with frameworks and video. Long-form writing required most coaching. Built templates and frameworks for structure.
