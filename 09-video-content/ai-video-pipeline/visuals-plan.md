# Visuals Plan: AI-Assisted Video Production Pipeline

## Visual Identity System

### Brand Elements
- **Lower Thirds:** Name, title, company
- **End Cards:** CTA, subscribe, contact info
- **Transitions:** Branded wipes and fades
- **Logo Treatment:** Consistent placement, opacity

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep blue (#1E3A5F)
- **Secondary:** Bright teal (#00B4D8)
- **Accent:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Text:** Light gray (#E8E8E8)
- **Background:** Dark charcoal (#2D2D2D)

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Montserrat Bold, 48pt+
- **Lower Thirds:** Montserrat Medium, 28pt
- **Body:** Inter Regular, 24pt
- **Captions:** Inter Regular, 20pt

## AI-Generated Visual Quality Standards

### Minimum Quality Thresholds
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum, 4K preferred
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps minimum
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (primary), 1:1, 9:16 as needed
- **Color Grading:** Consistent with brand LUT
- **Audio:** -12dB to -6dB, no clipping

### AI B-Roll Quality
- **Runway Gen-2:** 1080p minimum, checked for artifacts
- **Human review required** for all AI-generated visuals
- **Brand consistency** enforced at QA checkpoint
- **No AI-generated faces** (use real footage or illustrations)

### Caption Standards
- **Accuracy:** 96%+ (post-AI correction)
- **Timing:** Synced within 100ms
- **Style:** Montserrat Bold, white text, black outline
- **Size:** Minimum 24pt (readable on mobile)

## Video Format Specifications

### Product Demo (Primary Format)
- **Duration:** 3-5 minutes
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080 (16:9)
- **Structure:** Hook → Problem → Solution → Demo → CTA
- **AI Elements:** Transcript-based editing, auto-captions
- **Human Elements:** Script approval, demo execution, final QA

### Tutorial/How-To
- **Duration:** 5-8 minutes
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080 (16:9)
- **Structure:** Problem → Steps → Outcome → Summary
- **AI Elements:** Transcription, captions, rough cut
- **Human Elements:** Screen recording, step validation, final approval

### Customer Testimonial
- **Duration:** 2-3 minutes
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080 (16:9)
- **Structure:** Hook → Story → Results → Quote → CTA
- **AI Elements:** Transcription, captions, multi-format export
- **Human Elements:** Interview, B-roll capture, narrative approval

### Thought Leadership
- **Duration:** 4-6 minutes
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080 (16:9)
- **Structure:** Insight → Evidence → Application → CTA
- **AI Elements:** Script structuring, auto-captions
- **Human Elements:** Expert interview, strategic direction, final approval

### Social Clips (AI-Generated)
- **Duration:** 30-90 seconds
- **Resolution:** 1080×1920 (9:16) for Shorts, 1080×1080 (1:1) for Feed
- **Source:** Extracted from long-form via Pictory
- **AI Elements:** Clip selection, caption generation, format adaptation
- **Human Elements:** Clip approval, caption review, final export

## AI Tool Visual Outputs

### Runway Gen-2
**Use:** B-roll generation, background removal, visual effects
**Quality Check:**
- Check for artifacts (especially faces, text)
- Verify brand color consistency
- Ensure resolution meets 1080p minimum
- Human review required before use

### Descript
**Use:** Transcript-based editing, overdub, captioning
**Quality Check:**
- Transcription accuracy: 96%+ (spot-check)
- Caption sync: within 100ms
- Overdub voice: matches original
- Visual transitions: smooth

### ElevenLabs
**Use:** AI voiceovers, localization, accents
**Quality Check:**
- Natural-sounding pacing
- Accurate pronunciation (technical terms)
- Appropriate tone (professional vs. conversational)
- Consistency across versions

### Pictory
**Use:** Automated clip extraction, highlight reels
**Quality Check:**
- Clip selection makes sense (contextual)
- Transition points clean
- Captions accurate
- Suitable for intended platform

### Canva AI
**Use:** Thumbnail generation, design suggestions, template adaptation
**Quality Check:**
- Brand consistency
- Readability at small sizes
- Professional appearance
- A/B test approved designs

## Human-AI Handoff Points

### Pre-Production
1. **Content Brief** → Human creates concept → AI structures outline → Human approves
2. **Script Draft** → Human writes → AI suggests improvements → Human finalizes

### Production
3. **Filming** → Human executes → AI not involved
4. **Raw Footage** → Human reviews → AI transcription begins

### Post-Production
5. **Transcript** → AI generates → Human corrects errors → Human approves
6. **Rough Cut** → AI assembles → Human refines → Human approves
7. **Captions** → AI generates → Human reviews → Human approves
8. **Graphics** → Human/MRI design → AI assists → Human finalizes
9. **Export** → Human requests → AI formats → Human reviews

### Distribution
10. **Social Clips** → AI extracts → Human approves → AI adapts formats → Human posts

## Export Specifications

### YouTube
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
- **File Size:** < 2GB (for upload)
- **Thumbnail:** 1280×720px

### YouTube Shorts
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution:** 1080×1920
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16
- **Duration:** < 60 seconds
- **Captions:** Required

### LinkedIn/Social
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution:** 1080×1080 or 1080×1350
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 or 4:5
- **Captions:** Required for feed

### Website Embed
- **Format:** MP4 or WebM
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080
- **Compression:** Handbrake for web
- **Poster Image:** 1280×720px
