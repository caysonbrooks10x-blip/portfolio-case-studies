# Talking Points: AI-Assisted Video Production Pipeline

## Core Value Proposition
We build hybrid human-AI video production pipelines that amplify your team's output by 3x — handling mechanical tasks (transcription, rough cuts, captions, exports) with AI while your team focuses on strategy and creativity.

- The goal is human-AI collaboration, not replacement

- 60% of production time goes to mechanical tasks
- Transcription, rough cuts, captions, exports — AI handles these

- Cost per video: $3,200 → $1,050 (-67%)

- Automated social clip extraction
- Rapid localization for market expansion
- Scale impossible with purely manual processes

**5. Quality Maintained Through Human Oversight**
- AI output reviewed by humans
- QA checkpoints at every AI handoff

## Objection Handlers

**"Our content is technical — AI can't handle it"**
Response: "AI handles the mechanical work, not the content judgment. Your subject matter experts still create the content. AI just makes the production faster. We had the same concern — but the technical accuracy actually improved because reviewers had more time to focus on content quality."

**"Won't AI produce generic, robotic content?"**
Response: "Pure AI output does risk that. Our approach includes human review at every AI handoff. AI generates, humans refine. The pipeline has built-in QA checkpoints. Brand guidelines are explicit. The result is content that's indistinguishable from fully manual production — but created 3x faster."

Response: "At $309,600 annual savings on a $50,000-80,000 investment, the ROI is under 3 months. That's before considering the competitive advantage of 3x production speed and the value of launching products 6 weeks earlier."

## Questions Prospects Ask

**"How long does implementation take?"**
8 weeks to full operational capacity. Week 1-2: audit and design. Week 3-5: integration and testing. Week 6-8: training and optimization. We don't just hand over tools — we ensure the pipeline works for your specific workflow.

**"What if our team resists AI?"**
Change management is part of the engagement. We frame AI as augmentation, not replacement. Most teams embrace AI once they see it removing the tasks they didn't enjoy anyway.

**"What AI tools do you recommend?"**
We evaluate tools based on your existing stack, budget, and requirements. We typically integrate: Descript (transcription/editing), Runway (visual AI), ElevenLabs (voice), Pictory (clip extraction), CapCut (captioning), and ChatGPT (structuring).

**"How do we maintain quality control?"**
Every AI handoff has a human review checkpoint. We build QA protocols into the pipeline. Brand guidelines are codified. Final approval always human. The quality is maintained because humans stay in the loop.
