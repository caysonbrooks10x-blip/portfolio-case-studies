# AI-Assisted Video Production Pipeline — Case Study Summary

## Meridian Tech | Hybrid Intelligence Production System

**Industry:** B2B SaaS / Construction Technology
**Timeline:** 8 weeks
**Services:** AI workflow integration, production pipeline design, team training, automation implementation

---

## The Challenge
Meridian Tech needed to triple video production (4-5 → 12-15 videos/month) without adding headcount or budget. They were skeptical of AI tools but facing a go-to-market deadline for a major product launch. The bottleneck wasn't creativity — it was mechanical repetition that consumed 60% of production time.

## What We Built
A hybrid human-AI video production pipeline featuring:
- AI transcription and rough cut assembly (Descript)
- AI B-roll generation and enhancement (Runway Gen-2)
- AI voiceovers and localization (ElevenLabs)
- AI clip extraction for social (Pictory)
- AI captioning and multi-format export (CapCut)
- AI script structuring and content briefs (ChatGPT)
- Complete SOP documentation and team training

## Results at 8 Weeks
- **Videos/Month:** 4-5 → 12-15 (+240%)
- **Cost Per Video:** $3,200 → $1,050 (-67%)
- **Production Time:** 40 hrs → 18 hrs (-55%)
- **External Shoots:** 4 → 1-2/month (-75%)
- **Revision Rounds:** 3-4 → 1-2 (-60%)
- **Content Calendar Adherence:** 35% → 92% (+163%)
- **Monthly Savings:** $25,800 ($309,600 annually)

## Tools
Runway Gen-2, Descript, ElevenLabs, Pictory, Camtasia, Canva, CapCut, ChatGPT, Premiere Pro, After Effects

## Outcome
Pipeline operational and team trained. New product launch delivered 3 weeks ahead of schedule. Team now works at top of license — strategy and creativity, not transcription and clicking.
