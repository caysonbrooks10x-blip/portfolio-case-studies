# Talking Points: Short-Form Content Engine

## Core Value Proposition
We build sustainable short-form video content systems that produce 30-50 videos per month — not just create videos, but build the machine that creates them.

---

## Key Messages

**1. The Problem with "Just Create Content"**
- Most brands approach short-form video as a task, not a system
- Outsourcing is too expensive for daily posting
- In-house teams burn out trying to be creative on deadline
- The real need is infrastructure, not inspiration

**2. Our Approach: Build the Engine**
- We design the production system first
- Batch production workflow (15-20 videos per day)
- Platform-specific optimization for TikTok, Reels, Shorts
- Data-driven iteration every two weeks

**3. The Results Are Measurable**
- 89,200 TikTok followers in 90 days (Luxe Beauty)
- 86% reduction in cost per video ($85 → $12)
- 292% increase in content output
- 4.7% engagement rate (industry avg is 1-2%)

**4. Sustainability Is Built In**
- Complete SOP documentation
- Team training included
- Client runs the engine autonomously after 12 weeks
- No ongoing production fees required

## Objection Handlers (see objections.md for full responses)

**"We tried TikTok and it didn't work"**
Response: You tried *posting on TikTok*. There's a difference between that and running a TikTok growth *system*. Our approach uses data to iterate, trending audio to get distribution, and consistency to build momentum.

**"We don't have anyone who can be on camera"**
Response: Not every video needs a face. We have multiple content pillar formats that work without on-screen talent: text overlays, screen recordings, product-focused content, and voiceover formats.

**"Our product isn't exciting enough for video"**
Response: The most successful skincare, B2B, and financial accounts on TikTok prove otherwise. It's never about the product — it's about the hook, the education, and the connection.

## Questions Prospects Ask

**"How long until we see results?"**
Week 4-6 for initial traction. Week 8-12 for meaningful follower growth. Viral moments can happen at any point, but algorithm momentum typically builds after 30 consecutive posting days.

**"What do we need to provide?"**
Brand guidelines, product access, and ideally 2-4 hours per week for content review during the build phase. After launch, your main time investment is the production day (8 hours every 1-2 weeks).

**"Can we take over after the 12 weeks?"**
Yes — and that's the goal. We build the engine for you to own. We provide complete documentation, training, and a 30-day transition support period.
