# Short-Form Content Engine — Case Study Summary

## Luxe Beauty Co. | TikTok & Instagram Growth System

**Industry:** Direct-to-Consumer Skincare
**Timeline:** 90 days
**Services:** Content production system build, platform growth strategy, workflow automation

---

## The Challenge
Luxe Beauty Co. had 2.1K Instagram followers, zero TikTok presence, and was producing 8-12 posts monthly. Their competitors dominated short-form video while they relied on static imagery. They needed a content engine, not just a content creator.

## What We Built
A complete short-form content production and distribution system featuring:
- Batch production workflow (15-20 videos per 8-hour content day)
- 5 content pillars aligned to brand and audience
- 3-platform distribution (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts)
- Optimized posting cadence (up to 47 videos/month)
- Internal training and SOP documentation

## Results at 90 Days
- **TikTok:** 0 → 89,200 followers
- **Instagram:** 2,100 → 31,400 followers (+1,395%)
- **Engagement Rate:** 0.8% → 4.7%
- **Content Output:** 8-12/month → 47 videos/month
- **Cost Per Video:** $85 → $12 (-86%)
- **Monthly Leads:** 15 → 340 (+2,167%)
- **Revenue Attribution:** $2,200 → $18,400/month (+736%)

## Tools
CapCut Pro, Descript, ElevenLabs, Canva, Airtable, TikTok Creative Center

## Outcome
Client internalized the engine at 90 days. Follower growth continued to 127K at 6 months with zero ongoing production fees. System documented and team trained for autonomous operation.
