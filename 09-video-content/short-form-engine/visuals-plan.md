## Video Format Specifications

### Format 1: Hook (15 seconds)
**Use:** Grab attention, drive follows
**Specs:**
- First 3 seconds: Bold text or surprising visual
- Seconds 4-12: Core content (voiceover + visuals)
- Seconds 13-15: CTA + follow prompt
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (vertical)
- Resolution: 1080×1920
- File size: < 50MB

**Visual Treatment:**
- Quick cuts (every 2-3 seconds)
- Dynamic text animations
- Trending audio synced to cuts

---

### Format 2: Educational (30 seconds)
**Use:** Ingredient education, tutorial, myth-busting
**Specs:**
- Hook text overlay (2 seconds)
- Main content with voiceover (22 seconds)
- Key points in lower third (animated)
- CTA with swipe-up prompt (6 seconds)
- Aspect ratio: 9:16
- Resolution: 1080×1920

**Visual Treatment:**
- Product shots on clean background
- Text callouts with brand colors
- Subtle zoom on key moments
- Consistent lower third design

---

### Format 3: Story (60 seconds)
**Use:** Brand story, day-in-the-life, transformation
**Specs:**
- Intro hook (3 seconds)
- Narrative arc (50 seconds)
- Outro CTA (7 seconds)
- Aspect ratio: 9:16
- Resolution: 1080×1920

**Visual Treatment:**
- Mix of close-up and wide shots
- B-roll for pacing
- Interview-style setup if on-camera
- Emotional transitions (dissolves, match cuts)

## Content Pillar Visual Guides

### Pillar 1: Skincare Routine ("Get Ready With Me")
- **Setup:** Bathroom counter with soft lighting, products lined up
- **Camera:** Front-facing, slightly above eye level
- **Attire:** Clean, neutral, on-brand
- **B-roll:** Hands applying product, close-ups of textures
- **Background:** Branded backdrop or clean marble

### Pillar 2: Ingredient Education
- **Setup:** Flat lay with product + ingredient visual (molecule graphic)
- **Graphics:** Animated ingredient breakdown
- **Text:** Bold keyword callouts
- **Color coding:** Ingredient-specific accent colors

### Pillar 3: Before/After Transformations
- **Setup:** Consistent lighting, same angle, same backdrop
- **Format:** Split screen or swipe reveal
- **Requirements:** Real customer photos, consent documented
- **Graphics:** Subtle fade transitions, no misleading edits

### Pillar 4: Skincare Myths vs. Facts
- **Format:** Text-first with bold graphics
- **Structure:** "MYTH" red X → "FACT" green checkmark
- **Visual:** Minimal, focus on text animation
- **Audio:** Engaging voiceover or trending sound

### Pillar 5: Brand Storytelling
- **Setup:** Behind-the-scenes of production, founder moments
- **Tone:** Authentic, less polished than other content
- **B-roll:** Team, workspace, product creation
- **Music:** Softer, emotional undertone

## Editing Style Guide

### Transition Standards
- **Standard cut:** 0.3 seconds
- **Creative moments:** 0.5 seconds with slight zoom
- **B-roll to talking:** Dip to black (0.2s)
- **Never:** Hard cuts during speech

### Caption Style
- **Font:** Montserrat Bold
- **Size:** Minimum 24pt (readable on mobile)
- **Color:** White with black outline (2px)
- **Animation:** Pop-in, fade, or highlight
- **Position:** Lower third OR center (for emphasis)

### Pacing
- **Talking head:** 1.2x speed for energy
- **Tutorial:** 1.0x (clear demonstration)
- **Storytelling:** 0.95x (slightly slower for emotion)

### Color Grading
- **Skin tones:** Warm, natural, no orange shift
- **Products:** True-to-color, slightly brightened
- **Mood:** Clean, bright, premium feel
- **Lighting:** Three-point setup, soft fill

## Production Day Visual Checklist

### Pre-Production
- [ ] Lighting set up and tested
- [ ] Background styled and on-brand
- [ ] Products staged and ready
- [ ] Camera angle locked
- [ ] Audio levels checked

### During Production
- [ ] Every video: Check framing before recording
- [ ] Every video: 3 takes minimum
- [ ] B-roll captured for each main shot
- [ ] Quick review of footage before moving on

### Post-Production
- [ ] Raw footage organized by batch
- [ ] Best take selected per video
- [ ] Captions added with brand font
- [ ] Color grade applied consistently
- [ ] Export at 1080×1920, H.264
