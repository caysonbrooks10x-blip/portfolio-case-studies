# Case Study: Short-Form Content Engine for Luxe Beauty Co.

## Client Overview

**Client:** Luxe Beauty Co. — a direct-to-consumer skincare brand launched in 2022, specializing in premium serums and moisturizers targeted at women aged 28-45.

**Challenge:** The brand had a modest Instagram following (2.1K) and zero presence on TikTok. Their content output was 2-3 posts per week, mostly static product images with generic captions. Competitors were dominating the "skincare routine" and "skin positivity" niches with high-engagement video content. Leadership knew they needed to enter short-form video but had no in-house video production capability and a content budget that couldn't support hiring a full agency.

**Objective:** Build a sustainable short-form content engine that could produce 30-50 videos per month, grow TikTok from zero to 50K followers in 90 days, and increase Instagram engagement rate from 0.8% to 4%+.

## The Problem with Their Previous Approach

Before engaging our services, Luxe Beauty attempted to create short-form content in-house using an iPhone and a part-time social media manager. Issues included:

- Inconsistent posting schedule (1-2 times per week, never predictable)
- Poor lighting and audio quality that hurt brand perception
- No understanding of platform-specific optimization (TikTok vs. Reels vs. YouTube Shorts)
- Zero content repurposing — every video was a one-off
- No data-driven iteration; content was created based on "gut feeling"

## Our Approach: Building the Engine

### Phase 1: Infrastructure & Systems (Weeks 1-2)

We started by establishing the production infrastructure that would allow us to scale content output without sacrificing quality:

**Equipment Setup:**
- Ring light, lavalier microphones, and a lightweight tripod setup for under $400
- CapCut Pro for mobile-first editing (chosen for speed and TikTok-native features)
- Descript for transcript-based editing and caption generation
- Airtable as our content calendar and asset management system

**Content Pillar Framework:**
We defined 5 core content pillars aligned with Luxe Beauty's brand and audience interests:
1. Morning/Evening skincare routine "get ready with me" style
2. Ingredient education (hyaluronic acid, retinol, vitamin C)
3. Before/after transformations (real customer results)
4. Skincare myths vs. facts
5. Day-in-the-life brand storytelling

### Phase 2: Production Sprint (Weeks 3-8)

We batch-produced content in weekly "content days" — 8-hour sessions where we created 15-20 videos in a single day. This approach:

- Reduced setup/breakdown time by 60%
- Allowed for creative variety within consistent aesthetic
- Built a growing "content bank" that ensured never missing a posting day

**Video Specifications:**
- 3 vertical formats produced: 15-second hooks, 30-second educational, 60-second storytelling
- Every video included: dynamic captions, brand watermark, CTA overlay
- Audio strategy: trending sounds tracked via TikTok's Creative Center, plus original voiceovers via ElevenLabs for tutorial content

### Phase 3: Distribution & Optimization (Ongoing)

**Posting Cadence:**
- TikTok: 1 video daily, 7 days per week
- Instagram Reels: 5 videos per week
- YouTube Shorts: 3 videos per week (repurposed from TikTok with platform-specific tweaks)

**Hashtag & SEO Strategy:**
- 3 branded hashtags created and promoted
- 5-8 niche-specific hashtags per video (tracked and rotated based on performance)
- Description optimization with keyword-rich copy

## Lessons Learned

1. **Consistency beats virality.** Three of our top 10 performing videos had under 10K views in week one but accumulated massive views over 3-4 weeks as the algorithm continued distributing them.

2. **Batch production is non-negotiable.** Separating "creative days" from "posting days" allowed for strategic thinking rather than reactive content creation.

3. **Audio is 50% of the algorithm.** Videos with trending sounds performed 4.2x better than those with original audio or no sound.

4. **Captions drive retention.** Videos with dynamic, animated captions had a 23% higher watch time than those without.
