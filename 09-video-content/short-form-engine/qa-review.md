## Production QA

### Video Quality Standards
- [ ] Each video has minimum 3 takes recorded
- [ ] Lighting remains consistent throughout filming
- [ ] Audio is clear with no background noise
- [ ] Framing is consistent (vertical 9:16)
- [ ] No accidental camera movement or refocusing
- [ ] Product labels facing camera where relevant
- [ ] Hands appear clean and well-groomed for tutorial content

### Brand Compliance
- [ ] Logo watermark present and correctly positioned
- [ ] No competitor products visible in frame
- [ ] Claims made are substantiated (no "best ever" without data)
- [ ] Before/after claims include appropriate disclaimers
- [ ] No misleading before/after editing

---

## Post-Production QA

### Caption Quality
- [ ] All speech transcribed accurately
- [ ] Key words emphasized with CAPS or bold
- [ ] Font size readable on mobile (minimum 24pt)
- [ ] Text outline provides sufficient contrast
- [ ] Caption timing synced to audio
- [ ] Brand hashtag included in captions

### Visual Quality
- [ ] Color grading consistent across all videos
- [ ] No pixelation or compression artifacts
- [ ] Transitions smooth and intentional
- [ ] No blank frames or audio gaps
- [ ] Consistent intro/outro cards
- [ ] Resolution: 1080×1920 (9:16)

### Audio Quality
- [ ] Voiceover levels consistent (-12dB to -6dB)
- [ ] Music/sound effects at appropriate level (lower than voice)
- [ ] No audio glitches or pops
- [ ] Trending audio properly credited where required
- [ ] Original audio licensed appropriately

### Content Accuracy
- [ ] Ingredient names spelled correctly
- [ ] Product claims verified against brand guidelines
- [ ] Any medical/skincare claims reviewed by compliance
- [ ] CTA buttons/links functional and correct
- [ ] Bio link updated if promoting specific URL

## Lessons Learned for Future Projects

1. **Batch 1 production day was too long** (10 hours). Reduced to 8 hours in Batch 2 with same output by optimizing setup time.

2. **Initial content pillars needed adjustment.** Brand Storytelling pillar underperformed — pivoted to Customer Education at week 6.

3. **Client onboarding took longer than expected.** Built a pre-launch onboarding checklist for future projects.

4. **Trending audio tracking needed daily attention.** Added this to client's weekly routine during training.

5. **Caption review process was bottleneck.** Streamlined to AI-generated with spot-check QA.
