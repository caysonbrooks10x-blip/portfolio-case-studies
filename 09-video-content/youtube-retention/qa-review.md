## Post-Project Lessons

1. **Pre-roll planning was underestimated.** Building a pre-roll "highlights reel" for each video adds 2 hours to production but dramatically improves hook retention.

2. **B-roll shortage in first month.** Built a batch-shoot protocol that captures 3 months of B-roll in single sessions.

3. **Chapter optimization took iteration.** First version had 6-8 chapters (too many). Settled on 4-5 per video for optimal key moment generation.

4. **Speed modulation required training.** Marcus initially resisted "choppy" editing. After seeing retention data, he became the system's biggest advocate.

5. **Thumbnail A/B testing crucial.** Set up automated testing early would have accelerated CTR improvements.
