# Visuals Plan: YouTube Editing & Retention System

## Apex Fitness | Video Visual Strategy

---

## Visual Identity System

### Brand Elements
- **Channel Branding:** Bold, energetic, high-contrast
- **Lower Thirds:** Branded template with channel colors
- **Intro/Outro:** Consistent animated sequence (3 seconds)
- **Thumbnail Style:** Bold text, subject in action, bright colors

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Electric blue (#0066FF)
- **Secondary:** Black (#000000)
- **Accent:** Bright orange (#FF6B00)
- **Text:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Background:** Dark (#1A1A1A) for educational segments

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Montserrat Black
- **Lower Thirds:** Montserrat Bold
- **On-Screen Text:** Montserrat SemiBold
- **Thumbnails:** Aller Black (or similar bold sans)

---

## Video Format Specifications

### Standard Video Structure (12:00)
1. Hook (0:00-0:30) — Pre-roll + Pattern Interrupt + Promise
2. Context (0:30-1:30) — Brief setup
3. Main Content (1:30-8:00) — Core teaching with chapters
4. Quick Wins (8:00-9:00) — Fast actionable takeaways
5. Full Routine (9:00-11:00) — Complete workout demo
6. Outro (11:00-12:00) — Summary + CTA + End screen

### Aspect Ratio
- **Primary:** 16:9 (1920×1080)
- **Mobile optimized:** Text positioned in upper 2/3 of frame
- **End screens:** 16:9 with safe zones for all devices

---

## Hook Visual Strategy

### Pre-Roll Teaser (0:00-0:05)
- Show the most intense/dramatic moment from the video
- Jump cut between 3 quick clips
- Text overlay: Single provocative word or phrase
- Music: High-energy beat (3-4 second clip)

### Pattern Interrupt (0:05-0:15)
- Sudden visual change (different angle, zoom, B-roll)
- Contradictory statement or surprising fact
- Facial expression change if on-camera

### Promise Statement (0:15-0:30)
- Subject directly to camera
- Clear value proposition
- "In this video, you'll learn..."
- Chapter markers previewed

---

## Pacing Visual Guidelines

### Cut Frequency Targets
- **Talking head segments:** 12-18 cuts per minute
- **B-roll sequences:** 8-12 cuts per minute
- **Workout demonstrations:** 6-10 cuts per minute
- **Educational graphics:** 4-6 cuts per minute

### Speed Modulation
- **Normal delivery:** 1.0x speed
- **Emphasis moments:** 0.75x speed (key points)
- **Mundane/transition:** 1.5x speed
- **B-roll filler:** 1.25x speed
- **Dramatic pauses:** 0.5x on cut-away

### Jump Cut Patterns
- **Statement completion:** Cut mid-sentence for emphasis
- **Talking head variety:** Head position shifts every 2-3 sentences
- **Micro-expressions:** Quick zoom on reaction faces

---

## B-Roll Integration System

### B-Roll Types Required Per Video
- **Exercise form shots:** 15+ clips (multiple angles)
- **Gym environment:** 10+ establishing shots
- **Equipment close-ups:** 5+ product shots
- **Progress/results:** 3+ transformation visuals
- **Reaction shots:** 3+ subject expressions
- **Timer/clock inserts:** 2+ for workout segments

### B-Roll Placement Rules
- Insert B-roll every 45-60 seconds during talking segments
- Always cut to B-roll on topic transitions
- Show the exercise BEFORE explaining it
- Use B-roll to illustrate statistics or claims

### B-Roll Shot List Template
```
□ Wide establishing shot (3 seconds)
□ Medium subject shot (5 seconds)
□ Close-up hand positioning (3 seconds)
□ Close-up foot positioning (3 seconds)
□ Equipment close-up (2 seconds)
□ Subject face/reaction (2 seconds)
□ Gym environment (3 seconds)
□ Timer/clock (2 seconds)
□ Outcome/result shot (3 seconds)
```

## Chapter Visual System

### Chapter Card Design
- **Size:** 1920×400px
- **Background:** Semi-transparent black with gradient
- **Text:** White Montserrat Bold, 48pt minimum
- **Duration:** 1.5 seconds
- **Animation:** Fade in (0.3s), hold (0.9s), fade out (0.3s)

### Chapter Timing Display
- Always show current chapter in lower third
- Duration indicator (how long until next chapter)
- Progress bar showing video completion

### Key Moments Integration
- YouTube auto-generates key moments from chapters
- Timestamp format: 0:00, 2:30, 5:00, etc.
- Minimum 4 chapters per video
- Chapters should be 2-4 minutes each

---

## Lower Third Design

### Standard Lower Third
- **Position:** Bottom-left, 100px from edges
- **Size:** 600×80px
- **Background:** Brand blue with 80% opacity
- **Text:** White Montserrat Bold, 28pt
- **Duration:** 3-4 seconds

### Context Lower Third
- **Position:** Bottom-center
- **Use:** Reinforce key points
- **Style:** Slightly smaller, more subtle

---

## Text Overlay Guidelines

### Emphasis Text
- **Trigger words:** "Important," "Key," "Critical," "Warning"
- **Style:** Yellow (#FFD700) with black outline
- **Animation:** Pop-in, slight scale (1.1x), hold 1s, fade
- **Position:** Center frame, 20% from top

### Statistic Callouts
- **Large numbers:** 72pt+ Montserrat Black
- **Context text:** 28pt below
- **Background:** Semi-transparent brand color
- **Duration:** 4-5 seconds

### Graphic Inserts
- **Charts/diagrams:** Branded color scheme
- **Text boxes:** Rounded corners (16px radius)
- **Arrows/highlighters:** Brand accent color

---

## End Screen & Card Design

### End Screen Template (Last 20 seconds)
- **0:00-5:00:** Final value statement, subscribe reminder
- **5:00-10:00:** Best related video thumbnail (large)
- **10:00-15:00:** Playlist prompt
- **15:00-20:00:** Channel subscription button

### card Placement
- **Timing:** 5 seconds before end (or at natural break)
- **Style:** Branded template
- **Links:** Playlist > Individual video > Channel

### Subscribe Animation
- **Style:** Animated bell + subscribe button
- **Duration:** 5 seconds
- **Position:** Center-right of end screen

---

## Thumbnail Design System

### Thumbnail Specs
- **Size:** 1280×720px
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **File size:** < 2MB
- **Text:** Bold, high contrast, 60pt+ for main text

### Thumbnail Elements
- **Subject:** Face with expression (strong emotion)
- **Action:** Mid-movement, dynamic pose
- **Text:** 1-3 words, maximum contrast
- **Background:** High contrast, not busy
- **Branding:** Small channel logo (bottom-right)

### A/B Testing Approach
- Create 2-3 thumbnails per video
- Test with TubeBuddy or vidIQ
- Rotate every 30 days if views plateau

---

## Color Grading Guidelines

### Look & Feel
- **Tone:** Clean, high-contrast, energetic
- **Skin tones:** Warm, natural, healthy glow
- **Whites:** Clean, not blown out
- **Blacks:** Deep, not crushed

### LUT (Look-Up Table)
- Custom LUT for Apex Fitness brand
- Applied consistently across all videos
- Adjusted for different lighting conditions

### Platform Variations
- **YouTube:** Slightly saturated, warm tones
- **YouTube Shorts:** Higher contrast, punchier
- **Embed/website:** Neutral, web-optimized

## Equipment Setup

### Primary Camera Setup
- **Camera:** Sony A7 IV (or iPhone 14 Pro as backup)
- **Lens:** 24-70mm f/2.8
- **Lighting:** Key light (softbox), fill light (ring light), hair light (accent)
- **Background:** Branded backdrop or clean gym wall

### Audio Setup
- **Primary:** Shure SM7B + GoXLR mixer
- **Backup:** Rode NT-USB+
- **Ambient:** LaValier for workout demos
- **Levels:** -12dB to -6dB, no clipping

### Editing Workstation
- **CPU:** Intel i7 or AMD Ryzen 7+
- **GPU:** NVIDIA RTX 3060+ (for DaVinci Resolve acceleration)
- **RAM:** 32GB minimum
- **Storage:** 2TB SSD for projects
