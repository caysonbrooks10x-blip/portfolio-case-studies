### The Problem

YouTube's algorithm rewards one thing above all else: **retention**.

If viewers watch your videos all the way through, YouTube says "this content is good — show it to more people." If they click away, YouTube says "this content isn't engaging — stop recommending it."

Most creators focus on thumbnails and titles. Those matter. But if your video can't hold attention once viewers click, you're fighting gravity with a paper airplane.
