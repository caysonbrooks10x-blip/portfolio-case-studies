# Visuals Plan: Podcast Repurposing Workflow

## Growth Minds Podcast | Content Visual Strategy

---

## Visual Identity System

### Brand Elements
- **Podcast Cover Art:** Consistent across all platforms
- **Intro/Outro Animation:** 3-second branded sequence
- **Lower Thirds:** Host name, guest name, key titles
- **Logo Watermark:** Subtle, bottom-right, 10% opacity

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep navy (#1A1A2E)
- **Secondary:** Bright coral (#FF6B6B)
- **Accent:** Clean white (#FFFFFF)
- **Text:** Light gray (#E8E8E8)
- **Highlights:** Gold (#FFD93D)

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Montserrat Bold, 48pt+
- **Body Text:** Inter Regular, 24pt
- **Quote Attribution:** Inter Italic, 20pt
- **Social Captions:** Inter Medium, 18pt

---

## Format Specifications

### YouTube Long-Form Video
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080 (16:9)
- **Duration:** 45-90 minutes
- **Thumbnail:** Bold text, guest photo, high contrast
- **Chapters:** Every 5-8 minutes
- **Description:** 300+ words, timestamps, links

### YouTube Shorts
- **Resolution:** 1080×1920 (9:16)
- **Duration:** 30-60 seconds
- **Thumbnail:** First frame text, attention-grabbing
- **Captions:** Required (auto-generated + manual review)
- **Hashtags:** #shorts + 2-3 niche tags

### Audiograms
- **Resolution:** 1080×1080 (1:1) or 1080×1350 (4:5)
- **Duration:** 30-60 seconds
- **Visual:** Waveform + static image/video background
- **Captions:** Dynamic, synced to audio
- **CTA:** Subscribe or link in bio

### Quote Graphics
- **Resolution:** 1080×1080 (1:1) for Instagram, 1200×628 for LinkedIn
- **Format:** Single quote + attribution
- **Background:** Brand colors or lifestyle image
- **Text:** Bold, high contrast, readable at small sizes
- **Shareable:** Branded but not cluttered

### LinkedIn Carousels
- **Resolution:** 1080×1080 (1:1) per slide
- **Slides:** 5-10 slides per carousel
- **Format:** Title slide, content slides, CTA slide
- **Design:** Consistent brand treatment
- **Downloadable:** PDF export option

## Audiogram Visual Templates

### Template 1: Waveform + Guest Photo
- **Background:** Guest headshot (centered or left)
- **Overlay:** Animated waveform (bottom 40%)
- **Text:** Episode title, guest name, key insight
- **Duration:** 30-45 seconds

### Template 2: Waveform + Brand Background
- **Background:** Brand gradient or pattern
- **Waveform:** Centered, animated, brand accent color
- **Text:** Quote highlight, podcast name
- **Duration:** 30-60 seconds

### Template 3: Talking Head Clip
- **Source:** Riverside video recording
- **Edit:** Best 30-45 second clip selected
- **Captions:** Dynamic, branded style
- **Overlay:** Waveform at bottom

### Template 4: Text + Waveform
- **Background:** Full-screen text quote
- **Waveform:** Small, bottom corner
- **Design:** Minimal, high contrast
- **Duration:** 30 seconds

## LinkedIn Carousel Structure

### Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Headline:** Episode topic or insight theme
- **Subhead:** "X lessons from [guest/experience]"
- **Podcast Branding:** Logo, name
- **Design:** High contrast, professional

### Slides 2-8: Content Slides
- **One insight per slide**
- **Bold number or keyword**
- **2-3 sentences explaining
- **Visual:** Icon, photo, or graphic
- **Consistent layout template**

### Final Slide: CTA
- **Podcast name and logo**
- **"Listen to the full episode"**
- **Link or QR code**
- **Social handles**

---

## YouTube Shorts Visual Guidelines

### Hook Frame (First 2 Seconds)
- **Text:** Bold hook statement, upper 40% of frame
- **Visual:** Subject's expression, reaction, or key visual
- **No:** Blank screen, logo only, silence

### Throughout Clip
- **Dynamic captions:** Every word spoken
- **Cut every 3-5 seconds:** B-roll, zoom, reaction
- **High energy:** Speed ramping where appropriate
- **Brand watermark:** Small, corner

### Ending Frame
- **CTA:** "Follow for more" or "Link in bio"
- **Subscribe prompt:** Bell icon or text
- **Consistent with channel branding**

## Waveform Style Guide

### Audiogram Waveforms
- **Color:** Brand accent (coral #FF6B6B)
- **Style:** Filled bars, rounded edges
- **Animation:** Left-to-right sweep
- **Height:** 30-40% of frame
- **Background:** Dark or branded color

### Tech Specs
- **Software:** Descript waveform generator
- **Resolution:** Match video format
- **File type:** MP4 or GIF
- **Quality:** 1080p minimum

## Thumbnail Design System

### YouTube Long-Form
- **Format:** 1280×720px
- **Subject:** Guest photo or host
- **Text:** Bold, max 5 words
- **Expression:** Strong emotion (surprise, intensity)
- **Colors:** High contrast, bright
- **Branding:** Small logo, bottom-right

### YouTube Shorts
- **Format:** 1080×1920px (used for preview)
- **First frame text:** Key hook
- **Visual:** Subject reaction
- **Style:** Bold, fast, energetic
- **Consistent:** With channel aesthetic

## Production Day Visual Checklist

### Pre-Production
- [ ] Riverside recording tested
- [ ] Lighting: 3-point setup (if video)
- [ ] Audio levels checked
- [ ] Background clean and branded
- [ ] Guest release forms signed

### During Recording
- [ ] Multiple camera angles captured
- [ ] B-roll opportunities noted
- [ ] Best moments marked for clips
- [ ] Audio quality consistent

### Post-Production
- [ ] Transcription complete (Otter.ai)
- [ ] Highlight moments identified
- [ ] Clips extracted and edited (CapCut)
- [ ] Audiograms created (Descript)
- [ ] Quote graphics designed (Canva)
- [ ] Carousel assembled (Canva)
- [ ] Newsletter formatted (ConvertKit)
- [ ] All content scheduled (Buffer)

## Equipment List

### Recording (Riverside-Optimized)
- **Computers:** Host + guest each on own device
- **Microphones:** Built-in or USB (Riverside records locally)
- **Camera:** Built-in webcam or external
- **Lighting:** Ring light or desk lamp
- **Headphones:** To prevent audio bleed

### Post-Production
- **Editing:** Descript, CapCut, Canva
- **Scheduling:** Buffer, Notion
- **Transcription:** Otter.ai (automated)
- **Email:** ConvertKit
- **Podcast Hosting:** Buzzsprout
