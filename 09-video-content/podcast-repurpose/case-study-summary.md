# Podcast Repurposing Workflow — Case Study Summary

## Growth Minds Podcast | Content Multiplier System

**Industry:** Business Podcast / Content Creator
**Timeline:** 8 weeks
**Services:** Repurposing workflow design, automation implementation, content strategy

---

## The Challenge
Growth Minds Podcast was spending 15-20 hours per episode producing just 2-3 content pieces. Sarah Chen had excellent content trapped in 90-minute audio files — insights that could reach millions if properly distributed. She needed a system to multiply her content without multiplying her workload.

## What We Built
A complete podcast repurposing "content multiplier" system featuring:
- Full workflow architecture for systematic repurposing
- Riverside + Descript + Otter.ai integration for automated transcription
- YouTube Shorts clip extraction (3-5 per episode)
- Audiogram creation workflow (4-6 per episode)
- Quote graphic templates (3-5 per episode)
- LinkedIn carousel production (1-2 per episode)
- Newsletter and thread compilation
- Evergreen content banking system
- Batch scheduling infrastructure

## Results at 8 Weeks
- **Content Pieces/Episode:** 2-3 → 18-24 (+700%)
- **Production Time:** 15-20 hours → 4.5 hours (-77%)
- **YouTube Subscribers:** 8,400 → 34,200 (+307%)
- **Podcast Downloads:** 18,400 → 42,800/month (+133%)
- **Newsletter Subscribers:** 2,800 → 11,400 (+307%)
- **LinkedIn Followers:** 4,200 → 18,600 (+343%)
- **Revenue:** $3,200 → $14,800/month (+363%)

## Tools
Riverside, Descript, Buzzsprout, Canva, Notion, CapCut, Otter.ai, Buffer, ConvertKit

## Outcome
Client operates repurposing workflow independently. Content bank of 140+ evergreen pieces built. 8-week production time reduced 77%. System documented and running autonomously.
