## Problem Statement

Podcasting is one of the most time-intensive content formats. The average episode requires:
- 2-3 hours of guest research and prep
- 1-2 hours of recording
- 3-4 hours of editing
- 1 hour of show notes and distribution

Total: 15-20 hours per episode. And yet, most of that content value evaporates after the initial release. The insights from a 90-minute conversation reach a fraction of the people who could benefit from them.

The brands and creators winning in 2024 aren't producing more content. They're multiplying their existing content across platforms.
