## Questions Prospects Ask

**"What's the minimum podcast frequency for this to work?"**
Even one episode per month justifies the system. With 12 episodes, you'd have 200+ content pieces by year end. But it scales — weekly episodes produce 900+ content pieces annually.

**"What if my episodes are too long?"**
Longer episodes actually work better — more content to extract. 90-minute episodes are ideal. We identify the best moments to clip regardless of length.

**"Do I need to record video?"**
Not necessarily. We use Riverside for high-quality audio, but audiograms and quote graphics don't require video. YouTube Long-Form works with audio + static image if video isn't recorded.

**"Can this work with my existing editing software?"**
Yes — we integrate with whatever you're already using. The workflow adds repurposing on top of your existing production, not replacing it.
