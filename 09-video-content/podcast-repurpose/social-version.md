## Post 6: The Audiogram Strategy
Audiograms (waveform + video clip) outperform text-only posts by 3x on LinkedIn and Twitter.
Why?
→ Audio triggers curiosity
→ Visual waveform suggests "this is worth listening to"
→ Native video gets algorithmic priority

Every podcast episode should produce 4-6 audiograms.
