## Post-Project Lessons

1. **AI transcription needs spot-checking.** Otter.ai at 95% accuracy still misses names and technical terms. Built in manual review for key moments.

2. **Shorts clip selection is skill.** AI suggestions are helpful but human curation of "viral-worthy" moments is critical. Invested more in Sarah's clip selection training.

3. **Evergreen content strategy saved the system.** By week 4, Sarah had a busy week and couldn't repurpose. The content bank filled the gap. Built evergreen rotation as core feature.

4. **LinkedIn carousels were unexpected win.** Initially focused on Shorts. Carousels drove disproportionate lead gen. Added as primary format at week 3.

5. **Batch scheduling was harder than expected.** Scheduling 18 pieces per episode across 7 platforms required Buffer workflow optimization. Documented full scheduling SOP.
