## 1. Understanding Your Situation

CoreSync has a 34,000-contact email list built over 8 years, but it's being used as a broadcast channel — one newsletter a month to everyone, with no segmentation, no automation, and no lifecycle thinking. You're sending the same content to new leads, trial users, paying customers, and churned users. The result: low engagement, high unsubscribes, and revenue that's a fraction of what it could be.

The problem isn't your list size. It's that you're not treating email as the system it should be.

---

## 2. Our Proposed Solution

A 90-day email automation and lifecycle marketing overhaul in three phases:

### Phase 1: Audit & Architecture (Days 1-30)
- Full email program audit (list quality, engagement, deliverability, segmentation)
- Competitive analysis of 5 B2B SaaS email programs
- Lifecycle stage definitions (7 stages from Subscriber to Champion/Churned)
- Segment architecture (engagement tiers + lifecycle stages = 14 segments)
- Automation map documenting all trigger-based sequences

**Deliverables:** Audit report, lifecycle architecture document, segment definitions, automation map

### Phase 2: List Work & Automation Build (Days 20-60)
- List re-engagement campaign (4-week sequence for inactive contacts)
- Preference center build and deployment
- List cleaning (suppression, validation, hygiene)
- Welcome Series (3 emails)
- Lead Nurture Sequence (5 emails)
- Trial Activation Sequence (4 emails)
- Customer Onboarding Sequence (6 emails)

**Deliverables:** 6 automated email sequences, preference center, cleaned segment list

### Phase 3: Optimization & Attribution (Days 60-90)
- Re-engagement and win-back sequences built
- Multi-touch attribution implementation
- Broadcast email strategy (2x/month)
- A/B testing framework
- Revenue reporting dashboard
- Internal team handoff documentation

**Deliverables:** Full attribution model, optimization playbook, reporting dashboard
