Case study: How we took a B2B SaaS company's email revenue from $18,400 to $57,000 per month in 90 days.

That's a 3.1x increase.

From the same 34,000-contact list.

Here's what we did.

**The Situation:**
CoreSync Solutions had a 34,000-contact email list.
Built over 8 years.
One newsletter a month.
Same content to everyone — new leads, trial users, customers, churned users.

They were treating email like a bulletin board, not a revenue system.

**The Problem:**
Open rate: 18%
Click rate: 2.1%
Unsubscribe rate: 0.8% (too high)
Revenue attributed to email: $18,400/month

The math wasn't working. But the bigger problem: they had no visibility into what email was ACTUALLY worth.

**The Fix:**

1️⃣ LIFECYCLE ARCHITECTURE
We defined 7 lifecycle stages:
Subscriber → Lead → MQL → Trial → Customer → Champion → Churned

Everyone was getting the same email because they had no model for where contacts were in their journey.

2️⃣ SEGMENTATION
Built 14 segments based on:
- Engagement tier (highly, moderately, low, inactive)
- Lifecycle stage (subscriber, lead, trial, customer)

No more broadcasting to everyone.

3️⃣ LIST RE-ENGAGEMENT
Launched a 4-week re-engagement campaign to 8,200 inactive contacts.
Built a preference center.
Non-responders suppressed.

List went from 34K to 28K.
Active engagement went up 50%.

4️⃣ AUTOMATIONS
Built 6 behavioral email sequences:

• Welcome Series (3 emails) — New subscriber onboarding
• Lead Nurture (5 emails) — Content engagement sequence
• Trial Activation (4 emails) — Trial-to-paid conversion
• Customer Onboarding (6 emails) — Time-based post-purchase
• Win-Back (3 emails) — Re-engagement for inactive
• Re-Engagement (4 emails) — Churned user recovery

Every email triggered by behavior. Not schedule.

5️⃣ MULTI-TOUCH ATTRIBUTION
Here's the kicker: email wasn't driving $18,400/month.
It was driving $57,000/month.

Last-click attribution was hiding 3x of email's value.

Once we implemented multi-touch attribution, the actual revenue contribution was clear.

**The Results:**
• Email Revenue: $18,400 → $57,000/month (+210%)
• Open Rate: 18% → 34% (+89%)
• Click Rate: 2.1% → 4.8% (+129%)
• Unsubscribe Rate: 0.8% → 0.3% (-64%)
• Active List: 12,400 → 18,600 (+50%)
• Trial Activation Rate: 14% → 23% (+64%)

**The insight:**
Email is a system, not a newsletter.

Broadcasting to your entire list is the mistake. Behavioral triggers. Lifecycle segmentation. Multi-touch attribution.

That's what turns a contact list into a revenue engine.
