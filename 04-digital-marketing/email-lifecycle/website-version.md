## Overview

CoreSync Solutions is a B2B SaaS company offering project management and collaboration software. They had accumulated 34,000 contacts over 8 years but treated email as a simple broadcast channel — one newsletter a month to their entire list. No segmentation, no automation, no lifecycle thinking. The engagement transformed email from a neglected "nice-to-have" into CoreSync's highest-ROI marketing channel, with revenue growing 3.1x through behavioral automations and proper attribution.

## Process

We started with a comprehensive audit of their existing email program to understand list quality, engagement patterns, and segmentation gaps. We designed a 7-stage lifecycle architecture (Subscriber → Lead → MQL → Trial → Customer → Champion → Churned) and built 14 segments based on engagement and lifecycle stage. We cleaned the list through a re-engagement campaign, suppressed inactive contacts, and built 6 automated email sequences: Welcome Series, Lead Nurture, Trial Activation, Customer Onboarding, Win-Back, and Re-Engagement. We implemented multi-touch attribution to properly credit email's revenue influence across the customer journey.

## Results

The 90-day engagement delivered:
- **3.1x increase** in email-attributed revenue ($18.4K → $57K/month)
- **89% improvement** in open rates (18% → 34%)
- **129% improvement** in click rates (2.1% → 4.8%)
- **64% reduction** in unsubscribes (0.8% → 0.3%)
- **50% growth** in active list size
- **64% improvement** in trial activation rate
