# Deliverables — Email Automation + Lifecycle Marketing

## Phase 1: Audit & Architecture (Days 1-30)

### Audit Deliverables
- [ ] Full email program audit (list quality, engagement, deliverability)
- [ ] Contact segmentation analysis (engagement tiers, source breakdown)
- [ ] Campaign performance review (open rates, click rates, unsubscribes, revenue)
- [ ] Competitive analysis (5 B2B SaaS email programs benchmarked)
- [ ] Email deliverability assessment (DNS, SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- [ ] Attribution model review (current methodology and gaps)

### Strategic Deliverables
- [ ] Lifecycle stage definitions (7 stages: Subscriber → Champion/Churned)
- [ ] 14-segment architecture document
- [ ] Automation map (all trigger-based sequences)
- [ ] Content gap analysis
- [ ] Recommended email platform configuration

## Phase 2: List Work & Automation Build (Days 20-60)

### List Re-Engagement Deliverables
- [ ] Re-engagement campaign strategy (4 emails over 4 weeks)
- [ ] Preference center build and deployment
- [ ] List cleaning (suppression, validation, hygiene)
- [ ] Inactive contact segmentation
- [ ] Non-responder suppression protocol

### Automation Sequence Deliverables

**Welcome Series (3 emails):**
- [ ] Email 1: Welcome + brand introduction
- [ ] Email 2: Top resources / popular content
- [ ] Email 3: Trial offer with use case
- [ ] Trigger: New subscriber signup
- [ ] A/B subject line testing

**Lead Nurture Sequence (5 emails):**
- [ ] Email 1: Content based on signup source
- [ ] Email 2: Educational content (topic based on interest)
- [ ] Email 3: Case study from similar company
- [ ] Email 4: Integration/feature spotlight
- [ ] Email 5: Demo offer or trial CTA
- [ ] Trigger: Engaged subscriber (2+ opens, no trial)
- [ ] Progressive profiling embedded

**Trial Activation Sequence (4 emails):**
- [ ] Email 1: Day 0 — Trial activation checklist
- [ ] Email 2: Day 3 — Feature spotlight (high-usage features)
- [ ] Email 3: Day 7 — Success story / case study
- [ ] Email 4: Day 12 — Limited-time trial extension
- [ ] Trigger: Trial signup
- [ ] Urgency and conversion optimization

**Customer Onboarding Sequence (6 emails):**
- [ ] Email 1: Week 1 — Getting started checklist
- [ ] Email 2: Week 2 — Advanced feature tutorial
- [ ] Email 3: Week 4 — Integration walkthrough
- [ ] Email 4: Week 6 — ROI tracking tips
- [ ] Email 5: Week 8 — Community invitation
- [ ] Email 6: Week 10 — Expansion opportunity
- [ ] Trigger: First purchase
- [ ] Time-based delays between emails

**Win-Back Sequence (3 emails):**
- [ ] Email 1: "We miss you" + new feature highlight
- [ ] Email 2: Case study from similar company
- [ ] Email 3: Exclusive discount offer
- [ ] Trigger: 60+ days inactive (customer)
- [ ] Final sunset or preference update

**Re-Engagement Sequence (4 emails):**
- [ ] Email 1: "Still interested?" survey
- [ ] Email 2: Best content from the past quarter
- [ ] Email 3: Special offer for reactivation
- [ ] Email 4: Final unsubscribe prompt
- [ ] Trigger: 90+ days inactive (subscriber/lead)

### Template Deliverables
- [ ] 12 email templates designed (welcome, nurture, promo, onboarding, etc.)
- [ ] Mobile-responsive design
- [ ] Consistent branding across all templates
- [ ] CTA button designs for each template type
