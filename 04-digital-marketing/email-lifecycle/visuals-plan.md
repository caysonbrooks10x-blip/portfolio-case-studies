## Recommended Visual Style
- **Color palette:** Professional blues and greens (trust, growth, B2B)
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, Roboto)
- **Chart style:** Minimal, data-forward, professional SaaS aesthetic
- **Icons:** Lucid or Font Awesome for lifecycle stages
- **Overall feel:** Enterprise B2B, clean, trustworthy
