# Email Automation + Lifecycle Marketing — Sales Talking Points

## Opening Hook
"If I told you we grew a B2B SaaS company's email revenue from $18,400 to $57,000 per month in 90 days — from the same 34,000-contact list — would that be worth a conversation?"

## Problem Identification
- 34,000-contact list but being used as a broadcast channel (one newsletter to everyone)
- No segmentation — new leads, trial users, customers, churned users all got same content
- Zero automation — no welcome series, no lead nurture, no trial activation, no onboarding
- Low engagement: 18% open rate, 2.1% click rate, 0.8% unsubscribe rate
- Broken attribution: last-click showed $18,400 but multi-touch revealed $57,000

## Solution Highlights
- 7-stage lifecycle architecture (Subscriber → Champion/Churned)
- 14 contact segments based on engagement tier + lifecycle stage
- List re-engagement campaign + preference center
- 6 behavioral email automations (Welcome, Lead Nurture, Trial Activation, Onboarding, Win-Back, Re-Engagement)
- Multi-touch attribution implementation
- Broadcast email strategy (2x/month, segmented)

## Key Metrics to Quote
- Email Revenue: $18,400 → $57,000/month (3.1x)
- Open Rate: 18% → 34% (+89%)
- Click Rate: 2.1% → 4.8% (+129%)
- Unsubscribe Rate: 0.8% → 0.3% (-64%)
- Active List: 12,400 → 18,600 (+50%)
- Trial Activation Rate: 14% → 23% (+64%)
- Email-Influenced Pipeline: $92K → $214K/month
