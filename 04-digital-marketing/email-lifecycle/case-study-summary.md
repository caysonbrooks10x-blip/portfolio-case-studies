# CoreSync Solutions — Email Automation + Lifecycle Marketing
**90-Day Engagement | Email Revenue 3.1x | Open Rates +89%**

## The Challenge
CoreSync was treating email as a monthly newsletter — same content to their entire 34K list with no segmentation, no automation, and no lifecycle thinking. Revenue attribution showed $18,400/month.

## What We Did
- Comprehensive email audit (list quality, engagement, segmentation gaps)
- Designed 7-stage lifecycle architecture (Subscriber → Champion/Churned)
- Built 6 automated email sequences (Welcome, Lead Nurture, Trial Activation, Customer Onboarding, Win-Back, Re-Engagement)
- Implemented multi-touch attribution revealing true email revenue influence
- List re-engagement campaign and preference center
- 14 contact segments based on engagement and lifecycle stage

## The Results
| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Email Revenue (monthly) | $18,400 | $57,000 |
| Open Rate | 18% | 34% |
| Click Rate | 2.1% | 4.8% |
| Unsubscribe Rate | 0.8% | 0.3% |
| Active List Size | 12,400 | 18,600 |
| Trial Activation Rate | 14% | 23% |
| Email-Influenced Pipeline | $92K/mo | $214K/mo |

**Revenue Impact:** $684,000 annual email revenue run-rate (up from $220,800).

## Tools Used
Klaviyo, HubSpot, GA4, OptinMonster, Typeform, ZeroBounce, Looker Studio, Google Postmaster
