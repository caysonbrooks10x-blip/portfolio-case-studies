# Social Media Performance Marketing System

## Elevate Skincare — DTC Beauty / Premium Skincare

**The Ask:** Elevate had 28K followers across Instagram and Facebook but couldn't attribute meaningful revenue to social. They needed a performance system, not just more content.

**The Approach:** Content pillars framework → Batch creation workflow → Meta Pixel + CAPI attribution → Paid amplification of organic winners → UGC library + influencer integration → Retargeting sequences

**The Results:**
- Social Revenue: $94K → $386K annually (4.1x)
- CAC: $48 → $22 (-54%)
- ROAS: 0.9x → 3.8x
- Instagram Followers: 24K → 38.5K (+60%)
- Engagement Rate: 1.8% → 4.7%
- UGC Library: 0 → 200+ posts

**Tools:** Meta Business Suite, Instagram, Facebook, GA4, Klaviyo, Meta Ads Manager, Canva, CapCut, AspireIQ, Looker Studio

**Timeline:** 6 Months

**Client:** Aria Chen, Founder & CEO

---

## Overview

Elevate Skincare is a direct-to-consumer premium skincare brand. They had built a decent following on social but were treating it as a brand awareness experiment rather than a revenue channel. No performance framework, no paid amplification, no retargeting, no attribution. This engagement transformed social into their highest-ROI marketing channel.

## Process

We started with a full audit of their existing social performance to understand what content was actually working. Then we built a content pillars framework aligned to the customer journey — education, product showcases, social proof, brand story, and promotions. We implemented batch content creation so one person could produce a week's worth of content in a single session. We set up Meta Pixel and Conversions API for proper attribution, then launched paid amplification campaigns targeting people who'd engaged with organic content. We built a UGC library and influencer network with trackable codes so every partnership was measurable.

## Results

The 6-month engagement delivered:
- **4.1x increase** in social-attributed revenue ($94K → $386K annually)
- **$22 CAC** from social (vs. $48 before)
- **3.8x ROAS** on paid social spend
- **200+ UGC posts** approved for ad creative use
- **15 micro-influencer partnerships** with trackable ROI
