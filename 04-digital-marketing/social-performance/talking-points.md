# Social Media Performance Marketing — Sales Talking Points

## Opening Hook
"If I told you we grew a DTC skincare brand's social revenue from $94K to $386K in 6 months — a 4.1x increase — and reduced their CAC from $48 to $22, what would that be worth to you?"

## Problem Identification
- 28K followers across Instagram and Facebook but couldn't attribute meaningful revenue
- No content strategy — posting ad-hoc without knowing what converted
- No paid amplification — ignoring engaged audiences
- No retargeting system — leaving money on the table
- Broken attribution — social credited with only 8% of influence vs. actual 24%

## Solution Highlights
- Content pillars framework (education, product, social proof, brand, promotions)
- Batch creation workflow (4 hours = 1 week of content)
- Meta Pixel + CAPI implementation for proper attribution
- Paid amplification of high-performing organic content
- Retargeting sequences (engaged → website visitors → cart abandoners)
- UGC content library (200+ approved customer posts)
- 15 micro-influencer partnerships with trackable codes

## Key Metrics to Quote
- Social Revenue: $94K → $386K annually (+311%)
- CAC: $48 → $22 (-54%)
- ROAS: 0.9x → 3.8x (+322%)
- Instagram Followers: 24K → 38.5K (+60%)
- Engagement Rate: 1.8% → 4.7% (+161%)
- UGC Library: 200+ posts
- Attribution improvement: 8% → 24% of revenue

## The Methodology Story
"We didn't just 'post more.' We built a system where every piece of content had a job — education, consideration, or conversion. We set up proper tracking so we knew what content actually drove purchases. Then we amplified the winners with paid, built a retargeting sequence for engaged audiences, and created a UGC library that keeps our ad creatives fresh without constant production. The result is a compounding social machine."

## Client Validation Quote
"Social is our highest-ROI marketing channel now. We know exactly what we're doing every week."

## Objection Pre-Handling
- "We're already posting on social" → "Posting and having a system that converts are different things. We analyze what's actually working and amplify it."
- "Social doesn't convert for our brand" → "Elevate said the same thing. After proper attribution, social was driving 24% of revenue — they just couldn't see it before."
- "We tried influencers and it didn't work" → "Most influencer programs fail because there's no tracking. Every partnership we build has a unique code and UTM links. You'll know exactly what each post is worth."
