# Elevate Skincare — Social Media Performance Marketing
**6-Month Engagement | Social Revenue 4.1x | CAC $48 → $22**

## The Challenge
Elevate Skincare had 28K followers but couldn't attribute revenue to social. They were posting ad-hoc with no performance framework, no paid amplification, and no retargeting system.

## What We Did
- Built content pillars framework aligned to customer journey (Education, Product, Social Proof, Brand, Promotions)
- Established consistent 5 posts/week cadence with batch creation workflow
- Implemented Meta Pixel and CAPI for proper social attribution
- Launched paid amplification campaigns for high-performing organic content
- Built UGC content library of 200+ approved posts for ad creative
- Established 15-influencer partnership network with trackable discount codes

## The Results
| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Social Revenue (annual) | $94,000 | $386,000 |
| Social-Attributed Orders | 1,340 | 5,510 |
| Customer Acquisition Cost | $48 | $22 |
| ROAS | 0.9x | 3.8x |
| Instagram Followers | 24,000 | 38,500 |
| Engagement Rate (IG) | 1.8% | 4.7% |
| UGC Content Library | 0 | 200+ |

**Revenue Impact:** $386K annual social revenue, 4.1x growth in 6 months.

## Tools Used
Meta Business Suite, Instagram, Facebook, GA4, Klaviyo, Meta Ads Manager, Canva, CapCut, AspireIQ, Looker Studio
