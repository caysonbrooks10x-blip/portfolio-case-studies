Case study: How we took a DTC skincare brand's social revenue from $94K to $386K in 6 months.

That's a 4.1x increase.

Here's what we did.

**The Situation:**
Elevate Skincare had 28K followers across Instagram and Facebook.
They were posting content. Getting likes. Making no money.

No attribution. No paid amplification. No retargeting.
Just vibes and hope.

**The Problem (not what you think):**
It wasn't that social didn't work.
It was that they had no SYSTEM.

They were making content without knowing what content drove purchases.
Posting sporadically because there was no workflow.
Ignoring the biggest opportunity in social: retargeting engaged audiences.

**The Fix:**

1️⃣ CONTENT PILLARS FRAMEWORK
We defined what content served what purpose:
- 30% Education (build trust)
- 25% Product Showcases (drive consideration)
- 25% Social Proof (reduce purchase anxiety)
- 10% Brand Story (emotional connection)
- 10% Promotions (convert momentum)

2️⃣ BATCH CREATION WORKFLOW
Instead of creating content daily (chaos), we built a system:
1 person. 4 hours. All content for the next week.
Templates in Canva. Queue in Later/Buffer.
Sustainable and consistent.

3️⃣ PROPER ATTRIBUTION
Installed Meta Pixel and Conversions API.
UTM-tagged every link.
Social's contribution jumped from 8% to 24% of revenue once properly measured.

4️⃣ PAID AMPLIFICATION
High-performing organic posts got boosted to targeted audiences.
Engagement campaigns → Traffic campaigns → Retargeting.
We turned organic wins into scaled revenue.

5️⃣ UGC + INFLUENCERS
Built a branded hashtag program.
200+ customer posts approved for reposting.
15 micro-influencers with trackable discount codes.

UGC-style ads (real customers) consistently outperformed polished brand content.

**The Results:**
• Social Revenue: $94K → $386K/year (+311%)
• CAC: $48 → $22 (-54%)
• ROAS: 0.9x → 3.8x
• Instagram Followers: 24K → 38.5K (+60%)
• Engagement Rate: 1.8% → 4.7%
• UGC Library: 0 → 200+ posts

**The insight:**
Social isn't a brand awareness experiment.
It's a performance channel.

But only if you have the system to make it one.
