# Stratum Financial — Conversion Rate Optimization
**5-Month Engagement | Conversion Rate 2.1% → 6.5% | CAC $340 → $112**

## The Challenge
Stratum Financial was spending $100K/month on paid ads but converting only 2.1% of visitors to demo requests. Their $340 CAC was unsustainable for a $2,400 ACV product. No A/B testing, no systematic CRO.

## What We Did
- Comprehensive CRO audit (GA4, Hotjar, user interviews, competitive analysis)
- Built dedicated landing pages for 4 traffic segments (brand, competitor, product, SMB)
- Implemented A/B testing infrastructure (Optimizely)
- Ran 9 A/B tests over 5 months
- Simplified demo form from 7 fields to 3 fields
- Rewrote homepage hero with specific value proposition
- Added trust signals near CTAs (security badges, customer logos, testimonials)
- Built ROI calculator and exit-intent overlay

## The Results
| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Conversion Rate | 2.1% | 6.5% |
| Monthly Demo Requests | 252 | 780 |
| Effective CAC | $340 | $112 |
| Form Completion Rate | 33% | 68% |
| Bounce Rate | 73% | 51% |
| Landing Page CVR | 3.2% | 8.1% |

**Revenue Impact:** $1.8M incremental annual revenue at zero additional traffic cost.

## Tools Used
Google Analytics 4, Hotjar, Optimizely, Unbounce, Typeform, UserTesting, Looker Studio
