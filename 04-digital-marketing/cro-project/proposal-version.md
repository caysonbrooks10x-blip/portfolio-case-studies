# Conversion Rate Optimization Proposal
**Prepared for:** Stratum Financial
**Prepared by:** Cayson Brooks
**Date:** January 2026

---

## 1. Understanding Your Situation

Stratum Financial is spending $100,000/month on paid acquisition, driving approximately 12,000 website visitors. But only 2.1% are converting to demo requests — meaning you're paying $340 per qualified lead. For a product with a $2,400 ACV, that math is challenging.

The symptoms are clear: high traffic, low conversion, unsustainable CAC. But the causes are less obvious. Your homepage might be unclear. Your landing pages might not match your ad copy. Your forms might be asking for too much. Your trust signals might be buried.

Without systematic CRO, you're leaving money on the table with every visitor who doesn't convert.

---

## 2. Our Proposed Solution

A 5-month CRO program structured in four phases:

### Phase 1: Research & Audit (Weeks 1-4)
- GA4 deep-dive: traffic sources, conversion paths, drop-off analysis
- Hotjar installation and analysis: heatmaps, session recordings, rage clicks
- User interviews: 8 customer/prospect interviews to understand decision journey
- Survey deployment: 200+ responses on purchase motivators and objections
- Competitive teardown: landing pages of 6 direct competitors analyzed
- Test roadmap: prioritized list of A/B test opportunities

**Deliverables:** Research report, friction point map, test roadmap

### Phase 2: Quick Wins & Foundation (Weeks 5-8)
- CTA button copy optimization ("Get Started" → "Book Your Free Demo")
- Form field reduction (7 fields → 3 fields)
- Above-fold value proposition rewrite
- A/B testing infrastructure setup (Optimizely)
- Measurement plan documentation

**Deliverables:** First test launches, testing infrastructure operational

### Phase 3: High-Impact Testing (Weeks 9-16)
- Homepage hero restructuring (specific value prop)
- Trust signal placement optimization (near CTAs, on forms)
- Customer logo and testimonial placement
- Landing page restructuring (dedicated pages per traffic source)
- Exit-intent overlay development

**Deliverables:** 4-6 A/B tests running, initial test results

### Phase 4: Scale & Optimize (Weeks 17-24)
- Winning variants rolled out site-wide
- Dedicated landing pages built and tested
- ROI calculator build
- Social proof notification testing
- Final optimization recommendations

**Deliverables:** Full site optimization, ongoing testing framework
