## Recommended Visual Style
- **Color palette:** Professional fintech (blues, greens, whites, trust-inducing)
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, Roboto)
- **Chart style:** Minimal, data-forward, professional
- **Icons:** Lucid or Font Awesome for test results
- **Overall feel:** Enterprise fintech, trustworthy, data-driven
