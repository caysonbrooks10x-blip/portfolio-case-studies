# Conversion Rate Optimization Project

## Stratum Financial — Fintech / Business Banking

**The Ask:** Stratum was spending $100K/month on paid ads but converting only 2.1% of visitors. $340 CAC was unsustainable for a $2,400 ACV product.

**The Approach:** CRO audit (GA4, Hotjar, user interviews) → A/B testing infrastructure → Dedicated landing pages per traffic segment → Form simplification (7 → 3 fields) → Hero rewrite + trust signals → Exit-intent overlay + ROI calculator

**The Results:**
- Conversion Rate: 2.1% → 6.5% (3.1x improvement)
- Monthly Demo Requests: 252 → 780
- Effective CAC: $340 → $112 (-67%)
- Form Completion: 33% → 68%
- Bounce Rate: 73% → 51%

**Tools:** GA4, Hotjar, Optimizely, Unbounce, Typeform, UserTesting, Looker Studio

**Timeline:** 5 Months

**Client:** Rachel Torres, Head of Growth

---

## Overview

Stratum Financial is a fintech company offering business banking and financial management tools. They were spending $100K/month on paid acquisition but their 2.1% conversion rate meant they were paying $340 per demo request — unsustainable economics. The engagement applied systematic CRO research, A/B testing, and UX optimization to reduce their CAC to $112 without spending an additional dollar on traffic.

## Process

We began with 4 weeks of research: GA4 analysis to identify drop-off points, Hotjar session recordings to see where users were getting stuck, user interviews to understand the decision journey, and competitive analysis. We identified 9 high-impact test opportunities. Then we ran a rigorous A/B testing program using Optimizely, testing everything from CTA copy to landing page structure. We built dedicated landing pages for each traffic segment and simplified their demo form from 7 fields to 3. We rewrote the homepage hero with a specific value proposition and added trust signals near CTAs.

## Results

The 5-month engagement delivered:
- **3.1x conversion rate improvement** (2.1% → 6.5%)
- **67% CAC reduction** ($340 → $112)
- **528 additional demo requests/month** at zero extra traffic cost
- **$1.8M incremental annual revenue**
