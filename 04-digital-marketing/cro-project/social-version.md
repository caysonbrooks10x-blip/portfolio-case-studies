Case study: How we took a fintech company's conversion rate from 2.1% to 6.5% — a 3.1x improvement.

No extra traffic. No extra spend. Just better conversion.

Here's what we did.

**The Situation:**
Stratum Financial was spending $100,000/month on paid ads.
12,000 website visitors per month.
Only 2.1% converting to demo requests.
$340 CAC. Product ACV: $2,400.

The math didn't work.

They had high traffic. Low conversion. No testing infrastructure. No CRO program.

**The Problem (not what you think):**
It wasn't that their product wasn't good.
It wasn't that their traffic wasn't qualified.

It was that their website wasn't converting the traffic they already had.

**The Fix (5 months):**

1️⃣ RESEARCH FIRST
Before changing anything, we installed Hotjar and watched session recordings.
We interviewed 8 customers and lost prospects.
We ran a survey with 240 responses.

What we found:
- 73% of visitors left within 30 seconds
- Form abandonment was 67% (7 fields — too many)
- Trust signals buried in footer
- All paid traffic going to homepage (wrong destination)

2️⃣ A/B TESTING INFRASTRUCTURE
We set up Optimizely for proper server-side testing.
Every change would be validated by data, not intuition.

3️⃣ QUICK WINS (Weeks 5-8)
Changed CTA copy:
"Get Started" → "Book Your Free Demo"
+18% conversion

Simplified form:
7 fields → 3 fields
+42% form completion

4️⃣ HERO + TRUST SIGNALS
Rewrote homepage hero:
Generic: "Banking built for modern businesses"
Specific: "Move money 3x faster. 95% of startups approve in 24 hours."
+31% conversion

Moved trust signals near CTAs (not footer):
Security badges, customer logos, SOC2 certification
+22% conversion

5️⃣ DEDICATED LANDING PAGES
Built 4 landing pages for different traffic segments:
- Brand search landing page
- Competitor conquesting page
- Product-specific page (business banking)
- SMB/startup-focused page

Homepage CVR: 1.8%
Dedicated landing page CVR: 8.1%

That's a 4.5x difference.

**The Results:**
• Conversion Rate: 2.1% → 6.5% (+210%)
• Monthly Demo Requests: 252 → 780 (+210%)
• Effective CAC: $340 → $112 (-67%)
• Form Completion: 33% → 68% (+106%)
• Bounce Rate: 73% → 51% (-30%)
• Incremental Revenue: $1.8M/year

**The math:**
$50K investment (the CRO program)
$1.8M incremental annual revenue
That's a 36x return.

**The insight:**
You can double your leads without doubling your traffic.

Every visitor who doesn't convert is a wasted acquisition dollar.

If you're spending on paid ads and not optimizing conversion, you're burning money.
