# Conversion Rate Optimization — Sales Talking Points

## Opening Hook
"If I told you we could increase your demo requests by 3x without spending an additional dollar on traffic — would that be worth a conversation?"

## Problem Identification
- $100K/month paid traffic but only 2.1% converting to demo requests
- $340 CAC unsustainable for $2,400 ACV product
- No A/B testing infrastructure — changes made on intuition
- All paid traffic going to homepage instead of dedicated landing pages
- Form with 7 fields causing 67% abandonment
- Trust signals buried in footer, not near CTAs
- 73% bounce rate indicating value prop not being communicated

## Solution Highlights
- Research-first approach: GA4 analysis, Hotjar session recordings, user interviews, competitive teardown
- A/B testing infrastructure (Optimizely) for data-driven decisions
- 9 tests run over 5 months, 89% hit statistical significance
- Dedicated landing pages for 4 traffic segments
- Form simplification (7 → 3 fields)
- Hero rewrite with specific value proposition
- Trust signal placement near CTAs
- Exit-intent overlay and ROI calculator

## Key Metrics to Quote
- Conversion Rate: 2.1% → 6.5% (3.1x improvement)
- CAC: $340 → $112 (-67%)
- Demo Requests: 252 → 780/month (+210%)
- Form Completion: 33% → 68% (+106%)
- Bounce Rate: 73% → 51% (-30%)
- Landing Page CVR: 3.2% → 8.1% (+153%)
- $1.8M incremental annual revenue at zero extra traffic cost
- 36x ROI on $50K CRO investment
