# NovaTech Solutions — Paid Ads & Funnel Optimization
**90-Day Engagement | CAC Reduced 67% | 539% More Qualified Demos**

---

## The Challenge
NovaTech Solutions, a B2B SaaS company, was spending $42,000/month on paid ads across Meta, Google, and LinkedIn with a $187 CAC and only 23 qualified demos per month. Marketing and sales were misaligned, attribution was broken, and the math didn't work.

## What We Did
- Full-funnel audit across all three advertising platforms
- Rebuilt campaign architecture around a hub-and-spoke model (Google as intent hub, Meta/LI as retargeting spokes)
- Created dedicated landing pages with pre-qualification forms
- Implemented multi-touch attribution via GA4 and server-side tracking
- Established weekly optimization cadence with budget reallocation

## The Results
| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| CAC | $187 | $61 |
| Qualified Demos/Month | 23 | 147 |
| Cost Per Demo | $1,826 | $262 |
| ROAS | 0.8x | 3.2x |
| Monthly Pipeline | $276K | $1.47M |

**Revenue Impact:** $892,000 in new annual contracts closed within 90 days.

## Tools Used
Google Ads, Meta Business Suite, LinkedIn Campaign Manager, GA4, HubSpot, Unbounce, Google Tag Manager, Looker Studio
