# Paid Ads + Funnel Optimization — Sales Talking Points

## Opening Hook
"If I told you we reduced a B2B SaaS client's customer acquisition cost by 67% — from $187 to $61 — in 90 days, what would that be worth to you?"

## Problem Identification
- NovaTech was spending $42K/month with no clear ROI
- CAC of $187 meant 18+ month payback on $1,200 contracts — unsustainable
- 23 qualified demos/month wasn't enough to hit growth targets
- Leadership couldn't attribute revenue to specific ad channels

## Solution Highlights
- Full-funnel audit across Meta, Google Ads, and LinkedIn (not siloed management)
- Hub-and-spoke campaign architecture: Google for intent, Meta/LI for retargeting
- Server-side tracking implementation for accurate attribution
- Dedicated landing pages with pre-qualification flows
- Weekly optimization with budget reallocation

## Key Metrics to Quote
- CAC reduced 67% ($187 → $61)
- Qualified demos increased 539% (23 → 147/month)
- ROAS improved from 0.8x to 3.2x
- $892K in new contracts closed within 90 days
- Pipeline increased from $276K to $1.47M/month
- Sales cycle shortened from 94 to 61 days
- Average contract value increased 37% (better leads)

## The Methodology Story
"We didn't just 'run ads better.' We treated it as a system problem — tracking, funnel architecture, and sales alignment — and solved all three in parallel. That's why the results were fast and durable."

## Client Validation Quote
"NovaTech renewed for 6 months. They went from questioning if paid media worked at all to making it their primary pipeline driver."

## Objection Pre-Handling
- "We tried ads before and it didn't work" → "Most failures come from tracking issues, not ad issues. We fix attribution first, then optimize."
- "Our audience is too niche for paid" → "NovaTech said the same. We found them 147 qualified demos/month."
- "We don't have the budget" → "We reduced their CAC by 67% while actually decreasing ad spend by 8%. Efficiency beat volume."
