# Paid Ads Management + Funnel Optimization

## NovaTech Solutions — B2B SaaS

**The Ask:** NovaTech was spending $42K/month on paid ads across Meta, Google, and LinkedIn. CAC sat at $187. They were getting traffic, but not revenue.

**The Approach:** Full-funnel audit → Hub-and-spoke campaign architecture → Dedicated landing pages → Multi-touch attribution → Weekly optimization cadence

**The Results:**
- CAC: $187 → $61 (-67%)
- Qualified Demos/Month: 23 → 147 (+539%)
- ROAS: 0.8x → 3.2x
- Pipeline: $276K → $1.47M/month

**Tools:** Google Ads, Meta Business Suite, LinkedIn Campaign Manager, GA4, HubSpot, Unbounce, Google Tag Manager, Looker Studio

**Timeline:** 90 Days

**Client:** Marcus Ellington, VP of Revenue

---

## Overview

NovaTech Solutions is a B2B SaaS company offering project management software. Despite a significant paid media budget, they struggled with unsustainable customer acquisition costs and inconsistent pipeline generation. This engagement redesigned their entire paid advertising approach and delivered a 67% reduction in CAC with a 539% increase in qualified demo volume.

## Process

We started with a comprehensive audit across all three platforms to understand where budget was being wasted and where true conversions were happening. Based on findings, we rebuilt their campaign structure using a hub-and-spoke model, created dedicated landing pages with pre-qualification flows, and implemented proper multi-touch attribution so they could finally see which ads drove closed revenue.

## Results

The 90-day engagement delivered:
- **$892,000** in new annual contracts closed
- **312% ROI** on ad spend
- **37% increase** in average contract value (better leads = higher-value deals)
- Sales cycle reduced from 94 days to 61 days

## Client Testimonial

"We'd tried two other agencies. Within 30 days of starting, we had a unified dashboard showing exactly which ads were driving closed revenue. The CAC reduction from $187 to $61 was remarkable, but what impressed me more was the pipeline jump — we went from 23 demos a month to 147."
