## 1. Understanding Your Situation

NovaTech Solutions is spending $42,000/month across Meta, Google Ads, and LinkedIn but facing three critical problems:

1. **CAC of $187 per customer** makes unit economics unsustainable at current contract values
2. **23 qualified demos/month** is insufficient to hit growth targets
3. **No attribution clarity** — leadership can't connect ad spend to closed revenue

We've seen this pattern before. It typically stems from campaigns built without funnel architecture, siloed channel management, and tracking that doesn't capture the full customer journey.

---

## 2. Our Proposed Solution

A 90-day paid media overhaul structured in three phases:

### Phase 1: Foundation & Audit (Days 1-30)
- Full-funnel audit of all three ad platforms (Meta, Google, LinkedIn)
- Attribution model reconstruction using GA4 and server-side tracking
- Landing page audit with conversion funnel mapping
- Competitive analysis of your category

**Deliverables:** Audit report, revised attribution model, landing page recommendations

### Phase 2: Funnel Redesign & Activation (Days 31-60)
- Rebuild campaign structure using hub-and-spoke architecture
- Create dedicated landing pages for each campaign type
- Implement pre-qualification flow to improve lead quality
- Launch retargeting infrastructure across all platforms
- Begin structured A/B testing on creative and copy

**Deliverables:** 6 new landing pages, 3 campaign architectures, retargeting sequences

### Phase 3: Optimization & Scale (Days 61-90)
- Weekly performance reviews with budget reallocation
- Scale winning campaigns, kill underperformers
- Expand lookalike audiences based on best customer data
- Provide optimization roadmap for continued improvement

**Deliverables:** Weekly performance reports, 90-day ROI analysis, scale roadmap
