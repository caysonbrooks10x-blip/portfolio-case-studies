🔴 CASE STUDY: How we took NovaTech's CAC from $187 to $61 — a 67% reduction.

NovaTech Solutions was spending $42K/month on paid ads. They had traffic. They had leads. But their customer acquisition cost was $187 and the math didn't work.

Here's what we did:

**The Problem:**
• 23 qualified demos/month
• No attribution visibility (literally couldn't tell which ads drove revenue)
• Sales team drowning in unqualified leads

**The Fix (90 days):**

1️⃣ FULL-FUNNEL AUDIT
Before touching a single ad, we rebuilt their attribution model. Couldn't optimize what you can't measure.

2️⃣ HUB-AND-SPOKE ARCHITECTURE
Google Ads = intent capture (people actively searching)
Meta = retargeting (people who visited but didn't convert)
LinkedIn = enterprise decision-makers (warmth before conversion ask)

3️⃣ LANDING PAGE OVERHAUL
Created dedicated pages for each campaign type with pre-qualification forms. Lead quality improved immediately.

4️⃣ WEEKLY OPTIMIZATION CADENCE
Budget reallocation every 7 days. Killed underperformers in 5 days, scaled winners in 7.

**The Results:**
• CAC: $187 → $61 (-67%)
• Qualified demos: 23 → 147/month (+539%)
• ROAS: 0.8x → 3.2x
• Pipeline: $276K → $1.47M/month
• $892K in new contracts closed within 90 days

**The biggest insight:**
Retargeting is where the money is made.
Cold audiences converted at 1.2%.
Retargeting audiences converted at 11.4%.

If you're running paid ads without a proper retargeting infrastructure, you're leaving money on the table.

NovaTech renewed for 6 months after seeing the results. We're now helping them expand into international markets.
