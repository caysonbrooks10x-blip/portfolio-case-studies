## Recommended Visual Style
- **Color palette:** Professional blue/gray with green accents for positive metrics
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, Roboto, or similar)
- **Chart style:** Minimal gridlines, data labels prominent
- **Icons:** Lucid or Font Awesome icon set
- **Overall feel:** Enterprise SaaS aesthetic, not startup-casual
