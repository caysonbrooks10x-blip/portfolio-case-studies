## 1. Understanding Your Situation

Meridian Analytics has a strong product with clear market fit, but your SEO presence is essentially nonexistent. With only 2,100 organic visits per month and no content targeting commercial keywords, you're fully dependent on paid acquisition at $165 CAC. You've likely noticed that competitors dominate the search results for exactly the queries your buyers are making.

This is a common situation for product-led B2B companies. SEO is often treated as secondary to sales and paid marketing, which means when it finally gets attention, there's significant technical and content debt to address.

---

## 2. Our Proposed Solution

A 6-month SEO program structured in four phases:

### Phase 1: Technical Foundation (Month 1)
- Comprehensive SEO audit using Screaming Frog, Ahrefs, and Google Search Console
- Fix crawl errors, redirect chains, and indexation issues
- Resolve Core Web Vitals issues (page speed, CLS, LCP)
- Build logical site architecture with clear hierarchy
- Implement structured data (Organization, Product, FAQ schemas)

**Deliverables:** Technical audit report, remediation roadmap, updated XML sitemap, schema implementation

### Phase 2: Keyword & Content Strategy (Month 2)
- Comprehensive keyword research across your topic clusters
- Competitive analysis of who's ranking and why
- Content calendar for 6-month rollout (20+ pieces)
- Pillar page strategy for topic authority
- Conversion architecture design for each content type

**Deliverables:** Keyword research document, content calendar, content briefs for all planned pages

### Phase 3: Content Production & Optimization (Months 3-5)
- Publish 2 long-form blog posts per week (1,800-2,500 words)
- Build 4 pillar pages targeting head terms (3,000+ words each)
- Create 12 product-related landing pages
- Upgrade existing thin content
- Implement systematic internal linking

**Deliverables:** 40+ new SEO-optimized pages, internal linking framework, updated content strategy

### Phase 4: Link Building & Authority (Months 4-6)
- HARO responses for topical authority
- Guest posting on fintech/finance publications
- Broken link building outreach
- Resource page outreach for tools lists
- Industry partnership content collaborations

**Deliverables:** 30+ high-authority backlinks (DA 40+), domain authority increase of 10+ points
