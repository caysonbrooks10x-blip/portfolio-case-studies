# Meridian Analytics — SEO Growth Strategy
**6-Month Engagement | Organic Traffic 4.2x | CAC $12 vs $165 Paid**

## The Challenge
Meridian Analytics had 2,100 monthly organic visits and zero content targeting their buyers' search queries. They were fully dependent on paid acquisition with a $165 CAC, and leadership wanted to build a sustainable organic channel.

## What We Did
- Comprehensive technical SEO audit and remediation (47 crawl errors fixed, Core Web Vitals resolved)
- Built topic cluster content strategy across 5 keyword clusters
- Produced 52 SEO-optimized pages (28 blog posts, 4 pillar pages, 12 landing pages, 8 upgraded)
- Implemented link building outreach (47 DA 40+ backlinks acquired)
- Structured content with conversion architecture for demo requests

## The Results
| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Monthly Organic Traffic | 2,100 | 8,800 |
| Organic Demo Requests | 12/mo | 89/mo |
| Organic Pipeline | $48K/mo | $380K/mo |
| Organic CAC | $165 | $12 |
| Domain Authority | 23 | 41 |
| Top-10 Rankings | 3 | 47 |
| Backlinks (DA 40+) | 4 | 51 |

**Revenue Impact:** $1.4M in pipeline generated from organic search over 6 months.

## Tools Used
Screaming Frog, Ahrefs, SEMrush, Google Search Console, GA4, Accuranker, WordPress, Yoast SEO, Schema Pro, HARO, Pitchbox, Looker Studio
