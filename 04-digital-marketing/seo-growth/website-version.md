# SEO Growth Strategy + Implementation

**The Ask:** Meridian had 2,100 organic visits per month and zero content for their buyers' search queries. $165 CAC from paid, no organic channel. They wanted to reduce paid dependency.

**The Approach:** Technical SEO audit and remediation → Topic cluster keyword strategy → 52 SEO-optimized pages → Link building outreach → Conversion-optimized content architecture

**The Results:**
- Organic Traffic: 2,100 → 8,800/month (+319%)
- Organic Demo Requests: 12 → 89/month
- Organic Pipeline: $48K → $380K/month
- Organic CAC: $165 → $12
- Domain Authority: 23 → 41

**Tools:** Screaming Frog, Ahrefs, SEMrush, Google Search Console, GA4, WordPress, Yoast SEO, Schema Pro, HARO, Pitchbox

**Client:** Jennifer Kowalski, Head of Marketing

---

## Overview

Meridian Analytics is a financial data visualization platform serving CFOs and finance teams. Despite having a strong product with clear market fit, their SEO presence was nonexistent — they had 2,100 organic visits per month, no content targeting commercial keywords, and were fully dependent on paid acquisition. This engagement transformed SEO from a neglected channel into their highest-ROI marketing investment.

## Process

We began with a comprehensive technical SEO audit to fix crawl errors, resolve Core Web Vitals issues, and build a logical site architecture. Then we developed a topic cluster content strategy targeting 5 keyword clusters with commercial intent. Over 6 months, we produced 52 SEO-optimized pages — blog posts, pillar pages, and landing pages — each designed with conversion architecture to capture demo requests. We supplemented content with an outreach-driven link building campaign that acquired 47 high-authority backlinks.

## Results

The 6-month engagement delivered:
- **319% increase** in organic traffic (2,100 → 8,800/month)
- **$1.4M in pipeline** generated from organic search
- **$12 organic CAC** vs. $165 from paid channels
- **47 top-10 keyword rankings** (up from 3)
- Domain Authority increased from 23 to 41
