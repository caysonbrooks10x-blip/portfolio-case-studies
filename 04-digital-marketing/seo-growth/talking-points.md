## Opening Hook
"If I could show you how to get your cost per lead from $165 to $12 — and build a traffic channel that generates leads for years without ongoing ad spend — would that be interesting?"

## Problem Identification
- 2,100 organic visits per month from essentially zero SEO effort
- No content targeting commercial keywords their buyers search
- Fully dependent on paid acquisition at $165 CAC
- Domain Authority of 23 — below the threshold where ranking gets easier
- 47 crawl errors and Core Web Vitals issues slowing down existing traffic

## Solution Highlights
- Technical SEO foundation (fixing crawl errors, Core Web Vitals, site architecture)
- Topic cluster keyword strategy (5 clusters, 23+ target keywords)
- 52 SEO-optimized pages produced over 6 months
- Pillar-and-spoke content architecture for authority building
- Outreach-driven link building (47 DA 40+ backlinks)
- Conversion architecture in every piece of content

## Objection Pre-Handling
- "SEO takes too long" → "It does take patience, but we show ranking improvements by Month 2-3 even if traffic hasn't fully materialized. And unlike paid, every ranking you gain persists."
- "We tried content marketing before" → "Most content failures come from no strategy — random topics, no keyword targeting, thin content. We built a systematic topic cluster approach where each piece reinforces the others."
- "Our industry is too competitive" → "We avoided head terms dominated by big brands. We targeted long-tail commercial keywords where intent is higher and competition is lower."
