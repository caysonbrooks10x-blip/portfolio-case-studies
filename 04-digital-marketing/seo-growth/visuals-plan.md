## Recommended Visual Style
- **Color palette:** Professional blue/green palette (growth = green, authority = blue)
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, Roboto)
- **Chart style:** Minimal, data-forward, professional SaaS aesthetic
- **Icons:** Lucid or Font Awesome
- **Overall feel:** Enterprise B2B, not consumer-startup
