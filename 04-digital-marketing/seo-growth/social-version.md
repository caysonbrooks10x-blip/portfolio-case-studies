SEO case study: How we took Meridian Analytics from 2,100 to 8,800 monthly organic visits in 6 months.

And why organic CAC of $12 vs. $165 from paid changed how they think about marketing investment forever.

Here's what we did:

**The Situation:**
Meridian Analytics is a B2B SaaS selling financial data visualization software. Great product. Strong ICP.

Problem: Only 2,100 organic visits/month. Zero content. Fully dependent on $165 CAC paid channels.

They wanted to build an organic channel.

**The Approach (6 months):**

1️⃣ TECHNICAL FOUNDATION (Month 1)
Fixed 47 crawl errors. Resolved Core Web Vitals (CLS dropped from 0.31 to 0.08). Rebuilt site architecture. Implemented schema markup.

No new content until the foundation was right.

2️⃣ KEYWORD STRATEGY (Month 2)
Identified 5 topic clusters with commercial search intent:
- Financial dashboard software
- Data visualization for finance
- Fintech software comparison
- Executive reporting tools
- CFO dashboard solutions

Built a content calendar for 23+ target pages.

3️⃣ CONTENT ENGINE (Months 3-5)
Published 28 blog posts (1,800-2,500 words each).
Built 4 pillar pages (3,000+ words).
Launched 12 product landing pages.

Each piece designed with conversion architecture — not just traffic, but demo requests.

4️⃣ LINK BUILDING (Months 4-6)
Guest posts on fintech publications.
HARO responses.
Broken link building.
Resource page outreach.

Acquired 47 high-authority backlinks (DA 40+).

**The Results:**
• Organic traffic: 2,100 → 8,800/month (+319%)
• Organic demo requests: 12 → 89/month
• Organic pipeline: $48K → $380K/month
• Organic CAC: $165 → $12
• Domain Authority: 23 → 41
• Top-10 rankings: 3 → 47
• Total pipeline from SEO: $1.4M

**The insight that changed everything:**
SEO compounds. Month 6 traffic was 3x Month 3 traffic.

Because every piece of content that ranks:
- Generates traffic
- Passes authority to other pages
- Attracts more backlinks
- Improves rankings for everything else

The channel gets more efficient over time.

That's why $1.4M in pipeline at $12 CAC is only the beginning. That DA 41 domain will generate leads for years.
