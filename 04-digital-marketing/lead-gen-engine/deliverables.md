## Phase 3: Marketing Automation (Weeks 6-12)

### Automation Workflow Deliverables

**Lead Routing:**
- [ ] High-score lead routing (>70) to sales within 15 minutes
- [ ] Medium-score lead routing (40-70) to nurture
- [ ] Low-score lead routing (20-40) to content drip
- [ ] Slack notifications for high-value leads

**High-Intent Nurture Sequence (3 emails/5 days):**
- [ ] Email 1: Personalized intro + immediate value offer
- [ ] Email 2: Case study relevant to their interest
- [ ] Email 3: Consultation/demo offer with urgency

**Mid-Funnel Nurture Sequence (7 emails/30 days):**
- [ ] Emails 1-2: Educational content matching their interest
- [ ] Emails 3-4: Case studies showing results
- [ ] Emails 5-6: Service deep-dives
- [ ] Email 7: Consultation offer

**Awareness Nurture Sequence (6 emails/60 days):**
- [ ] Emails 1-2: Industry insights and trends
- [ ] Emails 3-4: Educational content
- [ ] Emails 5-6: Soft sales messages with high value

**Re-Engagement Sequence:**
- [ ] Email 1: "We miss you" + new content highlight
- [ ] Email 2: Case study or success story
- [ ] Email 3: Special offer or reactivation incentive
- [ ] Final: Preference update or unsubscribe prompt

**Enterprise Track:**
- [ ] Custom sequence for enterprise-scale prospects
- [ ] Executive-level content and messaging
- [ ] Consultation offer with senior contact

### Lead Scoring Configuration
- [ ] Demographic scoring (company size, industry, title)
- [ ] Engagement scoring (email opens, page visits, content downloads)
- [ ] Intent scoring (pricing page, demo page visits)
- [ ] Score threshold calibration
