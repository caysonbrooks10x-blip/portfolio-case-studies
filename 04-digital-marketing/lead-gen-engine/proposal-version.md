# Lead Generation Engine Proposal
**Prepared for:** Pinnacle Logistics
**Prepared by:** Cayson Brooks
**Date:** January 2026

---

## 1. Understanding Your Situation

Pinnacle Logistics has built a solid business through referrals and a dedicated sales team, but you're leaving significant growth on the table. With only 85 qualified leads per month and a $60K marketing budget, you're not capturing the companies actively searching for logistics providers online. Your website is informational but doesn't convert. You have no landing pages, no lead scoring, and no systematic follow-up for leads that aren't ready to buy immediately.

The result: your sales team chases unqualified prospects while high-quality leads slip away. Growth is capped by referral volume rather than marketing infrastructure.

---

## 2. Our Proposed Solution

A 4-month lead generation engine build in four phases:

### Phase 1: Strategy & Infrastructure (Weeks 1-4)
- Conversion audit and competitive analysis
- Lead qualification framework development (with sales team input)
- Landing page architecture design (12 pages)
- Content calendar for lead magnets
- HubSpot CRM setup and data cleaning

**Deliverables:** Strategy document, landing page wireframes, lead scoring model, content calendar

### Phase 2: Landing Pages & Lead Magnets (Weeks 3-8)
- Build 12 dedicated landing pages (per service line/intent)
- Create lead magnet content (reports, assessments, calculators)
- Implement form integrations with HubSpot
- Set up lead routing and notifications

**Deliverables:** 12 landing pages live, lead magnets deployed, lead routing configured

### Phase 3: Marketing Automation (Weeks 6-12)
- Build 5 automated nurture sequences
- Configure lead scoring model
- Set up engagement tracking
- Implement speed-to-contact SLA
- Build sales follow-up workflows

**Deliverables:** All nurture sequences live, scoring operational, Slack notifications active

### Phase 4: Paid Traffic & Optimization (Weeks 10-16)
- Launch Google Ads campaigns (brand, service, competitor conquesting)
- Launch LinkedIn campaigns (targeted to logistics decision-makers)
- A/B test landing page elements
- Optimize for cost per lead
- Refine lead scoring based on conversion data

**Deliverables:** Paid campaigns live, optimization underway, lead quality improving
