# Pinnacle Logistics — Lead Generation Engine
**4-Month Engagement | Lead Volume 5.8x | CPL $706 → $121**

## The Challenge
Pinnacle Logistics was generating only 85 qualified leads/month despite a $60K marketing budget. Their website was a digital brochure with no conversion mechanism, no landing pages, and no marketing automation.

## What We Did
- Built 12 dedicated landing pages (one per service line/intent)
- Developed lead scoring model with sales team input
- Created 5 automated nurture sequences for different score tiers
- Built a lead magnet ladder (reports → assessments → consultations)
- Implemented 15-minute SLA for high-value lead follow-up
- Launched Google Ads and LinkedIn campaigns to landing pages
- Set up HubSpot CRM integration with Slack notifications

## The Results
| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Monthly Qualified Leads | 85 | 495 |
| Cost Per Lead | $706 | $121 |
| Lead-to-Opportunity Rate | 12% | 34% |
| Landing Page Conversion | 1.2% | 8.4% |
| MQLs/Month | 120 | 680 |
| Sales Follow-Up Time | 48 hrs | 4 hrs |
| New Business Pipeline | $1.2M/qtr | $3.8M/qtr |

**Revenue Impact:** $4.2M in new business opportunities within first 6 months.

## Tools Used
HubSpot, Unbounce, Google Ads, LinkedIn Campaign Manager, GA4, Typeform, Slack, Looker Studio
