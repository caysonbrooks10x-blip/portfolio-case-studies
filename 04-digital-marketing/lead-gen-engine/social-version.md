Case study: How we built a lead generation engine that took a logistics company from 85 leads/month to 495.

5.8x more leads. In 4 months.

Here's what we did.

**The Situation:**
Pinnacle Logistics is a third-party logistics (3PL) provider.
Warehousing, fulfillment, freight management.

They'd built a solid business through referrals.
But referral-only growth has a ceiling.

Problem: Only 85 qualified leads/month.
$60K/month marketing budget.
No landing pages. No automation. No lead scoring.

Their website was a digital brochure.
Sales spent half their time chasing unqualified leads.

**The Problem:**
No conversion infrastructure.

When a company searched "e-commerce fulfillment provider" or "3PL Tennessee," Pinnacle wasn't capturing them.

No dedicated landing pages.
No lead magnets.
No way to capture intent.

**The Fix (4 months):**

1️⃣ LEAD QUALIFICATION FRAMEWORK
Before building anything, we worked with sales to define what a qualified lead actually looked like.

Company size? Industry? Current logistics pain points? Budget? Timeline?

We built a lead scoring model that sales helped design.
They trusted it because they built it.

2️⃣ LANDING PAGE ARCHITECTURE
Built 12 dedicated landing pages:

• Warehousing Services
• E-Commerce Fulfillment
• Freight Management
• Cold Chain Logistics
• 3PL Consulting
• Warehousing Cost Calculator
• Freight Rate Benchmark
• Supply Chain Assessment
• Case Study Bundle
• Demo Request
• Enterprise 3PL
• SMB 3PL

Each page: specific headline, service benefits, trust signals, lead capture form.

Homepage conversion rate: 1.2%
Dedicated landing page conversion rate: 8.4%

That's 7x better.

3️⃣ LEAD MAGNET LADDER
We created a tiered content strategy:

Bottom: Industry reports (easy to consume, low commitment)
Middle: Assessments and calculators (interactive, medium commitment)
Top: Consultations and demos (high commitment)

Captured leads at every funnel stage.
1,240 content downloads per month.

4️⃣ MARKETING AUTOMATION
Built 5 automated nurture sequences:

• High-Intent (score >70): 3 emails in 5 days with sales CTAs
• Mid-Funnel (score 40-70): 7 emails in 30 days with content
• Awareness (score 20-40): 6 emails in 60 days with education
• Re-Engagement: For leads with no activity in 60 days
• Enterprise: Customized for large prospects

5️⃣ SPEED-TO-CONTACT
Here's where we beat the competition:

When a high-scoring lead submits a form, sales gets a Slack notification in 15 MINUTES.

Average time-to-follow-up for industry: 48 hours.
Pinnacle's time: 4 hours.

That speed advantage means leads don't go cold.

6️⃣ PAID TRAFFIC
Launched Google Ads campaigns:
• Brand protection
• Service-specific (warehousing, fulfillment, freight)
• Competitor conquesting

Launched LinkedIn campaigns targeting logistics decision-makers.

**The Results:**
• Monthly Leads: 85 → 495 (+5.8x)
• Cost Per Lead: $706 → $121 (-83%)
• Lead-to-Opportunity: 12% → 34% (+183%)
• Landing Page CVR: 1.2% → 8.4%
• MQLs/Month: 120 → 680
• Sales Follow-Up Time: 48 hrs → 4 hrs
• New Business Pipeline: $1.2M → $3.8M/quarter

**The math:**
$67K investment (the lead gen engine)
$4.2M in new business opportunities in 6 months

That's a 62x return on a marketing infrastructure investment.

**The insight:**
You can't sell what you can't capture.

If you're not systematically capturing intent — with landing pages, lead magnets, and automation — you're invisible to the companies searching for you right now.
