# Lead Generation Engine

## Pinnacle Logistics — Third-Party Logistics (3PL)

**The Ask:** Pinnacle Logistics was generating only 85 qualified leads/month from a $60K marketing budget. No landing pages, no lead scoring, no automation. Sales chased unqualified leads while good prospects went cold.

**The Approach:** Lead qualification framework → 12 dedicated landing pages → 5 automated nurture sequences → Lead scoring model → Lead magnet ladder → Speed-to-contact SLA → Paid traffic integration

**The Results:**
- Monthly Leads: 85 → 495 (5.8x)
- Cost Per Lead: $706 → $121 (-83%)
- Lead-to-Opportunity: 12% → 34%
- Landing Page CVR: 1.2% → 8.4%
- Sales Follow-Up: 48 hrs → 4 hrs
- Pipeline: $1.2M → $3.8M/quarter

**Tools:** HubSpot, Unbounce, Google Ads, LinkedIn Campaign Manager, GA4, Typeform, Slack

**Timeline:** 4 Months

**Client:** James Whitfield, VP of Sales

---

## Overview

Pinnacle Logistics is a third-party logistics provider offering warehousing, fulfillment, and freight management services. They had built a solid business through referrals but needed systematic lead generation to fuel growth. Their existing website was informational but had no conversion mechanism. This engagement built a complete lead generation engine: 12 dedicated landing pages, marketing automation, lead scoring, and sales follow-up systems that transformed their pipeline generation.

## Process

We started with a comprehensive audit of Pinnacle's sales process and lead qualification criteria, involving the sales team in building a scoring model they would trust. We built 12 dedicated landing pages — one for each service line and audience segment — each with targeted content and lead capture forms. We created a lead magnet ladder from low-commitment industry reports to high-commitment consultations, capturing prospects at every funnel stage. We built 5 automated nurture sequences so leads who weren't ready to buy stayed engaged. We implemented a 15-minute SLA for high-scoring leads and integrated HubSpot with Slack for instant sales notifications.

## Results

The 4-month engagement delivered:
- **5.8x increase** in qualified lead volume (85 → 495/month)
- **83% reduction** in cost per lead ($706 → $121)
- **183% improvement** in lead-to-opportunity rate (12% → 34%)
- **8.4% landing page conversion rate** (vs. 1.2% previously)
- **$4.2M in new business opportunities** within 6 months
