## Recommended Visual Style
- **Color palette:** Industrial/logistics (blues, grays, accent orange for CTAs)
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter, Roboto)
- **Chart style:** Professional, data-forward
- **Icons:** Lucid or Font Awesome for logistics/supply chain
- **Overall feel:** Enterprise B2B, professional, trustworthy
