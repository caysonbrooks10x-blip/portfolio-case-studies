# Lead Generation Engine — Sales Talking Points

## Opening Hook
"If I told you we built a lead generation engine that took a 3PL company from 85 leads a month to 495 — in 4 months — and reduced their cost per lead by 83%, would that be worth a conversation?"

## Problem Identification
- 85 qualified leads/month from $60K marketing budget — inefficient
- No landing pages — website was a digital brochure with no conversion mechanism
- No lead scoring — every lead went to sales regardless of fit
- No marketing automation — leads who weren't ready went cold
- 48-hour average follow-up time — leads cooling off before sales called
- Sales team chasing unqualified leads while good prospects slipped away

## Solution Highlights
- Lead qualification framework built with sales team input (they trust the scores)
- 12 dedicated landing pages (one per service line/intent)
- Lead magnet ladder (reports → assessments → consultations)
- 5 automated nurture sequences for different score tiers
- 15-minute SLA for high-value lead follow-up
- HubSpot + Slack integration for instant sales notifications
- Google Ads and LinkedIn campaigns driving to dedicated landing pages

## Key Metrics to Quote
- Monthly Qualified Leads: 85 → 495 (5.8x)
- Cost Per Lead: $706 → $121 (-83%)
- Lead-to-Opportunity Rate: 12% → 34% (+183%)
- Landing Page Conversion: 1.2% → 8.4% (7x)
- MQLs/Month: 120 → 680
- Sales Follow-Up Time: 48 hrs → 4 hrs
- New Business Pipeline: $1.2M → $3.8M/quarter
- $4.2M in new opportunities in 6 months

## The Methodology Story
"We didn't just build landing pages. We built a complete lead generation system — starting with a lead scoring model that sales helped design and actually trusts. We built 12 landing pages matched to specific searches. We created a content ladder that captures leads at every stage. We automated nurture sequences so leads who aren't ready stay warm. And we implemented a speed-to-contact system that gets high-value leads to sales in 15 minutes. That's why close rates on marketing leads went from 12% to 34%."

## Client Validation Quote
"Sales team used to dread marketing leads. Now they fight over them."

## Objection Pre-Handling
- "We tried lead generation before and it didn't work" → "Most lead gen fails because of infrastructure problems — wrong landing pages, no lead scoring, slow follow-up. We fix the system, not just the tactics."
- "Our sales cycle is too long for automation" → "Long cycles are EXACTLY why automation matters. A 6-month sales cycle means leads go cold without nurture. Our sequences keep them warm until they're ready."
- "Our sales team won't trust lead scores" → "We involve sales in building the model. They define what qualified means. That's why Pinnacle's sales team now fights over marketing leads."
