# KPI Architecture — Case Study Summary

## Client
Velocity Health — Series B healthcare tech, $38M ARR, 180 employees, $28M raised

## Challenge
No unified KPI framework, inconsistent metric definitions across teams, 3-day board packages, sales/finance 26% variance, no leading indicators, OKRs disconnected from outcomes.

## Solution
Built comprehensive KPI architecture with 12 company "True North" metrics, team-level KPI trees, automated board reporting, and metric governance model.

## Results
- Board package: 3 days → 4 hours (94% reduction)
- Finance reporting time: 15 hrs/week freed
- Sales/Finance variance: 26% → < 2%
- Leading indicators: 8 metrics validated
- Series C preparation: Clean data room enabled $45M raise
- 4.2 month payback period

## Key Deliverables
12-company True North metrics, 5 team KPI trees, automated Looker board pack, metric definitions document, governance model.

## Tools
Snowflake, Looker, dbt, Fivetran, Census, Causal (OKR)

## Duration
20 weeks
