# KPI Architecture for Scaling Businesses

## The Challenge

Velocity Health was scaling from 50 to 180 employees but hadn't built the metric infrastructure to对齐 everyone. Different teams used different definitions. Board packages took 3 days. Sales and Finance were 26% apart on the same numbers. Product shipped features with no connection to business outcomes.

## The Solution

Built a comprehensive KPI architecture with 12 company "True North" metrics, team-level KPI trees, automated board reporting, and metric governance — giving everyone a common definition of success.

## Technical Approach

- Snowflake metrics warehouse with dbt semantic layer
- 12 company True North metrics with documented definitions
- 5 team KPI trees showing metric hierarchy
- Automated Looker board package (12 slides)
- Statistical anomaly detection for critical metrics
- Metric Review Board governance process
- Census reverse-ETL to operational tools
