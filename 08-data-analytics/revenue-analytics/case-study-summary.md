# Revenue Analytics & Forecasting — Case Study Summary

## Client
NovaTech Solutions — $47M ARR B2B SaaS, 380 enterprise customers

## Challenge
Forecast accuracy was 58% (essentially random), manual Excel models consumed 20+ hours/month, no visibility into churn risk or expansion signals.

## Solution
Built a revenue intelligence platform with BigQuery + Looker + custom ML forecasting engine combining Prophet and LightGBM ensemble models.

## Results
- 91% forecast accuracy (up from 58%)
- 90% reduction in manual modeling time (20hrs → 2hrs/month)
- $2.1M in retained revenue from churn prevention
- $840K in identified expansion opportunities
- Series C fundraise secured with reliable growth narrative
- 3.1 month payback period

## Tools
BigQuery, Looker, dbt, Python (Prophet, LightGBM), Segment, Amplitude, Salesforce, Stripe

## Duration
20 weeks
