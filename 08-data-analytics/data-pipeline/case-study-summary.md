# Data Cleanup & Reporting Pipeline — Case Study Summary

## Client
Pacific Northwest Manufacturing — $180M industrial manufacturer, 4 facilities, 850 employees

## Challenge
Monthly close took 8 business days, 47 Excel files, three conflicting versions of truth, no single customer record, 24-hour production data delay.

## Solution
Modernized data infrastructure with Snowflake warehouse, dbt transformations, and automated reporting pipelines. Connected 12 source systems, deduplicated vendor/customer records, implemented data quality framework.

## Results
- Monthly close: 8 days → 3 days (62% reduction)
- Finance data-gathering time: 60% → 15%
- Customer records: 3,400 → 2,100 master records
- Vendor records cleaned and deduplicated
- Production data: 24hr delay → 15 minutes
- Forecast accuracy: 45% → 87%
- 8.4 month payback period

## Tools
Snowflake, dbt, Metabase, Fivetran, Python, Great Expectations

## Duration
28 weeks
