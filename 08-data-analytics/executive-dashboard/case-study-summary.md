# Executive Dashboard Case Study Summary

## Client
Meridian Capital Partners — $2.4B private equity firm with 34 portfolio companies

## Challenge
Manual monthly reporting consumed 14+ hours per week, with data scattered across 7 disconnected systems. Board packages arrived late, inconsistent, and error-prone.

## Solution
Unified Looker + Snowflake executive dashboard ecosystem with standardized KPIs, automated ELT pipelines, and real-time portfolio visibility.

## Results
- 96% reduction in reporting time (14hrs → 45min/week)
- Board package prep: 3 days → 18 hours
- Zero reconciliation errors (from 12/quarter)
- $127K annual savings in recovered analyst hours
- 4.2-month payback period

## Tools
Snowflake, Looker, dbt, Fivetran, Python, Okta SSO

## Duration
16 weeks
