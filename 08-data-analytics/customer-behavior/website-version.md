# Customer Behavior Analytics for Healthcare Technology

## The Challenge

HealthBridge Platform processed 2.3M healthcare appointments annually but had no understanding of WHY customers behaved the way they did. 66% of provider accounts never activated. Product decisions were gut-feel. Customer success was reactive.

## The Solution

I built a comprehensive behavioral analytics system combining Mixpanel instrumentation, BigQuery warehouse, and predictive models to surface actionable insights about customer activation, engagement, and retention.

## Results

**109% Activation Rate Improvement**
34% → 71% of new accounts becoming active users

**25% Retention Improvement**
67% → 84% one-year retention

**61% Faster Time to Value**
18 days → 7 days to first successful action

**34% Reduction in Churn**
Predictive scoring enabled proactive intervention

**$4.2M Revenue Protected**
Annual retained revenue from improved retention

**2.8 Month Payback**
On $168K implementation investment

## Key Discovery

Providers who complete 3 appointments in 14 days have 89% one-year retention. Those who don't: 23% retention. This single insight transformed their onboarding strategy.

## Technical Approach

- Mixpanel SDK with 180 HIPAA-compliant events
- BigQuery warehouse for scalable event analysis
- Customer health scoring (5-factor weighted model)
- 23-feature retention prediction with 78% accuracy
- Census reverse-ETL to operational tools
