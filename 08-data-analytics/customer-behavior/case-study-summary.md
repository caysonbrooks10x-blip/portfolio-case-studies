# Customer Behavior Analysis — Case Study Summary

## Client
HealthBridge Platform — healthcare technology network, 2.3M appointments/year

## Challenge
34% activation rate, 67% retention, no understanding of behavioral drivers. Product decisions were intuition-based, CS was reactive.

## Solution
Built comprehensive behavioral analytics with Mixpanel instrumentation, BigQuery warehouse, customer health scoring, and predictive churn models.

## Results
- Activation rate: 34% → 71% (37% improvement)
- Retention: 67% → 84% (25% improvement)
- Time to first value: 18 days → 7 days
- 34% reduction in churn
- $4.2M retained revenue annually
- 2.8 month payback period

## Key Discovery
Providers who complete 3 appointments in 14 days have 89% retention vs. 23% for those who don't — enabling targeted intervention.

## Tools
Mixpanel, BigQuery, dbt, Looker, Jupyter, Census, Salesforce, Zendesk

## Duration
24 weeks
