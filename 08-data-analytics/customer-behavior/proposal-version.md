# Proposal: Customer Behavior Analytics for HealthBridge Platform

## Executive Summary

HealthBridge Platform needs to understand not just what customers do, but WHY they behave that way. This proposal outlines a 24-week engagement to build a behavioral analytics system that transforms raw event data into actionable intelligence for product, customer success, and growth teams.

## Problem Statement

Your platform processes 2.3M appointments annually. But you don't understand the behavioral patterns that drive activation, engagement, and retention. Product development relies on intuition. Customer success is reactive. Growth is stalling.

The cost: 66% of new provider accounts never activate. 33% of customers churn within a year. That's $4M+ in preventable revenue loss.

## Proposed Solution

**Phase 1: Behavioral Instrumentation (Weeks 1-8)**
- Comprehensive Mixpanel implementation across web, iOS, Android
- HIPAA-compliant event tracking with 180 events
- Data pipeline to BigQuery with de-identification layer
- CRM and support integration (Salesforce, Zendesk)
- Baseline cohort analysis framework

**Phase 2: Behavioral Intelligence (Weeks 9-16)**
- Customer journey mapping (47-step funnel analysis)
- Activation milestone identification
- Feature adoption clustering
- Customer health score model (engagement + outcome)
- Cohort retention analysis across 23 dimensions

**Phase 3: Predictive Models (Weeks 17-20)**
- 90-day retention prediction model
- Activation probability scoring
- Churn risk identification
- Intervention recommendation engine
- Operational integration via Census

**Phase 4: Operationalization (Weeks 21-24)**
- Real-time CSM dashboards
- Automated alert workflows
- Product analytics dashboard
- Executive reporting suite
- Training and documentation
