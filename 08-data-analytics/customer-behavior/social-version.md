# LinkedIn Post

Just finished a fascinating healthcare analytics project that changed how this company understood their customers.

HealthBridge Platform: 2.3M medical appointments/year. But only 34% of new provider accounts ever became active users. No one knew why.

We built a behavioral analytics system to answer: WHY do some customers activate and others don't?

Key finding that changed everything:

Providers who complete 3 appointments within 14 days of signup have 89% one-year retention.
Those who don't: 23% retention.

That's a 4x difference in retention based on a single behavioral milestone.

We built:
→ Comprehensive Mixpanel tracking (180 HIPAA-compliant events)
→ Customer health scoring model (engagement + outcome)
→ Retention prediction with 78% accuracy
→ Automated CSM alerts for at-risk accounts

Results:
• Activation rate: 34% → 71%
• Retention: 67% → 84%
• $4.2M in retained annual revenue
• 2.8 month payback

Healthcare analytics is tricky because of HIPAA — but the behavioral patterns are just as powerful once you have the right data infrastructure.
