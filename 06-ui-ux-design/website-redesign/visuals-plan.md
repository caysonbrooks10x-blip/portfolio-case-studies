## Technical Specifications

### Color Palette for Visuals
- Primary: #2563EB (trustworthy blue)
- Secondary: #059669 (health green)
- Improvement: #F59E0B

- Light background: #F8FAFC

### Typography
- Headlines: Poppins Bold
- Body: Inter Regular

### Export Settings
- Photos: PNG, quality 90%
- Graphics: SVG preferred
- Web: WebP with PNG fallback
- Video: MP4, H.264
