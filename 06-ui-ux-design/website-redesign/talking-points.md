## Answers to Common Questions

**"How did you achieve such dramatic conversion improvements?"**
By removing friction. We identified every point where patients dropped off and redesigned those moments. The booking flow alone went from 11 fields to 3.

**"Why mobile-first?"**
Because 62% of their traffic was mobile, but it converted at 0.8%. Mobile wasn't an afterthought—it was the primary design surface.

**"Did the new design feel too 'casual' for healthcare?"**
We tested extensively. Patients described the new design as "trustworthy but approachable"—exactly the balance needed for telehealth.

**"What about accessibility?"**
We achieved WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. Healthcare audiences often include older users and those with disabilities—accessibility was a priority, not an afterthought.
