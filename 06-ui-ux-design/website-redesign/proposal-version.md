# Proposal: Meridian Health Website Redesign

**Prepared by:** CayCay Brooks, Lead UX/UI Designer  
**Date:** November 2023  
**Duration:** 12 weeks  
**Investment:** $95,000

---

## Executive Summary

Meridian Health has a patient retention problem disguised as a website problem. Your conversion rate of 2.1% is costing you an estimated $1.86M annually in lost appointments. This is not a branding exercise—it is a revenue intervention.

We propose a complete website redesign focused on one metric: conversion.

---

## Understanding Your Situation

**What the data tells us:**
- 78% bounce rate means most visitors leave without engaging
- 67% booking abandonment means even interested patients are lost
- 0.8% mobile conversion means you're ignoring 62% of your traffic
- 47-second average session means the site isn't communicating value

**What patients tell us:**
- "It feels like a hospital website from 2015"
- "I couldn't figure out how to book"
- "I didn't trust it with my health data"

---

## Our Approach

### Phase 1: Discovery (Weeks 1-2)
- Stakeholder interviews (8 participants)
- User research (16 patients/prospects)
- Technical audit (PageSpeed, accessibility)
- Competitive analysis (12 sites)
- Conversion funnel analysis

### Phase 2: Strategy & Architecture (Week 3)
- Persona development
- User journey mapping
- Information architecture redesign
- Content strategy
- Conversion funnel optimization

### Phase 3: Design (Weeks 4-8)
- Wireframes (45 screens)
- Visual design (12 page templates)
- Design system creation
- Interactive prototyping
- 3 rounds of usability testing

### Phase 4: Development & Launch (Weeks 9-12)
- Frontend development
- CMS implementation
- Performance optimization
- Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- A/B testing setup
