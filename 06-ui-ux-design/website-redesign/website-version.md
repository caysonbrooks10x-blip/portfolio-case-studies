# Meridian Health — Website Redesign Case Study

**How we turned a 2.1% conversion rate into 6.8%—and added $2.24M in annual revenue.**

---

## The Challenge

Meridian Health had built a quality telehealth platform, but their website looked and performed like a 2015 hospital portal. Patients were leaving before booking.

## Our Approach

We conducted deep user research, rebuilt the information architecture, and designed a mobile-first experience with frictionless booking at its core.

## The Solution

- **Mobile-first design** (mobile traffic was 62% of visits)
- **3-step booking flow** (reduced from 11 fields to 3)
- **Trust-first messaging** (credentials, reviews, security badges)
- **Performance optimization** (PageSpeed 34 → 89)

## Results

- Conversion: **2.1% → 6.8%** (+224%)
- Mobile conversion: **0.8% → 4.2%** (+425%)
- Bounce rate: **78% → 42%** (-46%)
- Booking abandonment: **67% → 18%** (-73%)
- PageSpeed: **34 → 89** (+162%)

**Annual revenue impact: $2.24M**
