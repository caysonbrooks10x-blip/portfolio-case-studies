# Altitude — Brand Identity System Case Study

**Building a brand as extraordinary as the adventures it represents.**

---

## The Challenge

Altitude had been named in National Geographic's "Top 10 Adventure Operators" twice—but their brand looked like a 2005 outdoor gear catalog. Premium experiences, generic presentation.

## Our Approach

We conducted deep brand discovery, defined a "luxury adventure" positioning, and created a complete identity system rooted in "Alpine Modern" design language.

## The Solution

- **"Alpine Modern" direction** — Sophisticated minimalism meets mountain imagery
- **Custom logo system** — 5 variations for flexible application
- **Premium color palette** — Summit White, Altitude Black, Golden Hour
- **Typography system** — Canela serif + Aktiv Grotesk
- **80 custom icons** — Adventure activities, navigation, amenities
- **48-page brand guidelines** — Comprehensive implementation guide

## Results

- Brand perception: **3.4 → 4.6/5** (+35%)
- "Premium" associations: **12% → 67%** (+458%)
- Average booking value: **$8,400 → $11,200** (+33%)
- Partnership inquiries: **+300%**
- Revenue: **$3.2M → $4.1M** (+28%)
