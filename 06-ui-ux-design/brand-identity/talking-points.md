## Answers to Common Questions

**"How did you arrive at the 'Alpine Modern' direction?"**
We tested three territories—Rugged Luxury, Alpine Modern, and Golden Hour—with target customers. Alpine Modern tested 3x better for "premium" and "different from competitors."

**"Why custom icons instead of stock?"**
80 custom icons ensured every touchpoint felt intentional and ownable. Stock icons would have undermined the premium positioning.

**"How did you handle the typography selection?"**
We paired Canela (elegant serif) with Aktiv Grotesk (modern sans). The combination is sophisticated without being stuffy—perfect for luxury adventure.

**"What's the 48-page brand book for?"**
It gives the team everything they need to implement consistently. Logo usage, color specifications, typography rules, photography direction, voice and tone—it's all documented.
