# Proposal: Altitude Brand Identity System

**Prepared by:** CayCay Brooks, Brand Identity Designer  
**Date:** August 2023  
**Duration:** 10 weeks  
**Investment:** $48,000

---

## Executive Summary

Altitude has built an exceptional adventure travel business—one that National Geographic has recognized twice. But your brand looks like every other outdoor company. You're selling transformative experiences and settling for generic presentation.

We propose a complete brand identity system that positions Altitude as the definitive luxury adventure brand—a visual identity that matches the caliber of what you actually deliver.

---

## Understanding Your Situation

**What your brand currently says:**
- "We're an outdoor company like many others"
- "We're for hardcore adventurers, not people who want comfort"
- "We're older, more conservative than the startups"

**What you want your brand to say:**
- "We're the gold standard for adventure"
- "We combine luxury with wilderness"
- "We're sophisticated, exclusive, transformative"

**The gap:** Your experiences are worth $10K+. Your brand looks like it's worth $1K.

---

## Our Approach

### Phase 1: Discovery (Weeks 1-2)
- Brand audit (current assets and inconsistencies)
- Competitive analysis (20 brands)
- Stakeholder interviews (8 participants)
- Positioning workshop (2 days)

### Phase 2: Creative Direction (Week 3)
- Creative territory exploration (3 directions)
- Moodboard development
- Strategy presentation
- Direction selection

### Phase 3: Design (Weeks 4-7)
- Logo design (5 concepts)
- Color system development
- Typography selection and specification
- Iconography (80 icons)
- Graphic element library
- Photography direction

### Phase 4: Applications & Guidelines (Weeks 8-9)
- Key application mockups
- Brand guidelines document (48 pages)
- Template creation
- Asset preparation

### Phase 5: Handoff (Week 10)
- Team training session
- Documentation
- Implementation support
