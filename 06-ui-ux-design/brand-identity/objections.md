# Altitude — Objection Handling

## "Our old logo has recognition—we've used it for 8 years"

**Acknowledge:** "Brand equity is real, and your current logo has history."

**Pivot:** "But recognition without the right associations can hurt. Your current logo says 'outdoor company.' The goal isn't a new logo—it's a new positioning. The logo is just the most visible element."

---

## "We need something that appeals to our existing customers"

**Acknowledge:** "Existing customers are valuable—they're your advocates."

**Pivot:** "Research showed your existing customers wanted the brand to match the experience. They were embarrassed by the disconnect. This rebrand is what your best customers have been waiting for."

---

## "This seems expensive for 'just a logo'"

**Acknowledge:** "The investment is significant."

**Pivot:** "This isn't 'just a logo.' It's a complete identity system: 5 logo variations, 6 colors, 2 typefaces, 80 icons, 48 pages of guidelines, templates, and training. And the ROI—12:1 based on booking value alone."

---

## "Our team will struggle to implement this"

**Acknowledge:** "Change is always challenging."

**Pivot:** "That's why we include a 2-hour training session and 90-day support. Plus, the guidelines are designed for real-world use, not theoretical perfection. We show common scenarios and how to handle them."

---

## "We want something more 'outdoorsy'—this feels too corporate"

**Acknowledge:** "There's a perception that outdoor = rugged, casual."

**Pivot:** "Altitude is luxury adventure. Your customers sleep in heated yurts and eat farm-to-table meals. 'Too corporate' misses who you actually serve. This brand matches the product."

---

## "Can we see more options before deciding?"

**Acknowledge:** "Of course—more exploration is possible."

**Pivot:** "We've presented 3 macro directions and 5 logo concepts. What specific feedback do you have? We can explore variations on the selected direction."

---

## "The photography direction seems expensive to execute"

**Acknowledge:** "Premium photography requires investment."

**Pivot:** "We've provided detailed specifications so you can phase the photography investment. Stock photography that meets the standard exists. And the investment compounds—you'll use these images for years."

---

## "What if the new brand doesn't resonate?"

**Acknowledge:** "No brand launch is risk-free."

**Pivot:** "We tested extensively before finalizing. Brand perception among target audience went from 3.4 to 4.6 in research. And we'll monitor launch metrics closely."
