## Partnership: Arc'teryx Case Study

Post-launch, Arc'teryx approached Altitude about a co-marketing partnership.

**Partnership Value:**
- Joint adventure experiences (4/year)
- Co-branded content campaign
- Retail presence in 12 Arc'teryx stores
- Estimated value: $180K first year

**Arc'teryx Brand Director Quote:**
"Altitude's new identity signals the kind of quality and authenticity we look for in partners. Their brand finally matches the caliber of their experiences."
