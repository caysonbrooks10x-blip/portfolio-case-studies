# Verve — Mobile App UI System Case Study

**Turning a "confusing finance app" into a Gen Z favorite.**

---

## The Challenge

Verve had strong product-market fit but losing 68% of users before they completed onboarding. The app worked—users just couldn't figure that out.

## Our Approach

We redesigned the entire mobile experience with Gen Z users in mind: dark mode aesthetic, gamification that makes finance feel like a game, and progressive feature discovery.

## The Solution

- **Dark mode UI** (bold, modern, gaming-aesthetic)
- **4-screen onboarding** (90 seconds, not 4 minutes)
- **Celebration system** (milestones, streaks, shareable wins)
- **Progressive disclosure** (features unlock as users earn them)
- **85-component design system**

## Results

- D1 Retention: **41% → 67%** (+63%)
- D7 Retention: **18% → 44%** (+144%)
- Onboarding: **32% → 74%** (+131%)
- Feature discovery: **12% → 56%** (+367%)
- Subscription revenue: **$42K → $168K/mo**
