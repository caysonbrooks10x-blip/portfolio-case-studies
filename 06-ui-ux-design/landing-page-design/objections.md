# NovaTech — Objection Handling

## "We already have a design team"

**Acknowledge:** "Your team is clearly capable."

**Pivot:** "This project requires specialized CRO expertise. Landing page optimization is a discipline of its own. We'd work alongside your team, bringing conversion expertise while your team maintains the product."

---

## "Our forms need to collect qualifying information for sales"

**Acknowledge:** "Sales needs good data to close."

**Pivot:** "We agree—data is crucial. But 58% form abandonment means sales gets zero data from most visitors. We'd rather get 4 fields from 100 visitors than 10 fields from 42. Progressive profiling lets you collect more after initial conversion."

---

## "Our product is complex—video won't show enough"

**Acknowledge:** "Complexity is a real challenge."

**Pivot:** "We kept the video to 90 seconds, focused on the most common use case. Users who want depth can explore the documentation. The video's job is to create enough interest to request a demo."

---

## "We've tried CRO before and it didn't work"

**Acknowledge:** "There are a lot of bad CRO practices out there."

**Pivot:** "Our approach is different: user research before design, systematic testing, and performance optimization. We don't implement vanity changes—we test hypotheses with real users."

---

## "The timeline seems aggressive for proper testing"

**Acknowledge:** "8 weeks is focused."

**Pivot:** "We've built in 3 A/B tests during development. The framework is set up for ongoing testing post-launch. We'll have statistically significant results on key tests within 4 weeks."

---

## "How do we ensure mobile performance?"

**Acknowledge:** "Mobile optimization is critical."

**Pivot:** "We designed mobile-first. LCP went from 4.2s to 1.4s. Mobile PageSpeed went from 34 to 87. This isn't an afterthought—it's the primary design surface."

---

## "What if our sales team doesn't like the leads?"

**Acknowledge:** "Sales-marketing alignment is crucial."

**Pivot:** "We track lead quality metrics, not just quantity. Our leads converted to paid at 22%, above their historical average. Better conversion rate AND better quality."

---

## "Can we implement this incrementally?"

**Acknowledge:** "Big bang launches carry risk."

**Pivot:** "We can launch the main demo request page first, then add trial and enterprise variants. But we recommend all variants launch together for consistent tracking and unified optimization."
