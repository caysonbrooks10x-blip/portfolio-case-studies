## Answers to Common Questions

**"How did you achieve such dramatic CPL reduction?"**
By improving conversion rate, the same budget generates more leads. We also improved lead quality—users who converted through the ROI calculator were 3x more likely to become paying customers.

**"What made the ROI calculator so effective?"**
Users who interact with the calculator are pre-qualifying themselves. They see the value before they commit. When they fill out the form, they're already sold.

**"How do you handle different traffic sources?"**
We built 4 page variants optimized for different sources: LinkedIn (high-intent), Google Ads (comparing), retargeting (warm), display (awareness). Each has different messaging emphasis.

**"What's the ongoing optimization process?"**
We set up the testing framework and Optimizely. Monthly, we review data and implement new tests. The client has a retainer for continuous improvement.
