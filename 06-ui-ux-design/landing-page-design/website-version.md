# NovaTech — Landing Page Design Case Study

**Turning $12-18 clicks into customers, not bounces.**

---

## The Challenge

NovaTech was spending $2M+ annually on paid traffic and converting at 1.8%. Every click was money down the drain.

## Our Approach

We redesigned the entire landing page experience with conversion at the center: minimal forms, ROI calculators, systematic testing, and performance-first design.

## The Solution

- **4 landing page variants** (segmented by traffic source)
- **ROI calculator** (34% conversion lift)
- **4-field form** (156% form completion improvement)
- **90-second video demo** (67% engagement)
- **A/B testing framework** (continuous optimization)
- **Performance optimization** (PageSpeed 34 → 87)

## Results

- Conversion: **1.8% → 5.2%** (+189%)
- Cost per lead: **$187 → $62** (-67%)
- Trial signups: **340 → 1,240/mo** (+265%)
- Bounce rate: **72% → 38%** (-47%)
- Annual revenue impact: **$5.18M**
