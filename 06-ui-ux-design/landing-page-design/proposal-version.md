## Executive Summary

NovaTech is spending $12-18 per click and converting at 1.8%. Every visitor who doesn't convert is pure loss. You're generating awareness, but the landing pages aren't finishing the job.

We propose a complete landing page redesign focused on one metric: cost per acquisition. We'll build pages that convert cold traffic into qualified leads at a fraction of current costs.

## Understanding Your Situation

**What the data tells us:**
- 150,000+ monthly visitors from paid channels
- 1.8% conversion rate (industry benchmark: 4.5%)
- $187 cost per lead (target: $85)
- 72% bounce rate (visitors leave immediately)
- 58% form abandonment (even interested visitors don't finish)

**What users tell us:**
- "I didn't know what NovaTech did in the first 5 seconds"
- "The form asked for too much information"
- "I needed to see it working before I cared"
