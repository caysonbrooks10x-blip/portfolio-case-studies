## Executive Summary

Stratos has a design debt problem that's costing you $2M+ annually. Eight product teams have built UI in isolation, creating 340 components where there should be 85. Your engineers spend 40% of their time rebuilding what should already exist. Your enterprise customers notice the inconsistency.

We propose a comprehensive design system that will unify your product experience, accelerate feature development, and position Stratos as the design leader in enterprise project management.

---

## Understanding Your Situation

**What the audit found:**
- 340 unique UI components in production (should be 85)
- 47 button variants (should be 1)
- 12 modal patterns (should be 1)
- 4 different navigation systems in the same product
- No shared design tokens
- No component governance

**What your teams report:**
- Engineers: "20% of every sprint is rebuilding UI"
- Product: "We ship features slower than competitors"
- Customers: "It feels like five products bolted together"
- Sales: "We're losing deals to more cohesive competitors"
