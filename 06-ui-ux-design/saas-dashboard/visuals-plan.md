## Technical Specifications

### Color Palette for Visuals
- Primary: #6366F1 (Prism brand)
- Success: #10B981
- Improvement highlight: #F59E0B
- Dark background: #0F172A
- Text: #F8FAFC

### Typography
- Headlines: Inter Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Data: JetBrains Mono

### Export Settings
- Photos: PNG, quality 90%
- Graphics: SVG preferred, PNG fallback
- Web: WebP with PNG fallback
- Print: PDF, CMYK
