## Answers to Common Questions

**"How did you get user buy-in for such major changes?"**
Phased rollout with parallel old UI available for 30 days. In-app tours and changelog made the transition smooth.

**"How did you handle the technical constraints?"**
Engineering was at the table from day one. Their constraints forced elegant solutions that actually improved the UX.

**"What about mobile?"**
The dashboard is desktop-primary, but we designed responsive breakpoints that maintain usability on tablets for monitoring use cases.
