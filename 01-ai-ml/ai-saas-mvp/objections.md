# Objection Handling: RepFlow — AI SaaS MVP Build

---

## Objection 1: "We already have Gong/Chonus. Why would we need RepFlow?"

**Response:**
"Gong and Chorus are excellent products — for post-call analysis. They tell you what happened after the call ends.

RepFlow is the only product that coaches reps during the call, in real-time. The moment has passed by the time Gong analyzes it.

Our pilot data shows: reps who receive real-time coaching during calls improve their close rates by 18% within 60 days. Post-call analysis alone doesn't produce that kind of improvement because the behavior change happens too late.

The best customers use both: Gong for strategic analysis and pipeline management, RepFlow for real-time rep coaching."

---

## Objection 2: "Won't reps find the real-time cues distracting?"

**Response:**
"That's the right concern — and we tested extensively to find the balance.

The cues are designed to be read in 2 seconds or less. They're color-coded by urgency. They're suppressed automatically during critical customer statements (when the rep should be listening, not looking at their screen).

We also built an adaptive frequency algorithm: senior reps see 60% fewer cues than new reps. The system learns each rep's baseline and adjusts.

In our pilot, 89% of reps reported the cues were 'not distracting' or 'helpful' after 30 days of use. Only 4% requested to disable the feature entirely."

---

## Objection 3: "Our reps won't want AI monitoring their calls."

**Response:**
"That's a legitimate adoption concern — and we built RepFlow to address it.

First, the cues are invisible to customers. We tested with sales leaders and they couldn't identify when RepFlow was active from the customer side of calls.

Second, the product is positioned as coaching, not surveillance. Reps see the value directly — their close rates improve, their manager reviews become more targeted, their follow-up is automated.

Third, we gave reps visibility into their own data. They can see their coaching trends, their performance vs. team average, and their improvement over time.

In our pilot, rep adoption was 94% within 30 days without any mandatory enforcement. They used it because it made their job easier."

---

## Objection 4: "How do you handle data privacy and call recording consent?"

**Response:**
"Data privacy is built into the product architecture, not bolted on.

First, we require explicit consent management per customer — a compliance checklist that enterprise procurement teams can review with their legal teams.

Second, all call recordings are encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Data retention is configurable per-customer, from 30 days to 365 days.

Third, we provide GDPR and CCPA compliance tooling: data deletion workflows, consent management, and audit logs.

Fourth, for EU customers, we offer data residency options so recordings never leave EU infrastructure.

We also support enterprise single-tenant deployment options for customers with strict data sovereignty requirements."
