# RepFlow — AI SaaS MVP Build

## Overview

RepFlow was founded by Marcus Webb (former Salesforce enterprise sales leader) and Dr. Sonia Patel (computational linguist) to solve a $4.2B problem: sales reps forget 67% of training content in 30 days, and the average enterprise rep ($1.2M revenue) receives only 30 minutes of coaching per month.

They engaged me to design and build their MVP — an AI-powered sales coaching platform that analyzes calls in real-time, provides instant coaching cues to reps during calls, and generates post-call summaries with improvement recommendations.

## The Challenge

Real-time AI coaching requires sub-200ms latency — speech-to-text, NLP analysis, coaching cue generation, and WebSocket delivery all need to happen faster than a human can perceive. Existing call intelligence tools only analyzed calls after they ended. The market needed real-time intervention.

## The Solution

Built RepFlow's MVP in 9 weeks: AssemblyAI real-time STT, GPT-4o coaching engine, React rep interface with sub-200ms cue delivery, manager dashboard, post-call AI summaries, and CRM integrations.

## Results

- **MVP launched** in 9 weeks (on time)
- **3 enterprise pilot customers** with 420 reps onboarded
- **1.4 second** call processing latency (beat 2 second target)
- **+47 NPS** from pilot participants
- **4.7/5** manager satisfaction
- **$2.4M seed round** closed 4 weeks post-pilot
- **$12M** post-seed valuation
