---

🚀 **Built a $12M-valued AI SaaS in 9 weeks. Here's what I learned.**

Just shipped RepFlow — an AI-powered sales coaching platform — from concept to pilot with 420 users in 9 weeks.

The founders came to me with a clear insight and a pitch deck. Nine weeks later, they closed a $2.4M seed round at a $12M valuation.

Here's the build story:

📞 **What we built:**
Real-time AI coaching during sales calls — subtle cues appear on the rep's screen advising on talk ratio, sentiment shifts, objection handling. Post-call AI summaries with performance scorecards. Manager dashboard for team coaching. CRM integrations (HubSpot + Salesforce).

The technical challenge: sub-200ms latency. Speech → STT → NLP → Cue generation → WebSocket delivery all needs to happen faster than human perception. We hit 180ms average.

📊 **Results:**
• MVP launched on time (9 weeks)
• 3 enterprise pilot customers
• 420 sales reps onboarded
• +47 NPS from pilot
• 4.7/5 manager satisfaction
• $2.4M seed closed 4 weeks post-pilot

Key lessons:

1. **Real-time latency is a product problem, not just technical.** Every 100ms slower reduces cue effectiveness 15%. We obsessed over it.

2. **Build enterprise-ready from day one.** SOC 2 prep, SSO, RBAC — it added 2 weeks but enabled enterprise deals immediately.

3. **The first 3 customers define your product.** Pick for feedback willingness, not just revenue.

4. **AI summaries must be perfect before launch.** Users forgive imperfect real-time cues. They do not forgive imperfect written summaries.
