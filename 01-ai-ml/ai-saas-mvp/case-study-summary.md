# Case Study Summary: RepFlow — AI SaaS MVP Build

## The Client
RepFlow | Founded March 2024 | Pre-seed stage  
AI-powered sales coaching platform for enterprise B2B SaaS

## The Problem
67% of sales training forgotten in 30 days. Average enterprise rep ($1.2M revenue) gets 30 minutes of coaching per month. Sales managers spend 4.5 hours/rep/month reviewing calls manually. The market needed real-time AI coaching during calls, not just post-call analysis.

## The Solution
Built RepFlow's MVP from concept to launch in 9 weeks. Real-time speech-to-text (AssemblyAI), AI coaching engine (GPT-4o) generating sub-200ms cues to reps during calls, post-call AI summaries with improvement recommendations, manager dashboard for team coaching, and CRM integration (HubSpot/Salesforce).

**Tech Stack:** AssemblyAI, GPT-4o, React/Next.js, Supabase, Vercel, Railway, Stripe, Posthog

**Timeline:** 9 weeks MVP development
