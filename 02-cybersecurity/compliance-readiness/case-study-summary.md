## The Engagement

TechFlow, a B2B SaaS company with $28M ARR, engaged us to achieve SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certification simultaneously—required by enterprise prospects blocking $4.2M in deals. With no formal compliance program in place, we built their entire security and compliance framework from scratch over 16 weeks.

## The Challenge

- 180 employees, $28M ARR, no existing compliance program
- Three enterprise deals worth $4.2M blocked by certification requirements
- SOC 2 and ISO 27001 needed simultaneously
- Tight timeline: 90 days to close deals
- No policies, procedures, or control documentation

## The Solution

We conducted a comprehensive dual-framework compliance engagement:
- SOC 2 Type II + ISO 27001:2022 gap analysis

- 65 SOC 2 controls + 93 ISO 27001 controls implemented
- Drata compliance automation platform deployed
- 85% of evidence collection automated

- 42 Medium severity
