# Secure Authentication + Access Control Redesign

## OmniBank — Enterprise Banking Security Transformation

---

### The Challenge

OmniBank, a regional bank serving 1.2 million customers across 85 branches, faced regulatory enforcement actions following an OCC examination that identified critical authentication and access control deficiencies. With a 90-day deadline and $18B in assets at stake, they needed expert help.

### What We Did

Designed and implemented a zero-trust identity architecture in 12 weeks:
- Okta centralized identity platform with adaptive MFA
- CyberArk privileged access management
- Saviynt access governance and certification
- RBAC for core banking (847 discrete roles)
- Splunk centralized logging and monitoring
- Third-party vendor access management

### The Results

- **Regulatory examination passed** with zero critical findings
- **$12M in enforcement penalties** avoided
- **Account takeover reduced by 97%** (2,340 to 89 attempts/month)
- **Fraud losses reduced 98%** ($180K to $4.2K/month)
- **100% MFA coverage** for online banking and admin access
- **79x ROI** on engagement value

### Investment

**$185,000** | **12 weeks** | **5-person team**
