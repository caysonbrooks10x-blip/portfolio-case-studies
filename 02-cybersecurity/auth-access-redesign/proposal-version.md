## 1. Understanding Your Needs

OmniBank is a regional bank serving 1.2 million customers with $18B in assets. Following your regulatory examination, you face a 90-day deadline to demonstrate meaningful progress on identity and access management deficiencies. Your examination identified critical gaps: no multi-factor authentication, shared administrative accounts, inadequate privileged access management, and missing access certification processes.

Your specific requirements:

1. **Immediate priority:** Address regulatory findings before the 90-day deadline
2. **Comprehensive redesign:** Zero-trust identity architecture across all channels
3. **Regulatory compliance:** Satisfy OCC, FDIC, and state banking requirements
4. **Sustainable governance:** Build ongoing access governance practices
