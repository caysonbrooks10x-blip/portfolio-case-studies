# Meridian Health Penetration Test — Executive Summary

## The Engagement

Meridian Health, a digital health platform serving 1.2 million patients across 47 clinics, engaged us for a comprehensive penetration test following a near-miss phishing incident and new HIPAA compliance requirements. Over 8 weeks, our red team conducted external, internal, web application, and social engineering testing, discovering 31 vulnerabilities including 2 critical issues that could have enabled complete network compromise.

## The Challenge

- 1.2 million patient records at risk
- Recent phishing incident created urgency
- Cyber insurance renewal required documented pen test
- HIPAA compliance audit in 4 months
- Board demanded measurable security improvement

## The Solution

We conducted a comprehensive red team assessment:
- External network testing (black box)
- Internal network testing (assume breach)
- Patient portal web application testing
- Phishing simulation (340 targets)
- Physical security assessment

## Key Findings

**31 vulnerabilities identified:**
- 2 Critical (domain admin access, patient data enumeration)
- 8 High severity
- 12 Medium severity
- 9 Low severity

**Attack achievements:**
- Domain Admin achieved in 4 hours
- 47 workstations compromised
- 1.2M patient records accessible
- Phishing: 37% click rate, 26% credential capture

## The Outcome

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Time to Domain Admin | 4 hours | >1 month |
| Patient data exposure risk | Critical | Low |
| Phishing success rate | 37% | 12% |
| Critical vulnerabilities | 2 | 0 |

**Results:**
- HIPAA compliance audit: Passed
- Cyber insurance renewal: Approved
- Board security assessment: Positive
- 90% reduction in phishing susceptibility

**Investment:** $125,000 | **Timeline:** 8 weeks | **Team:** 6 operators
