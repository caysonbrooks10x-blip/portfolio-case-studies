## 3. Methodology

### Red Team Approach

We simulate realistic adversaries:
- Nation-state motivated (healthcare data is high-value)
- Organized crime (ransomware threat)
- Insider threat (workforce members)

### Rules of Engagement

- Zero client data touched without explicit permission
- Proof-of-concept exploits only (no destructive testing)
- Logging of all activities for accountability
- Emergency contacts for escalation

### Deliverables

1. **Technical Findings Report** — Detailed vulnerability documentation with proof-of-concept
2. **Executive Summary** — Board-ready overview of risks and remediation
3. **Remediation Roadmap** — Prioritized action plan
4. **Re-Test Report** — Verification of fixes
5. **IOC List** — Indicators of compromise for monitoring
