# Penetration Testing

## Meridian Health — Healthcare Red Team Assessment

---

### The Challenge

Meridian Health, serving 1.2 million patients across 47 clinics, needed a comprehensive penetration test to satisfy cyber insurance requirements and HIPAA compliance audits following a recent phishing incident.

### What We Did

Conducted an 8-week red team assessment:
- External network penetration testing
- Internal network (assume breach scenario)
- Patient portal web application testing
- Phishing simulation (340 targets)
- Physical security assessment

### The Results

- **31 vulnerabilities** identified (2 critical, 8 high)
- **Domain Admin achieved** in 4 hours from initial access
- **1.2M patient records** potentially accessible
- **Phishing click rate reduced** from 37% to 12% post-training
- HIPAA compliance audit: **Passed**
- Cyber insurance renewal: **Approved**

### Investment

**$125,000** | **8 weeks** | **6-person red team**
