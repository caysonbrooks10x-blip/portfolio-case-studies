- "Meridian Health had 156 security misconfigurations in their AWS environment — including unencrypted databases with 2.3 million patient records. After our engagement, they passed their HIPAA audit and reduced their attack surface by 94%."

## Challenge Points

- **Cloud migration gaps:** "Meridian moved 70% of their workloads to AWS in 18 months. Security didn't keep pace — they had the infrastructure but not the expertise."
- **HIPAA pressure:** "Healthcare organizations can't afford security incidents. OCR fines range from $100 to $50,000 per violation, and breach notification costs add up quickly."
- **Skills gap:** "Their IT team knew traditional security but not cloud-native security. We fixed the immediate problems and built lasting capability."
- **Multi-account complexity:** "847 AWS resources across 5 accounts with no centralized security governance. That's a lot of attack surface."

## Methodology Highlights

- "We combine automated CSPM scanning with manual expert review — tools find misconfigurations, humans find the context."
- "Three AWS security specialists worked across all five accounts simultaneously to maximize efficiency."
- "We don't just assess and leave. We implement the fixes alongside your team, train your staff, and make sure you can maintain it."

## Security Best Practices to Mention

- "IAM is your first line of defense in the cloud — most breaches involve compromised credentials."
- "Encryption at rest isn't optional for healthcare data. It's the law."
- "Network segmentation prevents lateral movement. If an attacker gets in, they shouldn't be able to reach your most valuable assets."
- "Continuous monitoring replaces point-in-time assessments. You can't audit your way to security."
- "Multi-account AWS environments need Service Control Policies to prevent risky actions at the organizational level."

- "Are your development and production environments segmented? Could a compromised staging resource reach production data?"
- "How confident are you that your cloud environment meets HIPAA requirements?"
