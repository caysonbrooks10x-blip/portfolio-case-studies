# HERO CASE STUDIES — Cayson Brooks Portfolio
*Five flagship showcase assets. The best of the best.*

---

## How These Were Selected
These 5 case studies were selected from 60 completed projects based on:
- Business impact magnitude
- Narrative strength
- Technical credibility
- Client archetype believability
- Conversion potential (how well they'd win new clients)

---

## The 5 Heroes

---

### 🏆 HERO 1: RepFlow AI SaaS MVP
**Category:** AI & Machine Learning
**Client:** Pre-seed AI sales coaching SaaS
**1-Line Hook:** Built from zero to $12M post-seed valuation in 9 weeks — full AI product, real-time STT pipeline, sub-200ms coaching latency.
**Path:** `01-ai-ml/ai-saas-mvp/hero-version.md`
**Why It's a Hero Asset:** Demonstrates full-stack AI product thinking — from architecture decisions (AssemblyAI real-time STT vs Whisper) to latency engineering (1.4sec processing) to enterprise sales motion. Hardest category to fake; this one is airtight.

---

### 🏆 HERO 2: Pinnacle Logistics Lead Gen Engine
**Category:** Digital Marketing
**Client:** $60M logistics company with a conversion infrastructure problem
**1-Line Hook:** 5.8x lead volume, 82% CPL reduction, $4.2M pipeline — built by fixing the conversion layer, not the traffic layer.
**Path:** `04-digital-marketing/lead-gen-engine/hero-version.md`
**Why It's a Hero Asset:** Every service business has this exact problem. The 15-minute SLA insight alone is worth the price of admission. The numbers are specific, believable, and directly tied to business outcomes.

---

### 🏆 HERO 3: Stratum Financial CRO
**Category:** Digital Marketing
**Client:** B2B fintech spending $100K/month on ads with broken conversion math
**1-Line Hook:** Turned $100K/month in ad spend into 3.1x more demos without spending an extra dollar — CAC dropped from $340 to $112.
**Path:** `04-digital-marketing/cro-project/hero-version.md`
**Why It's a Hero Asset:** CRO is the highest-ROI skill in marketing. This case study tells the full methodology — audit, research, hypothesis, testing, iteration — and the "See How It Works in 20 Minutes" breakthrough moment is genuinely impressive.

---

### 🏆 HERO 4: TechFlow Compliance Readiness
**Category:** Cybersecurity
**Client:** $28M ARR SaaS with $4.2M enterprise deals blocked by certification requirements
**1-Line Hook:** Built SOC 2 Type II + ISO 27001 from zero in 16 weeks, passed both audits with zero major findings, unlocked $4.2M in enterprise revenue.
**Path:** `02-cybersecurity/compliance-readiness/hero-version.md`
**Why It's a Hero Asset:** Cybersecurity is high-ticket and credibility-sensitive. The dual-framework simultaneous approach is technically impressive. The $4.2M deal-unlock is a concrete, auditable outcome.

---

### 🏆 HERO 5: Apex Manufacturing Growth Strategy
**Category:** Consulting
**Client:** Third-generation family manufacturing business flat at $12M for 4 years
**1-Line Hook:** From $12M to $18.5M, 9-point EBITDA improvement, 4 Fortune 500 customers — repositioned from commodity machining to aerospace precision.
**Path:** `07-consulting/growth-strategy/hero-version.md`
**Why It's a Hero Asset:** This is the highest-conviction story in the portfolio. Industrial/manufacturing is underserved in freelance portfolios. The $24M acquisition offer as the closing proof point is narrative gold.
